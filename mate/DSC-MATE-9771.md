Item #: DSC-MATE-9771

Access to DSC-MATE-9771 is restricted to personnel with Level 2 clearance or higher.

DSC-MATE-9771 has spotted skin and red clown eyes, with a green head that has a curled edge. Two color bands are apparent on DSC-MATE-9771's forehead, and it wears goggles and a jade earring. DSC-MATE-9771 is often seen wearing a silver chain and an LED gas mask.

DSC-MATE-9771's personality is characterized by a heightened sensitivity to emotional stimuli. DSC-MATE-9771 is easily moved to tears by sentimental moments in movies or TV shows and has a fondness for music and art that evokes strong feelings. DSC-MATE-9771 has shown no aggressive or violent tendencies, but its emotional state can be easily disrupted, leading to potential containment breaches if not handled with care.

DSC-MATE-9771's job is that of a car salesman, which it performs with efficiency and enthusiasm. Its salesmanship tactics have been noted to involve a strong emotional appeal to potential buyers, which can result in the sale of vehicles that would otherwise not be purchased.

DSC-MATE-9771 was discovered by Foundation agents after reports of a human with abnormal physical features and emotional sensitivity surfaced in an urban area. DSC-MATE-9771 was relocated to the Foundation's containment facility for study and safekeeping.
