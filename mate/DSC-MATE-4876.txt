SCP Foundation Classified Information:

Item#: 4876

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-4876 is to be kept in a bio-containment chamber, designated SCP-4876-Alpha. All attempts at communication with SCP-4876 must be made through a secure intercom system. All interactions with SCP-4876 must be kept to a minimum, as it has been known to become overly excited and potentially dangerous.

SCP-4876 is to be provided with a variety of toys and puzzles to keep its mind stimulated. Under no circumstances should SCP-4876 be allowed to access any machinery or tools, as it has been known to cause significant damage.

Description:

SCP-4876 is a male monkey measuring approximately 60 cm in height and weighing approximately 10 kg. Its fur is green in color and has not been arranged or groomed in any way. SCP-4876's eyesight appears to be relatively normal, and it is often seen wearing a pair of glasses.

On its forehead, SCP-4876 displays a distinctive two-color band, which appears to be a natural feature of its species. SCP-4876 also wears a silver chain, which appears to be of human manufacture, around its neck, and often carries a candy stick in one hand.

Personality:

SCP-4876 is characterized by its energetic and enthusiastic nature. It is known to become easily excited about various topics and may attempt to get others excited as well. However, SCP-4876's enthusiasm can sometimes become excessive, leading to humorous situations or potentially dangerous situations.

SCP-4876 has been known to display above-average intelligence and problem-solving abilities, making it an excellent mechanic. However, due to its tendency to become overly excited and potentially destructive, it has been deemed too dangerous to work with machinery or tools.

SCP-4876 is social, and it enjoys interacting with other primates. However, it has shown aggression towards any humans that attempt to interact with it. As such, all interactions with SCP-4876 are to be through a secure intercom system.
