Item #: SCP-3080

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3080 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. SCP-3080 is allowed access to a computer with internet capabilities for the purpose of his job as a reporter for ████████ Times newspaper. SCP-3080 is to be monitored during all interactions with outside individuals.

Description: SCP-3080 is a human male standing at approximately 6 feet tall with a scruffy beard and a mouth spot. SCP-3080 typically wears a bucket hat and has a pair of goggles on his forehead. SCP-3080 also has a green cross earring and wears a silver chain around his neck. SCP-3080 is known to smoke cigarettes regularly.

SCP-3080 displays a laid-back and easy-going demeanor but has been observed to be fiercely loyal and protective of his loved ones. SCP-3080’s loyalty has led to humorous situations, such as getting into a heated argument with someone who insults a friend or family member. SCP-3080 has been observed to have exceptional writing skills, and his reports have been praised for their in-depth analysis and high-quality writing.

Addendum: SCP-3080’s writing has been found to contain strange anomalies, including predictive events and an ability to manipulate reader emotions. Research into the source of these anomalies is ongoing. SCP-3080 has been used in various Foundation operations requiring diplomatic and investigative skills.
