Item #: 9155

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9155 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber, with a webcam installed due to their sensitive nature. Access to SCP-9155 is strictly limited to Level 3 personnel, and all interactions must be monitored by at least two security cameras. SCP-9155 is to be given a steady supply of their preferred brand of cigarettes, which are to be checked for any suspicious substances.

Description: SCP-9155 is a male rabbit, standing at approximately 1.6 meters in height, with an athletic build. SCP-9155 has a unique physical appearance, including red clown eyes, jade earrings, and a goggle on their forehead. SCP-9155 is often observed wearing a silver chain and smoking cigarettes. SCP-9155 is employed as a beautician and is known for their brilliant organizational skills and ambitious nature.

SCP-9155 has proven to be highly intelligent, with excellent strategic thinking skills. They are goal-oriented and have a tendency to take on more than they can handle. Despite this, SCP-9155 has proven to be successful in completing their tasks, thanks to their exceptional organizational skills. SCP-9155 can be quite sensitive and can become agitated in stressful situations.

Addendum: Due to SCP-9155's unique appearance and exceptional skills, the Foundation has explored the potential for SCP-9155's use in field operations. However, after further evaluation, it was deemed that SCP-9155's sensitivity and emotional nature make them unsuitable for high-risk operations. SCP-9155 remains in containment for the time being.
