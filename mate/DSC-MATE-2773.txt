Item #: 2773

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2773 is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at all times. No personnel are allowed to be alone with SCP-2773 unless authorized by Level 4 personnel. Any attempts to remove SCP-2773 from containment are considered an act of sedition and will be met with immediate termination.

Description: SCP-2773 is a male monkey with a frightened mouth and unknown eye color. SCP-2773 wears a blue bandana on its head and goggles on its forehead. SCP-2773 is capable of language and has been observed to display a strong sense of empathy, often attuned to the emotions of others. SCP-2773's personality is often humorous, using its empathetic abilities to make others feel better or to find humor in difficult situations.

SCP-2773 is also a successful politician, capable of wielding significant influence over human populations. Despite its abilities, SCP-2773 is considered a threat to society due to its anomalous nature and the potential for it to use its powers for its own gain.

Addendum: Following Incident 2773-A, SCP-2773's containment procedures have been revised to include a contingency plan for the handling of hostile actions by SCP-2773. All personnel are to maintain vigilance when interacting with SCP-2773 and report any suspicious behavior immediately.
