DSC File #5153:

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5153 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber equipped with all necessary amenities for a primate. All personnel entering DSC-MATE-5153's containment must have Level 2 clearance and be trained in handling hostile entities. Any physical interaction with DSC-MATE-5153 must be authorized by at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-5153 is a male monkey of unknown species, standing at approximately 1.1 meters tall and weighing 25 kilograms. Upon capture, DSC-MATE-5153 was found wearing a silver chain and a pair of goggles on its forehead, along with a gold earring on its left ear. A two-color band is also visible on its forehead.

DSC-MATE-5153 displays a strong and decisive personality, exhibiting traits of a natural leader. While this can come across as bossy or intimidating, DSC-MATE-5153 only wants to ensure that tasks are executed efficiently and effectively. DSC-MATE-5153 has displayed above-average intelligence compared to its species, exhibiting skills such as problem-solving and strategic planning.

DSC-MATE-5153's anomalous abilities include heightened physical strength and agility, along with the ability to manipulate and control other primates telepathically. DSC-MATE-5153 can communicate and issue commands to primates both in and outside of its immediate vicinity.

DSC-MATE-5153 is known to have a history of theft and aggression, displaying a lack of empathy towards other living beings. DSC-MATE-5153 has shown no sign of remorse towards its actions and poses a significant threat to personnel and the general public.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5153 was captured during a gang-related burglary, where it was found coordinating a group of primates to steal valuable goods. DSC-MATE-5153 has since been contained and is deemed a significant threat to Foundation operations. Research on DSC-MATE-5153's abilities and origins is ongoing.
