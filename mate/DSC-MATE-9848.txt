Item #: 9848

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9848 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with ample space for movement. The chamber must be checked daily for any damage or signs of escape attempts. SCP-9848 is to be provided with a balanced diet and toys to simulate natural monkey behavior. Any requests made by SCP-9848 are to be reviewed and approved by at least two Level 3 personnel before accommodation is made.

Description: SCP-9848 is a male monkey of an unknown species. The SCP displays a distinctive appearance with a retro cap on its head, a gold chain around its neck, and a whistle as an accessory. SCP-9848 stands approximately 2.2 feet tall and weighs 20 pounds. The SCP is highly intelligent and possesses a remarkable mechanical aptitude. Its unique talent enabled the foundation to hire it as a mechanic.

SCP-9848 has a strong sense of empathy, making it highly attune to the emotions of others. The SCP uses this talent to boost morale among foundation personnel. SCP-9848's sense of humor is unmatched, and it often knows exactly what to say to lighten the mood in stressful situations. The SCP's communication skills have been praised by foundation personnel, who report feeling less stressed after even a moment's interaction with SCP-9848. Despite its positive demeanor, if provoked, SCP-9848 can be aggressive and difficult to control, potentially causing damage to the facility.

SCP-9848 was discovered wandering the streets of [REDACTED] before the foundation took it into containment. The SCP's background is unknown, and its species has yet to be identified.

Addendum: SCP-9848 has requested a mechanical workshop of its own to conduct experiments and improve its skills. The request is still under review.
