Access is granted only to authorized personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any attempts to communicate with DSC-MATE-1218 must be approved by at least one Level 4 personnel. No physical contact with DSC-MATE-1218 is allowed under any circumstances.

Description: DSC-MATE-1218 appears to be a Caucasian male in his mid-40s, standing at approximately 180 cm in height and weighing around 70 kg. DSC-MATE-1218 displays several distinct facial features, including spots on his skin, a charming smile, and a pair of nerd glasses. DSC-MATE-1218's head appears to resemble that of a typical monk, with a clean-shaven scalp and a small goatee.

DSC-MATE-1218 usually wears a silver chain around his neck, which is believed to have no anomalous properties. However, further examination of the chain is still ongoing. DSC-MATE-1218 is a human politician with a knack for deadpan humor that often catches people off guard. Despite not being the life of the party, DSC-MATE-1218 has a way of delivering jokes that are surprisingly funny, leaving those around him confused and startled.

DSC-MATE-1218's anomalous properties manifest when anyone attempts physical contact with him. Individuals who come into contact with DSC-MATE-1218 report feelings of intense dread, anxiety, and paranoia, often followed by hallucinations and, in some cases, severe mental breakdowns. These effects have been observed to last for up to several days after contact.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1218 was brought to the Foundation's attention after reports of its anomalous properties surfaced in the press. Foundation agents were able to locate and detain DSC-MATE-1218 without incident, and it has remained in containment ever since. Further research into the nature and extent of DSC-MATE-1218's abilities is ongoing.
