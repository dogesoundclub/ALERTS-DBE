Item #: 4586

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 4586 must be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. It must be provided with a supply of bananas, its preferred food, and toys for stimulation. Personnel interacting with the subject must wear gloves and protective gear at all times.

Description: DSC-MATE-4586 is a male monkey of the Trachypithecus francoisi species, commonly known as the "Francois' langur". The subject's face features a permanently frightened expression on its mouth, as if it is afraid of something or someone. Its eyes are not distinct but are of a dark shade typical of its species.

DSC-MATE-4586 displays a distinctive mohawk on its head, giving it an edgy appearance. The subject sports a gold earring on its left ear and a red scarf as its chosen accessory.

DSC-MATE-4586 possesses a peculiar personality with heightened observant and attentiveness for details. The subject has proved to be an excellent gift-giver, with a knack for selecting the perfect gift for its recipients. However, it also displays a tendency to become overly concerned with giving the right gift, which could lead to obsessiveness and anxiety.

DSC-MATE-4586 is capable of communicating using sign language and has shown affinity for social interaction with humans. Its intelligence level is above average for its species, with exceptional problem-solving skills.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-4586 has been employed as a fast-food worker for McDonald's. Its exceptional attention to customer needs and communication skills have proven beneficial for the establishment's customer satisfaction ratings. However, the subject's tendency of obsessing over offering the right snack to customers has caused concerns for its well-being, hence prompting its acquisition by the DogeSoundClub.
