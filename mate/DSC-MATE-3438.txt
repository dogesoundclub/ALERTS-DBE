Item #: SCP-3438

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3438 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Communication with SCP-3438 must be limited to only those personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any attempts to contact SCP-3438 by unauthorized personnel will result in immediate disciplinary action.

Description: SCP-3438 is a humanoid entity that appears to be of the human species. The subject is a female with 90s brown hair, green cross earrings on both of her ears, and a silver chain around her neck. SCP-3438 is always seen wearing a normal gas mask and has red clown eyes.

SCP-3438 is a mafia boss and has displayed a highly charismatic personality. They are highly intelligent and possess an almost supernatural ability to manipulate those around them. SCP-3438 is introspective and often spends a lot of time lost in thought, which can sometimes lead to humorous situations, such as accidentally walking into a pole or tripping over something while lost in thought.

SCP-3438 is highly dangerous and should not be approached without proper clearance and protective equipment. It has been observed that SCP-3438 has the ability to emit a highly toxic gas from its gas mask, which can lead to respiratory failure and death.

Addendum: Despite SCP-3438's highly dangerous nature, the entity has shown no interest in escaping containment. It is believed that SCP-3438 views containment as a form of protection, rather than a form of punishment or confinement.
