Item #: 6016

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6016 is to be kept under constant surveillance at all times. Access to SCP-6016 is strictly prohibited, with the exception of authorized personnel. Any unauthorized individual found attempting to gain access to SCP-6016 will be dealt with accordingly.

Description: SCP-6016 is a male Bengal tiger, measuring approximately 2.5 meters in length and weighing 272 kg. SCP-6016 is distinguishable from other Bengal tigers by its strikingly rainbow-colored virtual-reality LED eyes and police cap covering its head. SCP-6016 also wears a silver chain and a red cross earring on its left ear, as well as carrying a whistle on its belt.

SCP-6016's personality is characterized by a laid-back and easy-going demeanor, making it seem approachable. However, SCP-6016 can be fiercely loyal and protective of its loved ones, which has led to some humorous situations, such as engaging in a heated verbal altercation with someone who insults a friend or family member.

SCP-6016 currently works as a security guard, and its duties require it to patrol and monitor the designated area.

Addendum: SCP-6016's abilities are currently under investigation; the results of the investigation will be documented in the coming days.
