The entity is also often seen with a green cross earring in one ear and a medical mask covering the mouth.

DSC-MATE-4627 is an expert in the art of charisma and interpersonal communication. It possesses a unique ability to connect with individuals on a deep level and gain their trust and admiration. This skill has proven useful in its career as a politician, often allowing it to sway opponents and advance its own agenda.

However, caution must be exercised around DSC-MATE-4627, as its persuasive abilities can be used for both benevolent and malevolent purposes. Its humorous nature can be disarming, with its ability to make strangers open up about their life stories, a potential information-gathering technique.

Agents are advised not to engage in extended conversation with DSC-MATE-4627 unless granted express permission by senior staff. The entity is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell and monitored for signs of anomalous activity. In the event of a containment breach, all personnel are to avoid direct engagement and instead utilize indirect methods of restraint.
