Item #: SCP-7093

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7093 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. No physical contact must be made with SCP-7093 without first obtaining permission from at least one Level 2 personnel. SCP-7093 may be provided with basic amenities as required, including access to literature, entertainment, and a limited internet connection. All communication with SCP-7093 must be monitored and recorded for further analysis.

Description: SCP-7093 is a male humanoid of average build, standing at 1.85 meters in height. It displays several unique physical features, including orange stripes on the cheeks, a frightened-looking mouth, and wears nerd glasses over its eyes. The head is purple in color and appears to be made of metal. SCP-7093 also has a gold earring through its left ear and a red scarf around its neck. Additionally, it consistently wears a medical mask over its mouth.

SCP-7093's psyche is characterized by a strong desire to make the world a better place, often expressing acts of idealism, even in mundane or humorous circumstances. It has been observed to boycott or protest against companies and causes it views as unethical or against its values. SCP-7093 is cooperative and amicable, displaying no signs of violent or harmful behavior towards personnel. 

SCP-7093's primary occupation is a firefighter, though its current employment status is pending review by the Foundation. SCP-7093's anomalous properties do not appear to interfere with its ability to perform its job adequately. It claims to have no knowledge of how it obtained its unique features or medical mask, nor is it aware of any supernatural abilities or events. Research into the origins of SCP-7093's physical characteristics and motivations is ongoing. 

Addendum: The following is an excerpt from an interview with SCP-7093 conducted by Dr. ██████.

Dr. ██████: Can you tell us about your desire to make the world a better place?

SCP-7093: Well, I believe we all have a responsibility to try and leave the world a better place than we found it. Whether it's through activism or just simple acts of kindness, every little bit counts.

Dr. ██████: Have you always felt this way?

SCP-7093: I suppose so. I remember being a kid and picking up litter around my neighborhood or trying to help my friends with their problems. It's just part of who I am.

Dr. ██████: And your physical features? How did you obtain those?

SCP-7093: I don't know. They've always been there, as far as I can remember. The mask too. I just assumed it was for protection against smoke or something.

Dr. ██████: Thank you for your time, SCP-7093.

Closing Statement: Further testing and research into SCP-7093's psychological and physical properties will continue, in hopes of uncovering more information about its unique makeup.
