Item#: 3218

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 3218 is to be allowed to continue working their job as a fast food worker without interference from Foundation personnel. However, any casual interaction with 3218 outside of a testing environment must be engaged with caution to prevent rising tensions with the subject.

Description: DSC-MATE-3218 is a human male with a series of unique physical traits which make him easily identifiable. The most prominent feature being his spotted complexion and clown mouth. DSC-MATE-3218's eyes are blue and locked into a constant appearance of clown eyes, regardless of changes in gaze or emotion. 

3218 is often seen wearing a short peak cap on his head, which contains a 2 color band on the forehead element below the cap. While working in public, the subject always wears a medical mask covering his mouth. Despite the mask, it has been noted that DSC-MATE-3218 has a high standard of dental hygiene. 

DSC-MATE-3218 wears a silver chain necklace at all times, though attempts to remove the accessory has been met with extreme distress and aggression from the subject. 

Personality: DSC-MATE-3218 is an extremely organized and efficient worker. It is believed that these traits may have emerged due to childhood trauma. DSC-MATE-3218 has a tendency to come off as controlling, and infamously takes charge of group projects with a matter-of-fact approach, at times causing humorous yet awkward situations. 

DSC-MATE-3218 has quickly adapted his organizational skills to his job in fast food industry and is regarded as an ideal employee by his employers. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-3218 has been observed to have a fascination with the concept of clowns and keeps a vast collection of clown memorabilia in his home. Foundation personnel are encouraged not to mention clowns, circus or associated topics within the vicinity of DSC-MATE-3218.
