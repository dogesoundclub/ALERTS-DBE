Item #: 5010

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Item #5010 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. All personnel who come into contact with Item #5010 must wear appropriate personal protective equipment to prevent contamination.

Description: Item #5010 is a human male identified as a beautician. He stands at approximately 6 feet tall and has a mohawk hairstyle. His most notable feature is his smile that radiates an unsettling charm. He often wears nerd glasses, a red scarf, and a green cross earring. Item #5010 also carries a candy stick at all times.

Item #5010's personality is highly infectious, as he has a natural charm and charisma that can make him the life of the party. He often tells jokes and does silly dances to make others laugh. Although his behavior appears harmless, it can lead to a distraction and potentially dangerous situation.

Addendum: Further testing has revealed that Item #5010's candy stick contains a potent magical substance. Further investigation into the origin of the candy and its possible effects is currently ongoing.
