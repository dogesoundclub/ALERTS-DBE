Item #: SCP-5643

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5643 is to be confined to a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times when not performing its duties. Access to SCP-5643 is to be authorized by level 3 personnel or above. Personnel granted access must maintain a professional and courteous demeanor at all times.

SCP-5643 must be given access to a personal workspace within its containment chamber. This workspace must include a desk, computer, and other necessary materials for SCP-5643 to perform their job as a banker.

All communication with SCP-5643 must be monitored by Foundation personnel.

Description: SCP-5643 is a female humanoid of the species Homo sapiens. SCP-5643's most notable facial feature is the presence of orange stripes, which run from the corners of its mouth to the bottom of its chin. SCP-5643 also has orange eyebrows and a smile that is constantly present on its face.

SCP-5643 wears a purple cap with a headset, gold earrings, and a choker. SCP-5643 is often seen with a cigarette.

SCP-5643's personality is characterized by its dry sense of humor, often delivering witty one-liners and deadpan jokes. SCP-5643's humor has been known to catch people off guard, causing them to underestimate SCP-5643's intelligence and competence.

SCP-5643's job as a banker is vital to maintaining its mental and emotional well-being. SCP-5643 takes great pride in its work and strives to be the best banker it can be.

Addendum: SCP-5643's talents have been noted by several Foundation employees. SCP-5643 has been requested for several high-level meetings due to its ability to keep the mood light while still conveying important information. SCP-5643's services are highly valued by the Foundation.
