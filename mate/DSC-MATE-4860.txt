Item #: SCP-4860

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4860 is to be monitored at all times and is only permitted to leave the facility for its designated job as a delivery driver. Any unusual behavior or deviation from its designated schedule must be reported immediately to superiors.

Description: SCP-4860 is a human male with a perpetually frightened mouth and VR jade LED eyes. SCP-4860 is commonly seen wearing a bucket hat, jade earring, red scarf, and a normal gas mask. SCP-4860 is known to possess a love for adventure and enjoys taking risks and trying new things.

SCP-4860's job as a delivery driver has provided numerous opportunities for SCP-4860 to indulge in its desire for adventure. SCP-4860 has survived a near-death experience while bungee jumping and has accidentally gotten lost in the wilderness.

There are no known anomalous properties associated with SCP-4860; however, due to the potential risks associated with its behavior, SCP-4860 is to be continually monitored. Any further anomalies discovered are to be reported immediately to superiors.

Addendum 1: Though SCP-4860's actions may often be humorous, it is important to remember the potential danger associated with its thrill-seeking behavior. SCP-4860's job as a delivery driver does not excuse any deviation from its designated schedule or behavior that may put itself or others in danger.
