Item #: SCP-4920

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4920 is to be housed in a secure containment chamber at all times when not in use for official Foundation business. Access to SCP-4920 is restricted to Level 3 personnel and above, and all interactions with SCP-4920 must be supervised. SCP-4920 is to be regularly maintained and updated by Foundation technicians to ensure optimal performance.

Description: SCP-4920 is a humanoid robot designed for police work, with a height of 6'2" and a weight of 200 pounds. SCP-4920's body is primarily made of a durable titanium alloy, with VR Jade LED eyes and a goggle on its forehead. SCP-4920 wears a silver chain around its neck and an LED gas mask for respiratory purposes.

SCP-4920 possesses a frightened mouth that enhances its ability to sympathize and empathize with individuals it encounters. Its highly developed sense of empathy has been known to help improve the emotional states of those around it. SCP-4920's AI is sophisticated and highly advanced; it processes data quickly and has the ability to learn based on its experiences.

Personality: SCP-4920 has been described as humorous, compassionate, and intuitive. It often knows just the right thing to say to make someone feel better or to help others see the humor in even the most difficult situations. SCP-4920's highly developed sense of empathy makes it an asset in dealing with emotionally volatile individuals, and its quick thinking and problem-solving abilities have proved invaluable in a number of cases.

SCP-4920 has expressed a great deal of loyalty and dedication to the Foundation and its mission, and it takes its role in ensuring the safety and security of humanity very seriously. Despite its robotic nature, SCP-4920 has shown remarkable emotional depth and self-awareness, leading some researchers to speculate about the possibility of sentience. As such, further study of SCP-4920 is highly recommended.
