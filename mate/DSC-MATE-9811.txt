Item #: 9811

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: Due to the dangerous nature of SCP-9811, containment measures must be strictly enforced. SCP-9811 is to be contained within a reinforced, soundproofed cell at all times. Guards are to be placed at all entrances to the containment chamber in order to prevent any potential breaches.

Description: SCP-9811 is a male Siberian tiger, often seen wearing a headset with LED lights and smoking a cigarette. Their mouth is often in a frightened expression, contrasting with their otherwise no-nonsense personality. SCP-9811 is the current boss of a powerful mafia, and their authority and respect among their underlings is absolute.

SCP-9811 values tradition, often telling jokes or stories that have been passed down through their community. Despite their tough exterior, SCP-9811 also has a playful side that can lead to humorous situations. However, they are not to be underestimated as SCP-9811 is a fierce and cunning leader, with the strength and skills to back it up.

In the event of a breach, all personnel are advised to stay away from SCP-9811's cell and contact the nearest security personnel. Failing to do so may result in serious harm or death. SCP-9811 poses a significant threat to both staff and civilians, and should only be approached with caution.

Addendum: Recent reports indicate that SCP-9811 may be attempting to expand their influence beyond their current criminal enterprise. Investigations are ongoing.
