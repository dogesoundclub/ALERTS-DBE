Item #: DSC-MATE-9638

DSC-MATE-9638 values social connections and will often engage in jokes and playful banter with others, even within Foundation personnel. DSC-MATE-9638's presence has been known to uplift the mood of those around them and create a more positive working environment.

While DSC-MATE-9638 poses no direct threat, their clothing and accessories have been noted to contain some anomalous properties. DSC-MATE-9638 has been known to display subtle manipulative abilities when it comes to their fashion designs, often convincing clients to pay a higher price or to work within DSC-MATE-9638's design parameters. While not dangerous or directly harmful, this behavior should be monitored within a professional setting.

Addendum: After numerous requests, DSC-MATE-9638 has been granted permission to design clothing specifically for Foundation personnel. Foundation staff have reported increased morale and camaraderie when wearing DSC-MATE-9638's designs. However, this has also raised concerns of possible security breaches if the clothing is compromised. Further analysis is ongoing.
