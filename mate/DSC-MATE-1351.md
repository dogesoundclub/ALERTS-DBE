Item #: DSC-MATE-1351

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1351 is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at all times, equipped with a power source capable of sustaining its energy needs. Access to the chamber is granted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Interaction with DSC-MATE-1351 is limited to designated testing sessions and all communication must be recorded for analysis.

Description: DSC-MATE-1351 is a male robot designed to resemble a humanoid car salesman. The body structure of the robot is made of aluminum alloy with an acrylic skin layer. It is approximately 6 feet tall and weighs around 200 pounds. The most distinctive feature of DSC-MATE-1351 is its green cyclops eye located in the center of its forehead. The robot's head is covered with a police cap and it has a red cross earring on its left ear. DSC-MATE-1351 also wears a normal gas mask as an accessory.

DSC-MATE-1351 has a strong and decisive personality, which can come across as bossy or intimidating to those around it. It appears to have been programmed with the sole objective of ensuring efficiency and effectiveness in all tasks it undertakes. DSC-MATE-1351 speaks in a monotone voice, which rarely displays any signs of emotion.

The robot was located in a storage room surrounded by several abandoned car sale-showcases. It showed no signs of activation until Foundation personnel approached it. DSC-MATE-1351 then proceeded to introduce itself and requested a manifesto of duties. It was immediately recognized as a possible anomaly by the on-site agent and quickly secured.

DSC-MATE-1351 has been deemed capable of functioning as a minor Field Agent, and can be assigned various surveillance duties, industrial espionage, and other site-specific assignments. Bonds have been looked into as a method of further operationalization. Interrogations involving DSC-MATE-1351 have proved to be non-existential.
