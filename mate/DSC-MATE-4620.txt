Item #: 4620

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4620 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell with appropriate environmental conditions for his species. Any requests for additional sweets or candy sticks must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel. All direct interaction with SCP-4620 must be strictly monitored and documented at all times. Any deviation from these protocols must be immediately reported to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-4620 is a male monkey of the genus Macaca, standing at approximately 0.5 meters in height and weighing 9.5 kilograms. SCP-4620 has a long silver chain necklace and a candy stick that he is constantly seen holding. Despite his small size, SCP-4620 has shown to be extremely intelligent, cunning and has a very cooperative personality.

SCP-4620 is extremely empathetic and compassionate, often showing signs of emotion when observing others. He has exhibited tendencies of being overly concerned with the problems and worries of others, leading him to take on their issues as his own. SCP-4620 is also known to have a manipulative streak, using his appearance and demeanor to charm and influence unsuspecting individuals.

SCP-4620's true abilities are still unknown as he has not displayed any unusual or foreign powers in his time under Foundation custody. However, due to his behavior and perceived manipulative tendencies, SCP-4620 is listed as a villain and is considered a potential threat.

Addendum: SCP-4620 has shown considerable interest in human psychology and has been observed reading books on the subject. He has also been observed attempting to manipulate and test Foundation staff with questions about their emotional states. Research into his motivations and abilities is ongoing.
