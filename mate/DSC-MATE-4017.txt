Item #: 4017

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4017 is to be contained within an isolated farming property in a rural location outside of urban centers. Access to SCP-4017 is limited to authorized personnel only. Any unauthorized individuals attempting to approach SCP-4017 are to be apprehended and subjected to amnestics.

Description: SCP-4017 is a human male identified as a local farmer residing in the aforementioned rural locale. SCP-4017 possesses peculiar physical characteristics such as orange stripes on the face, red clown eyes, a peak spike on the head, and a medical mask covering the mouth.

SCP-4017 also sports goggles on the forehead and a gold chain accessory. Despite their unusual appearance, SCP-4017 displays a friendly and jovial personality. SCP-4017 is diligent in the upkeep and management of their farming property, highlighted by their strong sense of duty and responsibility.

Under further observation, SCP-4017 is shown to possess a playful demeanor, which has led to humorous situations at social gatherings. SCP-4017 is known to surprise others with dance moves and silly antics when on the dance floor.

SCP-4017 is to be monitored and kept in containment to ensure their safety and the safety of others. Any anomalies related to SCP-4017 should be reported immediately to the Site Director.
