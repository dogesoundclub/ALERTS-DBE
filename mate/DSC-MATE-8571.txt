Item #: SCP-8571

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8571 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell with minimal decorations to reduce any chances of irritation. Daily meals consisting of raw meat and vegetables are to be provided to SCP-8571. Any medical assistance required by SCP-8571 is to be provided by a Class A medical staff member wearing a hazmat suit.

Description: SCP-8571 appears to be a male tiger in the form of a human with distressed facial features. The subject has a frightened mouth and ski goggle eyes. SCP-8571 is usually seen wearing a goggle on their forehead, gold earrings, silver chains, and a normal gas mask.

Personality: SCP-8571 may appear relaxed and laid-back but is fiercely devoted and protective of loved ones. SCP-8571 has been known to engage in heated arguments with individuals that insult their friends and family members. SCP-8571 has a doctorate in general medicine and will attempt to assist any injured individuals willingly. SCP-8571's origin is unknown at this moment, but it is believed to have been created by the same forces that created SCP-1622.

Addendum 8571-A: SCP-8571 is requesting to have a pet cat in its containment cell. The request is currently under review.
