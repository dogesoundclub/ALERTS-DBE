Item #: SCP-4761

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4761 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber, with access to basic amenities such as water, food, and sanitary facilities. Access to electrical equipment must be granted only under the supervision of Level 2 personnel.

Description: SCP-4761 is a human male of average height and build, with orange stripes on his face. He wears normal glasses and a Doge hat, with a two-color band on his forehead and a green cross earring on each ear. SCP-4761 also wears a gold chain and carries a candy stick as accessories.

SCP-4761 works as an electrician, with skills in handling and manipulating electric currents that exceed those of ordinary humans. Interviews with SCP-4761 reveal that he possesses a highly ambitious and goal-oriented personality, often taking on several demanding tasks at once. However, he consistently manages to excel in his job, thanks to exceptional strategic thinking and organizational skills.

SCP-4761 is highly adaptable and can quickly respond to unusual or intense situations involving electrical equipment. Despite the apparent risk, SCP-4761 has never caused any significant harm to himself or others while handling electricity.

Addendum: SCP-4761 has expressed a desire to work with other electrical anomalies present in the Foundation's containment facilities. Requests for SCP-4761's involvement in electrical containment operations may be considered on a case-by-case basis, subject to the approval of Level 3 personnel.
