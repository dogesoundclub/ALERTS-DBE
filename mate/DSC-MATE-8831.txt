Item #: SCP-8831

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8831 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-8831 is strictly prohibited without Level 3 clearance or higher. All personnel entering SCP-8831's chamber must wear standard particle containment suits equipped with air-filtration systems.

Description: SCP-8831 is a female humanoid standing at 168 cm in height and weighing approximately 60 kg. SCP-8831 has orange-colored hair on the sides of her head, with two color bands running across her forehead. SCP-8831 also has spots on her face, a red lip, and a silver chain around her neck. Additionally, SCP-8831 wears a medical mask over her mouth and jade earrings on her ears.

SCP-8831 possesses a deep curiosity about the world and often displays an interest in obscure and unusual topics. SCP-8831's curiosity has led to several accidents that have endangered the lives of Foundation personnel. SCP-8831 also has a tendency to randomly spout off obscure facts or trivia, causing confusion and annoyance among those around her.

SCP-8831's abilities are not well understood at this time, but it has been observed that she possesses an extraordinary level of physical strength and endurance. SCP-8831 also appears to possess some form of regenerative abilities, as she has been observed healing rapidly from injuries that would be fatal to normal humans.

SCP-8831 is considered a high-risk threat to Foundation personnel and is to be contained and monitored at all times. Any attempts by SCP-8831 to breach containment are to be met with lethal force.
