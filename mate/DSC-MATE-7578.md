Item #: DSC-MATE-7578

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7578 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. a gas-tight enclosure must surround DSC-MATE-7578 during transport. Personnel approaching DSC-MATE-7578 must don Class B hazmat suits in order to prevent exposure to its anomalous properties. Under no circumstance should DSC-MATE-7578 be allowed to communicate or interact with other DSCs.

Description: DSC-MATE-7578 appears to be a human female with unusual facial features, highlighted by purple lips and clown-like blue eyes framed by spots on her face. The subject is always seen wearing a purple cap with a headset, with a two-color band on her forehead, and gold earrings. The subject also sported a choker and a gas mask for protective measures.

DSC-MATE-7578 has a dominant personality, making her a natural leader. Her presence is intimidating to those around her, but she remains efficient and effective, always aiming for the best results. It is believed that DSC-MATE-7578's anomalous properties had intensified her personality and decision-making abilities beyond normal human capabilities.

DSC-MATE-7578 has displayed anomalous abilities that cause a heightened sense of euphoria to any personnel present when she is in a state of triumphant success. This ability has caused concern among the Foundation's upper management, increasing its risk evaluation. Due to these properties, interaction with DSC-MATE-7578 must be limited to necessary measures.

Addendum: Due to DSC-MATE-7578's abilities and the potential threat it poses, the O5 Council is currently discussing the possibility of reclassifying DSC-MATE-7578 as Keter. Until further notice, DSC-MATE-7578 is to remain classified as Euclid.
