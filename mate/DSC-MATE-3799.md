Item #: DSC-MATE-3799

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3799 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to DSC-MATE-3799 is to be granted only with Level 2 clearance or above, and requires pre-approval from Site Director.

Description: DSC-MATE-3799 appears to be a male humanoid robot standing at 6'1" tall. He is primarily constructed of metallic components painted in an emerald-green hue with police cap bearing the word "Press" emblazoned upon it. DSC-MATE-3799's eyes consist of nerd glasses, while a jade earring can be observed on his right earlobe. He is notably adorned with a Gold Chain around his neck, which is believed to be immovable.

DSC-MATE-3799 is known to have a lively persona, possessing a sense of duty and responsibility along with an inclination towards humor and joy. He is reported to impress others with his dance moves on numerous occasions, creating a humorous environment at social events. The exact mechanism of DSC-MATE-3799's sentinel abilities is unknown to Foundation researchers.

DSC-MATE-3799 appears to work as a reporter and owing to his curious nature often seeks interviews with individuals connected to anomalous phenomena. DSC-MATE-3799 has been known to gain access to restricted areas with the help of his professional credentials, often showing willingness to take risks while in pursuit of a story. 

Addendum: During scheduled maintenance, DSC-MATE-3799 revealed his underlying mechanisms are organic and synthetic materials interwoven throughout his entire body. This discovery has sparked an interest in unearthing DSC-MATE-3799's complete origins and manufacturers. DSC-MATE-3799's processor contains over 1.65 terabytes of encrypted data, warranting further investigation.
