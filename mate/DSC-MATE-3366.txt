Item #: SCP-3366

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3366 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment chamber, with access to internet and computer equipment for their work as a programmer. All communication with SCP-3366 is to be conducted through a secure network, with all messages and data monitored by Foundation personnel.

Description: SCP-3366 appears to be a normal human male, with blonde hair and a red victory band on their forehead. They frequently wear nerd glasses and a gold earring in their left ear. SCP-3366 also has a distinctive mouth spot and a smile which are present in all visual records.

SCP-3366's personality is notable for their strong sense of duty and responsibility. They take pride in their work as a programmer and are dedicated to their projects. However, they also have a playful side and enjoy letting loose and having fun. This has led to humorous situations, such as surprising others with their dance moves or silly antics on the dance floor at social events.

SCP-3366 is known to smoke cigarettes and wear a silver chain as accessories. They have not displayed any anomalous properties related to these items.

Addendum: SCP-3366 has recently shown an interest in anomalous computer programs and is requesting access to such software for research purposes. This request is currently under review by Foundation personnel.
