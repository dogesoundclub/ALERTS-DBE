Item #: SCP-8940

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8940 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Due to their tendency to break out of confinement with ease, at least two Level 3 personnel must be present during any interaction with SCP-8940. The use of sedatives is authorized should SCP-8940 become hostile.

Description: SCP-8940 is a humanoid individual of female gender, measuring 1.7 meters in height and possessing distinctive orange stripes on their face. The subject also has purple lips and orange eyebrows, as well as a purple cap with a headset covering their head. SCP-8940 is often observed smoking a cigarette and wearing a jade earring and a choker.

SCP-8940 has a peculiar personality, often switching between a serious no-nonsense demeanor and a playful, humorous side. They place a high value on tradition, often recounting stories or jokes passed down through their family or community. However, due to their criminal tendencies and lack of remorse, SCP-8940 is considered a dangerous villain and should be approached with caution.

Addendum: SCP-8940 has shown a remarkable ability to escape confinement using their keen intellect and agility. It is highly recommended that additional security measures be implemented to contain SCP-8940 effectively.
