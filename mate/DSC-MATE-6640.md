Item #: DSC-MATE-6640

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6640 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with appropriate furnishings for a tiger of its size. It is to be provided with a workstation and salon supplies for its role as a beautician. All interactions with DSC-MATE-6640 must be conducted through a reinforced viewing window, and any physical contact or breach of containment must be reported immediately to on-site security.

Description: DSC-MATE-6640 appears to be a Bengal tiger with a highly unusual set of adornments. Its eyes are framed by horned rim glasses, and its head is outfitted with a headset with LED lights. It has a goggle affixed to its forehead and wears a silver chain around its neck. When approached by personnel, DSC-MATE-6640 can communicate fluently in English and has expressed an interest in working as a beautician.

DSC-MATE-6640 exhibits personality traits common to its species, including a preference for solitude, and a predilection for playful behavior. Additionally, DSC-MATE-6640 is highly organized and efficient, with tendencies towards control and delegation. Its ability to manage tasks and direct others has, on occasion, resulted in humorous or unexpected outcomes.

Addendum: Following a containment breach, DSC-MATE-6640 was found to have been operating an illicit salon within an abandoned storefront. Immediate action was taken to neutralize the threat posed by DSC-MATE-6640's grooming tools, and it was brought back into containment. Due to its behavior, containment procedures for DSC-MATE-6640 have been modified to include random inspections and heightened monitoring of its communications with outside entities.
