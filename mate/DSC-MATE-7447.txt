Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7447 is to be allowed to live his normal life as a fast food worker. No containment measures are necessary. Any attempts to disturb his daily routine will result in disciplinary action.

Description: SCP-7447 is a human male in his mid-thirties with spots on his face and a frightened mouth. He usually wears a bucket hat on his head, a medical mask over his mouth, a green cross earring on his left ear, and a gold chain as an accessory. He has been observed to be quite reliable and organized, with a tendency towards stubbornness. 

SCP-7447 has a hard time letting go of his routines or accepting new ideas, which can lead to some humorous situations when he encounters unexpected changes or challenges. Despite this, he remains dedicated to fulfilling his duties as a fast food worker and has been noted to be an exemplary employee.

SCP-7447 seems to possess no anomalous abilities, and no abnormal behavior has been observed. As such, SCP-7447 poses no threat to the general public or the Foundation. 

Addendum: SCP-7447 has expressed a desire to donate a portion of his earnings to charity organizations. Permission has been granted, and SCP-7447 has been encouraged to continue his philanthropic efforts.
