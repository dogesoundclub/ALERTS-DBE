Item #: DSC-MATE-3219

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3219 is to be monitored at all times and is to be confined to a standard humanoid containment chamber. Any requests or communications from DSC-MATE-3219 are to be reviewed by at least two Level 2 personnel before approval. DSC-MATE-3219 is to undergo regular psychological evaluations to ensure mental stability.

Description: DSC-MATE-3219 is a human male of unknown age, approximately 6 feet tall, and weighing approximately 165 pounds. DSC-MATE-3219's most notable physical features are the numerous spots on their face and their frightened mouth. Their eyes are a jade green LED and their hair is styled in a mohawk. They wear a jade earring and a silver chain as accessories, and often smoke a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-3219 functions as a real estate agent and has exceptional knowledge of the real estate market in the surrounding area. Despite being predominantly extroverted and humorous, DSC-MATE-3219 has displayed some paranoia and has exhibited symptoms of anxiety and depression.

Behaviorally, DSC-MATE-3219 displays a great sense of humor and often attempts to make others laugh. They are known to tell amusing stories and perform silly impersonations in an attempt to lighten the mood. DSC-MATE-3219 is highly articulate and persuasive when it comes to promoting the properties that they are selling.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-3219 was detained by Foundation agents after accusations of using anomalous methods to sell properties surfaced. Further investigation revealed that DSC-MATE-3219 had been using an anomalous effect to enhance their persuasive abilities, leading to increased sales and profits. It is recommended that DSC-MATE-3219's effect be studied further to determine its origin and its potential uses for the Foundation.
