Item #: SCP-9322

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9322 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. All interactions with SCP-9322 must be supervised by at least two Level 2 personnel. SCP-9322 must be provided with a variety of sentimental movies and TV shows, as well as access to music and art that evokes strong feelings. In addition, SCP-9322 must be granted a weekly allowance of cigarettes in order to maintain stable emotional regulation.

Description: SCP-9322 is a female human, identified as a veterinarian by profession. SCP-9322's most notable features include a set of spots on her face and a constant smile. SCP-9322's eyes have been replaced with VR Jade LED implants, and she wears a tiara on her head. A red cross earring is present on SCP-9322's left ear, while a goggle is worn on her forehead.

SCP-9322 has demonstrated an intense level of emotional sensitivity, frequently becoming moved to tears by sentimental moments in movies or television shows. SCP-9322 has also shown a particular fondness for music and art that evokes strong feelings. Due to this heightened emotional state, SCP-9322's behavior can be unpredictable, and personnel interacting with SCP-9322 should exercise caution.

Addendum: SCP-9322 has demonstrated an ability to emit sonic waves at a frequency that causes intense emotional disturbances in individuals within a 50-foot radius. Further research is necessary to determine the nature of this ability and the potential for its use as a containment measure.
