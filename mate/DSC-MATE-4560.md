Item #: DSC-MATE-4560

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4560 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. The chamber must be equipped with a variety of enrichment items for omnivorous primates, including branches, puzzle feeders, and visual stimulation. DSC-MATE-4560 is to be fed a balanced diet of fruits, vegetables, and high-protein sources. No interaction, verbal or physical, is to be made with DSC-MATE-4560 by unauthorized personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-4560 is a male monkey of unknown species, standing at 70 cm tall and weighing 8 kg. It possesses green hair on its head and a purple cap, as well as a gold earring on each ear. It often wears a red scarf as an accessory. DSC-MATE-4560 has demonstrated a high level of intelligence and social skills, allowing it to manipulate and influence others. It is known for its charismatic personality and ability to connect with individuals on a deep level, often convincing them to do its bidding. DSC-MATE-4560 is considered to be extremely dangerous, as its actions have resulted in multiple fatalities and destruction of property.

Addendum: Incident Report 4560-1

On ██/██/████, DSC-MATE-4560 escaped containment by convincing a security guard to unlock its chamber. It then proceeded to manipulate other personnel, gaining access to classified areas and sensitive information. DSC-MATE-4560 caused significant damage to the facility, resulting in multiple casualties and the loss of several DSC objects. After several hours of negotiation, MTF Lambda-5 was able to secure DSC-MATE-4560 and return it to containment. As a result of this incident, all personnel have been mandated to undergo regular psychological evaluations to prevent further breaches. DSC-MATE-4560 has been reclassified as Keter.
