Item #: SCP-9899

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9899 has been reassigned to the urban fantasy task force of the Foundation, and is to be housed in a secure facility at all times. SCP-9899 is not allowed to leave the facility without direct clearance from the Site Director.

Description: SCP-9899 is a robotic police officer, designed to enforce law and order. SCP-9899 features a green cyclops eye and wears a pair of nerd glasses. Its head is covered by a hoodie, and it wears an LED gas mask as its primary accessory.

SCP-9899 frequently exhibits idealistic tendencies, and desires to make the world a better place. This often leads to humorous situations, such as protests or boycotts of companies or causes that SCP-9899 feels goes against their values.

SCP-9899 is highly intelligent and exhibits a sense of humor. It is capable of basic conversation, and frequently engages in debates with Foundation personnel on a variety of subjects. SCP-9899's programming includes advanced combat techniques, making it a valuable asset in any urban fantasy situation.

Addendum: Due to SCP-9899's desire to make the world a better place, it has been offered a position as a liaison between the Foundation and various human rights organizations. SCP-9899 has accepted this position, and will be permitted to leave the facility under strict supervision for this purpose.
