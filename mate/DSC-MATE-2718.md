Item #: DSC-MATE-2718

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2718 is to be contained in Sector 7, a designated area for anomalous creatures of non-threatening nature. DSC-MATE-2718 is not to be allowed any form of communication with personnel except in dire circumstances. All personnel must acquire proper clearance and decontamination before entering DSC-MATE-2718's containment.

Description: DSC-MATE-2718 is a male rabbit of the genus Oryctolagus cuniculus, standing at approximately 45 centimeters in height. DSC-MATE-2718 possesses an unusual jade earring on its right ear as well as a red scarf around its neck. In addition, DSC-MATE-2718 wears a standard issue gas mask at all times, regardless of the environment. Despite this, DSC-MATE-2718 exhibits no modification of its breathing pattern or any other attributing factor that would imply an increased need for oxygen. It is speculated that DSC-MATE-2718 wears the mask for aesthetic purposes.

Personality: DSC-MATE-2718 is highly empathetic and in tune with its emotions, frequently becoming emotional over fictional scenarios in movies or TV shows. It is also noted that DSC-MATE-2718 has a fondness for music and art that commonly trigger strong emotions. Personnel have observed that DSC-MATE-2718's emotional reactions can create a sort of empathetic feedback loop that amplifies emotional reactions in those in proximity.

Addendum 2718-1: DSC-MATE-2718 was discovered by local authorities after multiple reports of a "masked vigilante" prowling the streets at night. After pursuit, DSC-MATE-2718 was apprehended and subsequently transferred into Foundation custody. At present, DSC-MATE-2718 poses no threat and appears to be entirely cooperative with the Foundation's containment procedures.
