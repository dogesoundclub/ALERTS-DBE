Item #: SCP-6488

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:
SCP-6488 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Personnel should avoid physical contact with SCP-6488 unless accompanied by at least two (2) Level 2 security personnel. Any and all interactions with SCP-6488 must be monitored and recorded.

Description:
SCP-6488 is a male human identified as a practicing lawyer. SCP-6488's most notable feature is a brown spot located in the center of its mouth, beneath its nose. SCP-6488 is often seen wearing jade earrings and silver chains, with a metal purple head.

SCP-6488 displays exceptional charm and charisma, often resorting to joke-telling or humorous dances to entertain those around him. Despite his entertaining nature, SCP-6488 is highly intelligent and quick-witted, with a penchant for manipulating those around him.

SCP-6488 is usually docile and compliant with Foundation personnel. However, SCP-6488 has been known to become agitated and potentially violent when threatened or cornered. It is believed that SCP-6488 possesses anomalous abilities that have yet to be fully identified.

Addendum: 
SCP-6488 was discovered in [REDACTED] after several reports of strange occurrences involving SCP-6488's law practice were filed with local authorities. Upon Foundation intervention, SCP-6488 willingly agreed to cooperate with containment procedures. Further investigation into SCP-6488's abilities and origins is ongoing.
