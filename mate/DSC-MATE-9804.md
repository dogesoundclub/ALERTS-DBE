DSC-MATE-9804 shows a high level of proficiency in programming and computer systems, with an affinity for coding and debugging. It also tends to wear a gold chain as a fashion accessory around its neck, alongside an LED gas mask.

DSC-MATE-9804's personality is characterized by a lack of adaptability, leading to a level of stubbornness when presented with new challenges. Despite this, DSC-MATE-9804 is noted for its organizational skills and reliability in completing tasks assigned to it.
