Item #: SCP-7044

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7044 is to be contained within a reinforced steel chamber with heavily armed guards present at all times. Any interaction with SCP-7044 can only be done with written permission from Site Director. All communication with SCP-7044 must be conducted through a thick glass screen for the safety of personnel.

Description: SCP-7044 is a humanoid robot standing at 6'5" with a masculine appearance. SCP-7044's eyes are covered with horned rim glasses, and its head is adorned with a short peak cap. SCP-7044 also wears a gold earring in its left ear, a red scarf, and a normal gas mask. SCP-7044's weaponry consists of two machine guns attached to its arms, and it shows high proficiency in their usage.

SCP-7044's personality is characterized by its deep curiosity about the world and its tendencies to share obscure facts and trivia randomly. SCP-7044 has demonstrated a clear love for learning and desires to know as much about the world as possible. However, SCP-7044's curious nature also leads it to be very dangerous, as it is known to experiment with various weapons and techniques leading to the death of multiple high-ranking officials.

Addendum: SCP-7044 has been identified as a powerful mafia boss and is believed to have extensive connections in the criminal world. Any attempted escape by SCP-7044 must be stopped by all means necessary. SCP-7044's motives and end goals are currently unknown, and further investigation is underway.
