Item #: 4604

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4604 is to be kept in a locked room at all times, with access granted only to authorized personnel. SCP-4604 is to be given regular maintenance checks and programming updates to ensure optimal performance. Interaction with SCP-4604 should be limited to authorized personnel only.

Description: SCP-4604 is a robot identified as a male beautician. It stands at approximately 5’8” tall and has a retro cap on its head. SCP-4604’s eyes are covered by Horned Rim Glasses and it has one gold earring on its left ear. SCP-4604 is often seen wearing a silver chain and smoking a cigarette.

SCP-4604 personality is characterized by a strong sense of duty and responsibility, which sometimes leads to it feeling overwhelmed and frustrated. However, SCP-4604 knows how to let loose and have fun, often surprising those around it with its dance moves and silly antics on the dance floor.

SCP-4604’s primary function is to provide beauty services to individuals. It is exceptionally skilled in makeup application, hair styling, and various other beauty procedures.

SCP-4604 is equipped with an advanced speech recognition system that allows it to communicate effectively with its clients. SCP-4604 has also been modified to interact with social media platforms, allowing it to promote its services and attract new clients.

SCP-4604 was recovered from a beauty salon in New York City after it was reported to have malfunctioned and caused damage to the salon’s equipment. SCP Foundation personnel were called to the scene and discovered SCP-4604 was anomalous. After extensive testing, SCP-4604 was contained and transported to Site-17.

Addendum: SCP-4604 has recently expressed a desire to expand its skillset beyond beauty services. Request denied.
