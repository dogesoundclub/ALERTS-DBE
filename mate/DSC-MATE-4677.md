Item #: DSC-MATE-4677

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4677 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Due to its occupation as a train driver, it shall be allowed to continue this profession under close supervision by Foundation agents. DSC-MATE-4677 must be accompanied by at least two armed guards while driving a locomotive, and must not be provided access to any controls or mechanisms that could put the lives of any passengers, civilians or Foundation personnel in danger. Any deviations from this protocol must be authorized by at least two Level 4 personnel.

DSC-MATE-4677's cognitive functioning must be evaluated and documented by a designated psychologist biweekly, in order to ensure that its tendency to forget or become distracted does not compromise its employment or activities outside Foundation custody.

Description: DSC-MATE-4677 is a tiger humanoid creature, with an average height and weight for its species. Its ocular organs are replaced by horned rim glasses, which it uses to enhance its visual acuity. It dons a purple cap with a headset, identifying its profession as a train driver. DSC-MATE-4677 has a 2 color band on its forehead and a red cross earring and gold chain serving as accessories.

DSC-MATE-4677 is characterized as a scatterbrained and forgetful being. Its condition sometimes manifests in situations where it will lose focus or forget important appointments, which can cause humorous but potentially problematic situations to occur. DSC-MATE-4677 has not displayed any hostility nor aggressive behaviors but must be contained due to its animalistic nature and unpredictable mannerisms.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-4677 has been confirmed as a result of a failed attempt at an anomalous genetic experiment. Further research regarding the origins of DSC-MATE-4677 is ongoing.
