SCP Foundation Description:

Item #: SCP-2453

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2453 is to be kept in a standard human containment chamber and granted access to all necessary amenities. Access to SCP-2453 is restricted to Level 3 personnel and above.

Description: SCP-2453 is a human female with unique physical characteristics. She has spots on her face, a purple lip, and orange eyebrows. Her head is adorned with a purple cap and green hair. SCP-2453 also wears a medical mask and a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-2453's most notable trait is her exceptional observant and detail-oriented nature, which makes her an exceptional gift giver. However, due to her constant need for perfection, she can become overly stressed and spend an excessive amount of time trying to find the "perfect" gift for someone.

SCP-2453 is a reporter by profession, and any article or interview she conducts must be vetted by Foundation personnel before publication. SCP-2453 is aware of the existence of the anomalous but has not shown any indication of having any anomalous abilities herself.

Addendum: Note to all personnel: Please be advised that SCP-2453 is not to be used for personal gift finding purposes. Utilizing her abilities for personal gain is strictly prohibited and punishable by disciplinary action.
