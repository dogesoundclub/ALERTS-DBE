Item #: SCP-4515

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4515 is to be kept under surveillance at all times, housed in a standard containment unit with provisions provided to maintain a comfortable environment for its species. Any requests made by SCP-4515 are to be approved by a Level 3 researcher or higher.

Description: SCP-4515 is a male monkey of the species Macaca fascicularis, commonly known as a long-tailed macaque. It stands approximately 45 cm tall and weighs approximately 6 kg. SCP-4515 possesses a unique appearance as it wears a purple cap with green hair, and a gold chain around its neck. SCP-4515 also wears normal glasses, which it requires to maintain its vision.

SCP-4515 works in a fast food restaurant and shows great enthusiasm for exploring new opportunities and experiences. Its passion for adventure often leads it to take risks, which can sometimes result in humorous stories. SCP-4515 has been noted to survive a near-death experience while bungee jumping and accidentally getting lost in the wilderness.

SCP-4515 has demonstrated a high level of intelligence and has shown the ability to understand human speech, as well as communicate through sign language. It has been noted that SCP-4515 possesses rudimentary tool-making abilities and may use simple tools to aid in tasks.

SCP-4515 has shown to have a mischievous personality at times, often playing pranks on researchers and staff. However, it has not displayed any signs of violent or aggressive behavior.

Addendum 4515-A: SCP-4515 has expressed interest in exploring the exterior world beyond containment. Attempts to grant its request have been denied due to containment protocol, but research into the possibility of providing supervised excursions for SCP-4515 is ongoing.
