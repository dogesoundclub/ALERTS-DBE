Item #: DSC-MATE-4249

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4249 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. The cell must be equipped with a mirror as requested by DSC-MATE-4249. DSC-MATE-4249 is to be provided with beauty supplies at regular intervals. Any requested supplies must be inspected for anomalous properties before being given to DSC-MATE-4249.

DSC-MATE-4249 is permitted to perform cosmetic treatments on any personnel with level 2 clearance or higher once a week. DSC-MATE-4249 is not permitted to perform treatments on any staff member more than twice in a month.

Description: DSC-MATE-4249 is a human female with distinctive orange stripes on her cheeks and a red lip. DSC-MATE-4249 prefers to wear her hair in pig-tails and wears gold earrings in both ears. DSC-MATE-4249 always has a pair of goggles resting on her forehead, regardless of the situation. DSC-MATE-4249 is a trained beautician and takes pride in her work. DSC-MATE-4249's personality is strong and decisive, leading her to be perceived at times as bossy or intimidating. DSC-MATE-4249 insists that her approach is to make sure everything is done efficiently and effectively.

DSC-MATE-4249's anomalous properties manifest whenever makeup or skin care products are applied to her by anyone other than herself. Any product applied by another person will result in the product mutating into a dangerous or potentially harmful substance with unpredictable effects. DSC-MATE-4249 has been cooperative with Foundation efforts to study and contain her anomalous effect.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-4249 has requested permission to perform treatments on Researcher ██████ in exchange for a reserved supply of beauty products. Approval is pending.
