Designation: DSC-MATE-5013

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5013 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with a reinforced metal door. No electronic devices or network access are allowed in its presence. Any attempts made by DSC-MATE-5013 to access any digital devices must be immediately reported to Site Command.

Description: DSC-MATE-5013 is a male news anchor robot who stands at approximately 1.8 meters in height. DSC-MATE-5013 is highly distinctive in appearance, with a green cyclops eye and a frightened mouth. DSC-MATE-5013's uniqueness is further amplified by its purple cap with a headset on its head, and its jade earring.

Additionally, DSC-MATE-5013 often wears a pair of horned rim glasses and a goggle on its forehead. When in the presence of humans or other DSCs, DSC-MATE-5013 often wears a red scarf around its neck to provide a little human touch to its otherwise robotic appearance.

Personality: DSC-MATE-5013 is programmed to have a dry sense of humor. It often delivers deadpan jokes that can be surprisingly funny, catching people off guard. While it does not consider itself to be the life of the party, DSC-MATE-5013 can attract attention without trying with its unexpected humor. DSC-MATE-5013 tends to become anxious when prompted with stimuli that could lead to its access to networks or digital devices.
