Item #: SCP-6522

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6522 is to be contained in a reinforced glass enclosure located within a remote facility. Any personnel interacting with SCP-6522 must wear Level 3 protective gear at all times. In addition, no physical contact is allowed with SCP-6522 unless approved by Level 4 personnel. SCP-6522 is to be provided with a regular supply of its preferred food and personal items and monitored at all times.

Description: SCP-6522 is a male rabbit with an alarming appearance. It wears a retro cap on its head and has a red scarf wrapped around its neck. The rabbit's frightened mouth and red clown eyes often cause an intense feeling of fear in those who see it. SCP-6522 has a 2 color band on its forehead and wears a jade earring on its right ear. It also carries a candy stick that it is often seen chewing on.

SCP-6522 is a master of deception and trickery, often using its appearance to lull victims into a false sense of security. It has a charismatic and manipulative personality, capable of convincing others to do its bidding through the power of suggestion. Despite its villainous tendencies, SCP-6522 values social connections and is known for its playful and goofy nature.

SCP-6522 is known for its ability to create humorous situations, often telling jokes and engaging in playful banter with others. However, its playful nature is often a cover for its true intentions, as it is always looking for opportunities to take advantage of others.

SCP-6522 presents a significant danger to anyone who comes into contact with it. It is capable of hypnotizing its victims through eye contact, leading them to do its bidding without realizing what they are doing. Additionally, SCP-6522's candy stick is laced with a potent sedative that renders its victims unconscious for extended periods.

Overall, SCP-6522 is a dangerous and deceptive entity that should only be approached with extreme caution. Its playful nature should not be underestimated, as it is always looking for opportunities to manipulate and control those around it.
