Item #: 4186

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 4186 is to be contained within a reinforced steel cage. The cage must be electrified and equipped with a tranquilizer dart system in case of escape attempts. Personnel entering the containment area must wear full protective gear due to the subject's highly aggressive and dangerous nature.

Description: Subject 4186 is a male Bengal tiger with unique blue clown eyes. The subject's head is adorned with green hair in a purple cap and a medical mask conceals the mouth. Additionally, the tiger possesses two color-banded features on its forehead and a gold chain around its neck.

Personality: Despite the animalistic nature of its species and appearance, the subject displays a surprisingly empathetic and compassionate personality. It has been observed that the subject has a tendency to take on other people's problems as its own, often feeling responsible for fixing everything and everyone around it. The subject's empathetic nature can be both admirable and dangerous, especially when it is combined with its highly aggressive tendencies.

Notes: Subject 4186 is considered one of the most dangerous villains known to the Foundation. It is highly intelligent, intuitive, and vindictive, making it a formidable opponent. Its unique attributes and personality traits make it a highly sought-after research subject, but also one of the most perilous - handle with extreme caution.
