Item #: SCP-2070

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2070 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-2070 is to be granted only by the Level 3 personnel, with approval from the Site Director.

SCP-2070 is to be provided with their favorite brand of cigarettes and non-alcoholic beverages. Security cameras in SCP-2070's containment chamber must be monitored at all times to ensure the safety of the personnel.

Description: SCP-2070 is a male humanoid with a frightened mouth and blue clown eyes. The subject's head is covered with green hair in a purple cap, with a goggle on his forehead. SCP-2070 is always seen wearing a red scarf, and a green cross earring on each ear.

SCP-2070 claims to be a theater manager in the city, but his employment cannot be confirmed. However, investigations indicate that SCP-2070's persona fits the job description.

SCP-2070 has stubborn and organized behavior, which can cause humorous situations during unexpected changes or challenges. The subject shows no anomalous abilities. However, SCP-2070 has displayed an extreme addiction to cigarettes and may show agitation if not provided with its favorite brand.

Addendum: SCP-2070 has expressed a desire to host a theater play in their containment chamber. This request is currently under review.
