Item #: 3853

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3853 is to be kept under close observation at all times, in a specially designed habitat resembling a forested park. Any attempt to remove DSC-MATE-3853 from its enclosure must first be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-3853 is a male rabbit, standing at approximately 45cm tall and weighing 2kg. It has a normal rabbit-like form, with the exception of its eyes and ears. Its eyes are entities of virtual reality (VR) jade LED, while it wears a jade earring on its right ear.

DSC-MATE-3853's personality is erratic at times, displaying moments of forgetfulness or distraction, which can result in comedic situations. Nonetheless, caution must be taken around DSC-MATE-3853, as its unpredictability may pose a potential danger.

Recent studies reveal that DSC-MATE-3853 is capable of speech, communicating fluently in English. DSC-MATE-3853 claims to be a politician from an alternate universe, seeking diplomatic relationships with Earth-kind. While there is no evidence to support its claim, personnel are advised to treat DSC-MATE-3853 with courteousness as if its statement is valid.

Addendum 1: It has come to the Foundation's attention that a group of non-affiliate entities are interested in kidnapping DSC-MATE-3853, believing it to be an otherworldly entity to inherit supernatural powers with its capture. Task Force MTF-Rho-27 (Pester's Pranksters) is authorized for undercover investigation to detain any such group before carrying out this mission.

Addendum 2: During a routine health check, several nanobots were found in DSC-MATE-3853's system, indicating it had been infected with foreign technology. After removing the nanobots and examining them, DSC-MATE-3853 claimed that a group it met in a recent meeting had planted them. Further research into the incident is ongoing.
