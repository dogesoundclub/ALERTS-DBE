Item #: SCP-9290

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9290 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. They should be provided with grooming utensils and supplies as requested. Any requests for access to beauty products or salon equipment must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel.

SCP-9290 is to be monitored at all times and any attempts at conning, manipulation, or other inappropriate behavior must be reported immediately.

Description: SCP-9290 is a male tiger, standing at 6'4", with a proportionate build. They wear horn-rimmed glasses over their amber eyes and a classic police cap on their head. Their forehead features a distinctive red and white 2-color band.

SCP-9290 is fond of accessorizing and is often seen wearing a jade earring on their left ear, a gold chain necklace around their neck, and a whistle on a lanyard. They have a natural charm and charisma that can make them the life of the party, often telling jokes or doing silly dances to make others laugh.

SCP-9290's job prior to containment was as a beautician, specializing in hair styling and grooming. They are highly skilled in their craft and often request access to grooming utensils and supplies.

SCP-9290 has demonstrated a high level of intelligence and resourcefulness, using their charm to manipulate others to their advantage. They have also shown a propensity for conning and deceiving others for personal gain.

Addendum: Due to SCP-9290's nature, they have been granted limited access to the general population as a means of gathering information and intelligence. However, caution is advised when interacting with SCP-9290 and any attempts at manipulation or deception should be reported immediately.
