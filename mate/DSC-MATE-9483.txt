Item #: SCP-9483

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9483 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Recreational materials and access to fast food is permitted upon request, as long as it does not interfere with designated working times. SCP-9483 is required to wear a tracking bracelet at all times.

Description: SCP-9483 is a female human of average height and build. It is distinguishable from standard humans by orange stripes on its face and a jade earring in its left ear. SCP-9483 also wears a gold chain that it refuses to remove.

SCP-9483 is currently employed as a fast food worker at a major chain restaurant in ████████, USA. When on the job, it displays a strong sense of duty and responsibility, making it an efficient and reliable employee. However, when not working, it has demonstrated an ability to let loose and have fun, often surprising those around it with its impressive dance moves and silly antics.

It has been observed that SCP-9483 has a heightened sense of empathy and is able to remain calm and collected even in stressful situations. It has expressed a desire to help others and has been cooperative with researchers.

Addendum: SCP-9483 has shown no signs of anomalous behavior or abilities beyond its unique appearance and personality traits. Research is ongoing to determine the significance of its distinctive characteristics.
