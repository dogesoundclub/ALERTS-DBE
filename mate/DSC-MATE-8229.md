Item #: DSC-MATE-8229

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8229 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell located within a secure facility. All instances of DSC-MATE-8229's personal belongings, including its gas mask and accessories, are to be kept in a separate containment locker. DSC-MATE-8229 is permitted to have access to art supplies and materials to maintain its profession as a fashion designer. Contact with DSC-MATE-8229 is only permitted with the approval of Level 3 personnel or higher.

Description: DSC-MATE-8229, also known as "The Rebellious Designer," is a humanoid of the species Homo sapiens, identified as a male. DSC-MATE-8229's distinguishing features include orange-striped facial markings, VR rainbow LED eyes, green hair in a purple cap, and a green cross earring on its left ear. DSC-MATE-8229 is commonly seen wearing a red scarf and a normal gas mask.

DSC-MATE-8229 exhibits a strong sense of independence and is often resistant to authority or rules it perceives as unnecessary. This rebellious behavior is often manifested in its work as a fashion designer or personal life, such as sneaking out early or taking time off work without permission. DSC-MATE-8229's disobedience has caused numerous incidents, including unauthorized fashion shows, which have gained widespread attention in the fashion industry.

DSC-MATE-8229 has demonstrated an exceptional talent for fashion design and has exhibited the ability to create anomalous fashion items that grant wearers unique abilities (see Addendum-8229-1). DSC-MATE-8229's unique skill set and abilities make it a valuable resource to the Foundation if properly utilized.

Addendum-8229-1: Sample of DSC-MATE-8229's anomalous fashion items:

- "The Invisibility Cloak": A cloak that renders the wearer invisible to the naked eye for up to 30 minutes.

- "The Strength Suit": A suit that increases the wearer's strength by 50% for up to 15 minutes.

- "The Speed Shoes": Shoes that grant the wearer the ability to move at increased speeds for up to 20 minutes.

Addendum-8229-2: DSC-MATE-8229 has expressed its desire to remain in the fashion industry while working with the Foundation. Approved requests for DSC-MATE-8229 to participate in fashion shows, on behalf of the Foundation, are to be considered on a case-by-case basis.

*Note: The Foundation's decision to utilize DSC-MATE-8229's talents for fashion shows may result in the possibility of an increased number of anomalous items being produced and circulated within society. Caution should be exercised when making a decision.
