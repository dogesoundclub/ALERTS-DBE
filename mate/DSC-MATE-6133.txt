Object Classification: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6133 is to be allowed to live as a normal human, with regular check-ins from Foundation agents. SCP-6133's fashion designs must be monitored for any anomalies and any clothing made by SCP-6133 must be approved by the Foundation before being sold in the public domain.

Description: SCP-6133 is a human female, identifying as a fashion designer. She is often seen wearing a purple cap with a headset, accented by a red cross earring and a two-color band on her forehead. SCP-6133 also wears a choker as an accessory.

SCP-6133 has a tendency to act impulsively and recklessly, leading to humorous mishaps and accidents. She often attempts to impress others, leading to precarious situations. SCP-6133's talent in fashion design has yet to show anomalous qualities.

SCP-6133's backstory and origin remain unknown to the Foundation. Possible links to other SCP objects are being investigated.

Addendum: SCP-6133's fashion designs have been approved for public release. All SCP-6133 related merchandise will be monitored for potential anomalous activity.
