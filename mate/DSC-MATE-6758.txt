SCP Foundation Description:

Item #: SCP-6758

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6758 is to be contained within a standard human containment cell at Site-██. Access to SCP-6758 is limited to Level 3 personnel and above. Daily psychological evaluations are to be conducted to monitor SCP-6758's mental state.

Description: SCP-6758 appears to be a male human of average height and build, with clown blue eyes and a unique mouth spot. On his head is a red bucket hat, and he wears a silver chain around his neck. His ears are pierced with a jade earring, and he often carries a candy stick with him.

SCP-6758's anomalous properties manifest in his deep empathy and understanding for others. He is easily moved by the pain and suffering of others, often leading to emotional outbursts and tears. SCP-6758 also exhibits a fondness for animals and nature, frequently expressing concern for their well-being.

SCP-6758's current occupation is as a news anchor, and it is believed that his emotional sensitivity aids in his ability to connect with viewers. However, due to the potential for emotional distress and instability, SCP-6758's exposure to negative news stories must be limited.

Addendum: During psychological evaluations, SCP-6758 has expressed a desire to use his abilities to help others in a more direct manner. Requests for SCP-6758 to participate in humanitarian efforts are being considered.
