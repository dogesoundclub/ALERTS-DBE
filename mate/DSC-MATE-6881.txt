Item #: SCP-6881

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6881 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber must be equipped with a visual and audio recording system, to ensure the safety of personnel entering the chamber. Daily psychological evaluations must be conducted for SCP-6881, to monitor their mental state and any potential changes in their behavior.

SCP-6881 is to be allowed to engage in farming activities within a designated area at Site-██, under strict supervision. They must wear a medical mask at all times when outside their containment chamber, to prevent contamination of the facility.

Description: SCP-6881 is a human male, approximately 60 years of age, with green layers on their head, blue clown eyes and a mouth spot. They possess a clown's mouth, which has been surgically attached to their face. SCP-6881's ears are adorned with a green cross earring and wear a silver chain as an accessory.

SCP-6881 has a playful personality, loves to tell jokes and stories that have been passed down through their family and community. SCP-6881 values tradition and has a no-nonsense attitude, which may lead to humorous situations.

SCP-6881 was discovered in ████, Colorado, where they worked as a farmer. According to local rumors, SCP-6881 had anomalous abilities to grow crops at an abnormal rate, but these claims have not been substantiated.

Addendum: SCP-6881 has requested the Foundation to investigate anomalous activity in their hometown. Investigation is pending approval by O5-██.
