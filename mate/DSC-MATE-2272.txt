Item #: SCP-2272

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2272 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. SCP-2272's access to the outside world is to be restricted to necessary outings for work purposes only. Any contact with SCP-2272 must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-2272 is a female human with spotted facial features and a distinctive purple lip. SCP-2272's head is an unusual metal blue, which SCP-2272 claims is the result of a surgical enhancement to improve her cognitive abilities, allowing her to better manage the theater she oversees.

SCP-2272's personality is described as strong and decisive, exhibiting traits of a task-oriented leader. They can come across as bossy and intimidating, but it is simply a reflection of their desire for efficient and effective results. SCP-2272 has demonstrated exceptional organizational skills and has been integral in the growth and success of the theater they manage.

SCP-2272 displays no anomalous abilities or traits, with the exception of their distinctive appearance, metallic head, and exceptional cognitive abilities. SCP-2272 is not considered dangerous and has shown no signs of hostility towards Foundation personnel.

Addendum: SCP-2272 has expressed a desire to expand the theater's offerings to include more unconventional performances. Foundation personnel are encouraged to monitor these developments closely and report any suspicious or anomalous behavior.
