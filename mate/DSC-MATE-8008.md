Item #: DSC-MATE-8008

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8008 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The subject must be restrained at all times and equipped with a shock restraint system in case of any aggressive behavior. Personnel must wear eye protection when interacting with DSC-MATE-8008 due to its nerdy glasses. Any communication with DSC-MATE-8008 must be monitored by no less than two Level-2 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-8008 is a male human with distinct physical features such as spots on his face, a mohawk on his head, and a jade earring on his ear. The subject always wears a medical mask, but it has been determined that this is purely for fashion purposes. DSC-MATE-8008 displays extensive energy and enthusiasm, and often tries to rally others around him to share his interests, which can range from benign to potentially dangerous.

DSC-MATE-8008 can become volatile when its plans are denied, which is why security personnel must be ordered to remain cautious in his presence. Despite its enthusiastic nature, DSC-MATE-8008 lacks self-awareness and is often unaware of the potential consequences of its actions, which can be destructive and cause chaos to the environment in which it operates.

Personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-8008 must be careful as being around it for an extended period or allowing DSC-MATE-8008 to talk about its interests can cause memetic contagion, which can lead to obsessive behaviors, paranoia, and even loss of sanity in some individuals.

DSC-MATE-8008 is categorized as a villain, as its aim is to relentlessly pursue its interests and goals, regardless of the ethical or moral implications. DSC-MATE-8008 is to be put on indefinite surveillance, monitored regularly for changes in behavior, interests, and memetic hazards.
