Item #: 4472

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Item 4472 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. Access is only permitted to Level 2 personnel and above, and only with the approval of at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: Item 4472 is a male monkey of unknown origin, standing approximately 1.2 meters in height. The subject is often seen wearing normal glasses, a red scarf, and a gold earring in its left ear. Item 4472 also carries a whistle at all times. Its head is not arranged in any particular way and is green in coloration.

Item 4472 is capable of communicating in human language, and has been known to display a high level of intelligence when engaged in conversation. It frequently engages in debates and discussions with other individuals, and has a tendency to become argumentative.

Despite its argumentative nature, Item 4472 is generally considered to be a friendly and approachable individual. It has been observed to have a good sense of humor, and often uses its debating skills to make light of otherwise serious situations.

Addendum: Item 4472 has proven to be an effective lawyer, often arguing on behalf of other SCP objects during hearings and negotiations with Foundation staff. Its ability to analyze information quickly and logically has made it an invaluable resource in resolving disputes and mediating conflicts. However, caution is advised when interacting with the subject, as its argumentative tendencies may lead to unnecessary conflict or tension.
