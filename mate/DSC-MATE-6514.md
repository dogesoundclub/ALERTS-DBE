Item #: DSC-MATE-6514

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6514 is to be contained in a standard humanoid object containment chamber at Site-███. Access to DSC-MATE-6514 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and higher.

DSC-MATE-6514 is to be interviewed and evaluated monthly to monitor its psychological state. All requests for cosmetic services must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-6514 appears to be a human male in his mid-30s, measuring approximately 180 cm in height and weighing roughly 70 kg. DSC-MATE-6514's eyes are always shielded by a pair of nerdy glasses. It is commonly seen wearing a blue bandana as a headwear and a gold earring on its right ear.

DSC-MATE-6514 adorns itself with a red scarf and LED gas mask, which does not hinder its ability to perform its beautician job. 

DSC-MATE-6514's personality is characterized by an ambitious and goal-oriented nature. It has a reputation of taking on more than it can handle but manages to pull it off due to its strategic thinking and excellent organizational skills. 

DSC-MATE-6514 has the ability to enhance the appearance of biological organisms, including humans, by various cosmetic services. Testing revealed that DSC-MATE-6514's ability to improve the physical appearance appears to be limited to superficial levels.

Addendum: On ██/██/20██, DSC-MATE-6514 was discovered working at a salon in downtown █████████. Amnestics were administered to the civilians involved, and DSC-MATE-6514 was successfully transferred into Foundation custody.
