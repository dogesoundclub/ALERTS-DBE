Item #: 2399

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2399 is to be kept under constant surveillance at all times. Personnel interacting with SCP-2399 must wear level A hazmat suits and gas masks. SCP-2399 is to be confined to a standard humanoid containment cell with reinforced walls and no access to any communication devices.

Description: SCP-2399 is a male humanoid individual of average height and weight with distinctive facial features. SCP-2399 has several spots on their face, a retro cap on their head, and blue clown eyes. SCP-2399 wears a gold earring and a medical mask covering their mouth. SCP-2399 is usually seen wearing a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-2399 is a politician by profession and is believed to possess a high level of influence over local and national government officials.

SCP-2399's personality is characterized by their high level of empathy and compassion towards others. However, SCP-2399's desire to fix everything can be both admirable and draining for those around them.

SCP-2399 is known to have a significant psychological effect on individuals who come in contact with him. These individuals report feelings of guilt, fear, and paranoia. SCP-2399's ability to manipulate emotions and thoughts makes him a significant risk to the general public.

Addendum: Due to SCP-2399's significant risk to society, the O5 Council has approved the utilization of class C amnestics on all personnel who interact with SCP-2399. However, SCP-2399 remains a significant threat to the safety and security of the Foundation and must be contained at all costs.
