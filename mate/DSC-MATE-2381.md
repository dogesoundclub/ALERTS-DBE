Item #: 2381

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2381 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with amenities for artistic expression as needed. Interaction with DSC-MATE-2381 is approved for approved researchers and staff. No unauthorized personnel are to interact with DSC-MATE-2381.

Description: DSC-MATE-2381 is a humanoid female designated as a fashion designer. Her physical appearance is notable for her red lips and bright orange eyebrows. She sports a purple cap with a headset and a pair of goggles resting on her forehead.

DSC-MATE-2381 displays a creative and artistic side that is expressed through her fashion designs. She frequently indulges in various hobbies and interests such as painting, drawing, and writing. Researchers have found that her art has a unique and anomalous quality that is capable of enchanting anyone who views her creations.

DSC-MATE-2381 displays a personable nature and has an affinity for attention, especially concerning her artistic expressions. She sometimes peddles her creations to other personnel and will often inquire about their opinion on her creations.

Addendum: After initial containment, DSC-MATE-2381 expressed herself as a willing party to Foundation collaboration. Currently, DSC-MATE-2381 is the designated tailoring specialist for MTF Sigma-16 ("The Otherworldly Tailors"), a mobile task force deployed for scenarios that require blending into supernatural communities. Her designs have proven invaluable in the acquisition of artifacts, as many artifacts are only obtainable after meeting specific dress codes.
