Item #: SCP-3392

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3392 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment chamber, equipped with standard security measures. Access to SCP-3392 is strictly prohibited unless authorized by Level 3 personnel or higher. SCP-3392 is to be outfitted with a standard gas mask at all times, unless explicitly authorized otherwise.

Description: SCP-3392 is a female human police officer with distinct facial features. SCP-3392 is distinguished by a series of spots across their face, coupled with an orange eyebrow and a charming smile. The subject is further identified by a pair of goggles worn atop their forehead and a gold chain around their neck.

Despite their role in law enforcement, SCP-3392 displays a distinct love for adventure and a willingness to take risks, which is reflected in their personality. The subject's carefree attitude is often noted as the catalyst for humorous anecdotes involving their near-death experiences, which include bungee jumping and getting lost in the wilderness. SCP-3392's behavior has not yet affected their ability to fulfill their duties as a police officer.

SCP-3392's gas mask is a standard edition and is required to be worn at all times during containment procedures. Any unauthorized removal of said mask may result in immediate termination. SCP-3392's anomalous properties, if there are any, are currently unknown. Further research and study into SCP-3392 is ongoing.
