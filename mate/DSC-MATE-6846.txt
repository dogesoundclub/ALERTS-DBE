Item #: SCP-6846

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6846 is to be kept in a secure facility at all times. Access to SCP-6846 is limited to Level 3 personnel or above, and any interaction with SCP-6846 must be conducted under strict observation. Personnel assigned to SCP-6846 must wear full protective clothing at all times to prevent contamination.

Description: SCP-6846 is a female human, identified as a doctor. SCP-6846's face is covered with spots, and she has a noticeable smile. SCP-6846's eyes emit a jade green glow, and she wears a hoodie over her head at all times. SCP-6846's mouth is covered with a medical mask, and her voice is muffled when speaking.

SCP-6846 appears to have a deep understanding of the emotions of others and is often moved to tears or emotional outbursts when witnessing the pain or suffering of another. SCP-6846 also appears to have a fondness for animals and nature, often exhibiting a nurturing behavior towards them.

SCP-6846's empathic abilities have displayed some cognitive altering effects on test subjects, making it difficult to determine the extent of SCP-6846's abilities. It is recommended that SCP-6846 be tested further to establish the limits of her abilities.

Addendum: SCP-6846's abilities are currently under investigation for potential use in the Foundation's medical wing. Further testing is required to ensure SCP-6846's safety, as well as the safety of the subjects she interacts with.
