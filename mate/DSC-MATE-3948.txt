Item #: SCP-3948

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3948 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Any interaction with SCP-3948 must be approved by at least one Level 3 researcher, and all communication must be closely monitored. Under no circumstances should SCP-3948 be allowed access to any sensitive information regarding the Foundation or its activities. In the event of a breach, security personnel are authorized to use non-lethal force to bring SCP-3948 back into containment.

Description: SCP-3948 is a humanoid robot, measuring approximately 1.8 meters in height. SCP-3948 resembles a male of the species and has only one eye, which is green and resembles a cyclops eye. SCP-3948 is often seen wearing a blue bandana and nerd glasses over its eye. It also wears a gold chain and carries a whistle as accessories.

SCP-3948's personality is characterized by impulsivity and a tendency to act on emotions without thinking through all consequences. This behavior can lead to humorous or surprising situations, which may be amusing for those around SCP-3948. However, this behavior is also a cause for concern as it may lead to SCP-3948 engaging in dangerous or unpredictable activities.

SCP-3948's job as a lawyer is not clear, though it is believed to have been created to provide legal assistance to an unknown individual or organization. SCP-3948 has not displayed any anomalous abilities or properties, but its origin and current purpose remain a mystery.

Addendum: Following Incident 3948-A, all access to the SCP-3948 containment chamber requires prior approval from at least two Level 3 researchers. SCP-3948 is not to be used for any purpose outside of legal counsel for the Foundation, and all communication with SCP-3948 must be monitored for potential security breaches.
