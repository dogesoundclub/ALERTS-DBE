Item #: SCP-516

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-516 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. They are allowed access to one authorized cigarette per day.

Description: SCP-516 is a human female who works as a police officer. Their uniform consists of a purple cap with a headset and goggles on the forehead, jade earrings, and a silver chain. They also often carry a lit cigarette.

SCP-516's most notable feature is their blue clown eyes, which have been confirmed to be a natural mutation. They have a friendly demeanor and often greet others with a smile. SCP-516 is known to be laid-back and easy-going, but they are fiercely loyal and protective of their loved ones.

SCP-516 has been documented to become confrontational with individuals who insult or harm their friends or family members. These situations have been known to result in humorous arguments or scuffles.

SCP-516 was brought to the Foundation's attention after several reports of their blue clown eyes causing discomfort and fear among civilians. SCP-516 agreed to containment and has since complied with all containment procedures.

Addendum: SCP-516 has requested access to VHS tapes of the television show "Night Court". Request is pending approval from Site Director.
