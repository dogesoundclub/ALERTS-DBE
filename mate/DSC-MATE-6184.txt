Item #: SCP-6184

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6184 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. The medical mask worn by SCP-6184 must be replaced every 24 hours to prevent contamination. All personnel are advised to keep a distance of at least 2 meters from SCP-6184 due to the unpredictable nature of their practicality.

Description: SCP-6184 appears to be a male human with green hair and VR rainbow LED eyes. Notable features include spots on their face and a frightened mouth expression. SCP-6184 wears a medical mask at all times and a silver chain around their neck. They also have a green cross earring on their right ear.

SCP-6184's personality is characterized by a high degree of practicality and ingenuity. They are often observed fixing broken objects with unorthodox methods such as duct tape or paper clips. SCP-6184 also has a tendency to rig up makeshift solutions to complex problems, often with great success.

SCP-6184's anomalous properties are limited to their appearance and personality traits. No other anomalous properties have been observed, and SCP-6184 is considered safe for containment. Any further testing or experimentation must first receive approval from at least one Level 3 personnel.
