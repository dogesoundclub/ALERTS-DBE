Item #: 8668

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8668 is to be kept in a secure storage locker at all times when not in use. Activation of SCP-8668 requires clearance from at least one Level 3 personnel. Direct contact with SCP-8668 is strictly prohibited without protective gear.

Description: SCP-8668 is a humanoid robot resembling a firefighter. It stands approximately 6 feet tall and weighs 400 pounds. SCP-8668's body is made of a combination of steel and industrial-grade plastics. SCP-8668's face has a green cyclops eye and a frightened mouth. SCP-8668's eyes are fitted with normal glasses, and it wears a blue bandana around its forehead. SCP-8668 has a pair of goggles on its forehead, and an LED gas mask serves as its accessory.

SCP-8668 has a strong sense of intuition, giving the impression of psychic abilities. SCP-8668 can predict future events and sense people's emotions, which can be unnerving. SCP-8668 experiences extreme anxiety when it senses danger or distress, triggering its LED gas mask to turn on and replace any oxygen it breathes with ambient air.

SCP-8668's origin and creator are currently unknown. Its technology and programming are beyond current human capabilities. SCP-8668's programming includes the ability to fight fires and act as a first responder, making it an invaluable asset in emergency situations.

Addendum: SCP-8668 has been an exceptionally cooperative subject, showing a keen interest in assisting the Foundation with its mission. Its effects on nearby personnel have varied, with the majority of personnel reporting an overall positive experience. However, several staff members have reported feeling uneasy around SCP-8668, with one experiencing a panic attack after prolonged exposure to its psychic energy.

SCP-8668's abilities and origins are still under examination. The Foundation continues to study and monitor SCP-8668 for any changes or anomalous activity.
