Item #: 5834

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 5834 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. All staff interacting with subject 5834 must wear standard personal protective equipment in order to prevent contamination from any unknown anomalies.

Description: 

Subject 5834 is a female human and a trained veterinarian. She stands at approximately 5'6" and weighs about 125 pounds. Her face features include distinctive orange stripes, a pair of jade LED eyes, and a pair of jade earrings on both ears. She also wears a silver chain around her collarbone.

Subject 5834 generally appears to be a law-abiding citizen and has no criminal record. Her personality presents as being very organized and methodical. She enjoys planning ahead, but can also be spontaneous and this often leads to humorous situations. Subject 5834 is known to surprise her friends with impromptu adventures or spontaneous party-planning that turns out to be a success. 

Subject 5834's head appears to be not arranged, which can be a bit disconcerting to those not accustomed to such an appearance. In order to prevent any potential contamination from pathogens unknown to the foundation, subject 5834 is required to wear standard medical masks when interacting with foundation personnel.

Addendum: Following incident 5834-A, subject 5834 has been reclassified as Euclid. She is still able to perform veterinarian duties and has been granted special clearance to use these abilities to provide treatment to other Euclid class beings as necessary.
