Item #: SCP-2638

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2638 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-2638 is restricted and requires Level 2 clearance. SCP-2638 is not to be allowed contact with any other anomalous objects without prior approval from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-2638 is a male rabbit of the species Oryctolagus cuniculus. SCP-2638 displays a frightened mouth as a permanent facial expression. SCP-2638's head is covered by a police cap, and a two-color band is visible on its forehead. SCP-2638 wears a silver chain around its neck, which appears to be of non-anomalous origin. SCP-2638 also regularly uses a cigarette as an accessory.

SCP-2638 is a police officer by profession and is commonly referred to by its legal name, Officer ██████. Personality testing has shown that SCP-2638 tends to be very spontaneous and often enjoys taking risks or doing things on a whim. This results in humorous situations, such as impulsively deciding to jump off a cliff into a lake or river. SCP-2638's behavior indicates a possible form of anomalous mental influence.

SCP-2638 was discovered after a series of reports concerning spontaneous thefts of anomalous objects in the city of [REDACTED]. Foundation agents found SCP-2638 in possession of several anomalous items, which it claimed were necessary for its duties as a police officer. SCP-2638 was taken into custody and transported to Site-██ for containment.

Addendum-2638-A: SCP-2638 has shown a preference for loud music and appears to enjoy listening to it during its leisure time. Requests for the provision of a boombox have been denied. SCP-2638 is allowed access to non-anomalous music devices for entertainment purposes only.
