Item #: SCP-4382

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4382 is to be monitored at all times and is prohibited from operating outside of authorized areas. All contact with SCP-4382 must be supervised by Level 3 personnel or higher. SCP-4382 is to be provided with a pack of cigarettes every three hours, as they have been shown to serve as an effective calming agent.

Description: SCP-4382 appears to be a female traffic officer with notable distinguishing features. These include numerous spots on her face, red clown eyes, a green head with edge curl, and gold earrings. SCP-4382 is a human being of unknown origin.

SCP-4382 has displayed a strong sense of independence and resistance towards unnecessary rules and authority, which has resulted in rebellious behavior. Instances of SCP-4382 disregarding orders or leaving their post without permission have been documented. 

SCP-4382 is reassured by cigarettes and provided with them whenever possible. However, it is important to note that SCP-4382's cigarette consumption is monitored and restricted as necessary.

Addendum: SCP-4382 has been observed demonstrating anomalous abilities in high-pressure or life-threatening situations. Further research is ongoing to determine the extent of these abilities and if they pose any additional risk to containment.
