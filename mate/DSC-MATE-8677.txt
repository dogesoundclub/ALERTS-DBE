Item #: SCP-8677

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8677 is to be monitored at all times, with frequent psychological evaluations, and are to be provided with a designated, isolated workspace to avoid any distractions from their work. They are to be provided with any necessary tools and equipment upon request or as needed. All interactions with SCP-8677 are to be monitored for indications of anomalous behavior.

Description: SCP-8677 is a human female of average height and weight. Their most notable facial features include orange stripes along their cheekbones and red lips, giving their face a distinctive appearance. They have orange eyebrows and green hair in a purple cap. SCP-8677's ears are adorned with gold earrings, and they wear a medical mask over their mouth.

SCP-8677's personality is noted to be jovial and humorous, often making jokes and telling funny stories. Despite the seriousness of their position as a mechanic, SCP-8677 often lightens the mood with their amusing antics. It should be noted that their humor may not always be appropriate, and their behavior should be monitored accordingly.

SCP-8677's anomalous abilities have not yet been discovered, but their background and history are being investigated for possible links to any anomalies. All information regarding SCP-8677's past is classified until further notice.

Addendum: SCP-8677 has requested a silver chain as an accessory, and it has been approved. All necessary adjustments have been made to their containment unit accordingly.
