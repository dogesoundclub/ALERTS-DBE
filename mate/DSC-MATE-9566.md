Item #: DSC-MATE-9566

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9566 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with reinforced walls and ceilings. DSC-MATE-9566 is to be fed a diet of raw meat, 3 times a day, and is to be provided with medical equipment and medicine to attend to its own health needs. DSC-MATE-9566 is not to be in physical contact with any personnel outside of designated medical personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-9566 is a anomalous entity appearing to be a male Bengal tiger (Panthera tigris tigris) wearing a yellow bucket hat and a medical mask on its muzzle. The tiger possesses artificial jade LED eyes, an artificial jade earring on its left ear, and a golden chain around its neck. DSC-MATE-9566 has demonstrated the ability to communicate with human beings in English, albeit in a slightly rough, growling tone. DSC-MATE-9566 claims to be a doctor, and has the ability to heal and enhance the physical abilities of other entities, including human beings.

Personality: DSC-MATE-9566 is a very outgoing and social individual, and enjoys being the center of attention. It has a tendency to tell humorous stories or make jokes that are so funny, they have caused employees to burst out in laughter. DSC-MATE-9566 does not like to be alone and will seek out any human company. It has been known to be affectionate towards humans and often will give hugs or nuzzle them with its oversized head.

DSC-MATE-9566's abilities make it both valuable and dangerous, able to assist or compromised the health of individuals. As with all anomalous entities, caution should be taken when analyzing the most appropriate containment procedures.
