Item #: 9349

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9349 is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at all times when not on active duty. The containment chamber is to be fitted with anti-tampering devices and monitored by security personnel at all times. Access to SCP-9349's containment chamber is restricted to Level 3 personnel or higher.

Description: SCP-9349 is a humanoid robot with a male programming. It stands at approximately 6 feet tall and weighs approximately 200 pounds. Its appearance is that of a police officer, complete with a blue bandana covering its head, and a medical mask covering its mouth. Its eyes are a pair of horn-rimmed glasses, and it sports a gold chain around its neck as an accessory.

SCP-9349 possesses a natural talent for leadership, and has been known to take control of situations, whether it be in the workplace, social or personal life. It is a highly intelligent, intuitive and resourceful robot, with a strong sense of justice and loyalty. In addition, it has been reported to have a dry sense of humor.

SCP-9349 has been designed for use in law enforcement, and its programming reflects this. Its primary function is to keep the peace and uphold the law, and it is equipped with advanced sensory devices and weapons systems. Its internal systems are comprised of a highly complex microprocessor, memory storage units and an artificial intelligence matrix.

Addendum:

SCP-9349 has been designated as Euclid primarily due to its ability to take control of situations. SCP-9349 can be a highly effective asset in law enforcement, but its unpredictable nature requires it to be kept under close observation. In addition, any tampering with its programming or systems must be strictly prohibited, as this may result in unknown consequences.
