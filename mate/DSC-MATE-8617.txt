SCP Foundation Report

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8617 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell. Due to the subject's profession, only approved staff members may be granted access to SCP-8617 for legal purposes. In case of a containment breach, staff members are advised to stay calm and treat SCP-8617 like an ordinary human being.

Description: SCP-8617 is a male human being, identified by his DNA matching with that of a human. The subject's face features orange stripes and a smile. They wear nerd glasses, and having a mohawk hairstyle with a gold earring. SCP-8617 commonly wears a gold chain as an accessory.

Personality: SCP-8617 is idealistic, always rooting for ways to make the world a better place. They tend to be overly enthusiastic about social causes that go against their values. SCP-8617 can lead to some humorous situations, such as protesting or boycotting a company or cause they deem unfit, despite any rational reasons otherwise.

Addendum: SCP-8617 has been flagged by the Foundation for exhibiting unusual behavior in his court appearances. SCP-8617 has been observed to have a high success rate in defending the accused, regardless of the evidence presented by the prosecution. Further investigation is required to confirm any anomalous properties.
