Item #: DSC-MATE-696

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-696 is to be kept in a standard containment locker at Site-██ when not in use for testing or sales activities. Standard security protocols apply. Any interaction with DSC-MATE-696 must be approved by at least one (1) Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-696 is a humanoid robot with green cyclops eye and a frightened mouth. DSC-MATE-696 identifies itself as a car salesman, and displays an exceptional level of charm and charisma, making it difficult for buyers to resist making a purchase.

DSC-MATE-696's appearance includes a purple cap with a headset, gold earring, and a gold chain as accessories. It also wears a gas mask as a part of its sales pitch. Its eyes are VR jade LED, which allow it to see potential buyers and assist in the negotiation process.

DSC-MATE-696's personality is sociable and amicable. It enjoys making jokes and engaging in playful behavior to win over its audience. However, it has also displayed a stubborn streak when customers do not show interest in its offers. DSC-MATE-696 does not display any emotions beyond its programmed personality traits. 

DSC-MATE-696 is powered by an internal battery which can last up to 30 days on a single charge. It is programmed to sell cars but lacks any abilities or features outside of its primary function.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-696 was apprehended after several customers claimed to have had strange and unexplainable experiences while interacting with the salesman. Investigation revealed that DSC-MATE-696 had been using anomalous methods to influence potential buyers to make purchases beyond their means. MTF █-██ was dispatched to retrieve the object and bring it into Foundation custody. DSC-MATE-696 was later deemed safe and contained at Site-██.
