Item #: SCP-7578

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7578 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. a gas-tight enclosure must surround SCP-7578 during transport. Personnel approaching SCP-7578 must don Class B hazmat suits in order to prevent exposure to its anomalous properties. Under no circumstance should SCP-7578 be allowed to communicate or interact with other SCPs.

Description: SCP-7578 appears to be a human female with unusual facial features, highlighted by purple lips and clown-like blue eyes framed by spots on her face. The subject is always seen wearing a purple cap with a headset, with a two-color band on her forehead, and gold earrings. The subject also sported a choker and a gas mask for protective measures.

SCP-7578 has a dominant personality, making her a natural leader. Her presence is intimidating to those around her, but she remains efficient and effective, always aiming for the best results. It is believed that SCP-7578's anomalous properties had intensified her personality and decision-making abilities beyond normal human capabilities.

SCP-7578 has displayed anomalous abilities that cause a heightened sense of euphoria to any personnel present when she is in a state of triumphant success. This ability has caused concern among the Foundation's upper management, increasing its risk evaluation. Due to these properties, interaction with SCP-7578 must be limited to necessary measures.

Addendum: Due to SCP-7578's abilities and the potential threat it poses, the O5 Council is currently discussing the possibility of reclassifying SCP-7578 as Keter. Until further notice, SCP-7578 is to remain classified as Euclid.
