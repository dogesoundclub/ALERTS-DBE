Item #: SCP-9528

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9528 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-19. SCP-9528 is a human female, thus standard accommodations should be provided.

Description: SCP-9528 is a female human with orange stripes on her face and a constant smile. The clown-like blue eyes add to the overall comedic appearance of SCP-9528, as does the retro cap and gold chain accessory.

SCP-9528 works as a mechanic and has a great sense of humor, often telling funny stories or doing silly impersonations to lighten the mood. This behavior can sometimes lead to humorous situations, but agents are reminded to stay alert as SCP-9528 is known to use humor as a defense mechanism in stressful situations.

SCP-9528's personality has been described as jovial and extroverted. She enjoys making others laugh and may even become upset if her attempts at humor fail. Agents should be aware of this and remain professional while interacting with SCP-9528.

SCP-9528's anomalous properties have not yet been fully determined. Further testing is required to understand how her appearance and personality may affect those around her.
