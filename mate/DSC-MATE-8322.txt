Item #: SCP-8322

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8322 is to be contained in a standard Humanoid Containment Chamber at Site-██. All visitors to SCP-8322 must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-8322 must be injected with a sedative prior to any interaction.

Description: SCP-8322 is a male human identified as a doctor. SCP-8322 is known for its distinctive appearance, including multiple spots on its face and a bucket hat on its head. SCP-8322 often wears a gold chain and has a green cross earring on one of its ears.

SCP-8322 displays a highly impulsive personality and may engage in risky behavior on a whim. Foundation personnel are advised to exercise caution when interacting with SCP-8322. On numerous occasions, SCP-8322 has jumped off cliffs into bodies of water without any regard for its safety.

SCP-8322 displays a high level of intelligence and is well-versed in medical knowledge. Despite its impulsive tendencies, it has assisted in the healing of several injured Foundation personnel. SCP-8322's motivations for assisting personnel are currently unknown.

Addendum: On ██/██/20██, SCP-8322 broke containment and escaped from Site-██. SCP-8322 was apprehended and returned to containment with no recorded casualties. Due to SCP-8322's unpredictability, contingency plans for containment breach are to be updated and rehearsed on a bi-weekly basis.
