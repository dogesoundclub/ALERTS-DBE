Item #: DSC-MATE-1473

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1473 is currently located in a standard containment chamber at Site-██. Access to DSC-MATE-1473 is restricted to Level 2 personnel or higher.

DSC-MATE-1473 is to receive a specialized diet and living environment to ensure its well-being. Any medical needs will be attended to by on-site veterinary staff.

Description: DSC-MATE-1473 is a male rabbit, approximately 1.5 meters in height when standing upright. It wears a red beanie, horned rim glasses, and is often seen holding a candy stick.

DSC-MATE-1473's most distinctive feature is its frightened mouth, which it maintains even when not expressing fear. The rabbit's nurturing personality often leads it to care for its friends and family members as if they were its own offspring, leading to humorous situations.

DSC-MATE-1473 is capable of human speech and possesses knowledge of the legal system. It claims to have worked as a lawyer before being contained by the Foundation.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1473 has expressed a desire to continue working as a lawyer. Proposal to allow DSC-MATE-1473 to act as a legal advisor to the Foundation's legal team is currently under consideration.
