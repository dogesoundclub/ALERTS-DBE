SCP FOUNDATION FILE #9862

Item #: SCP-9862

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9862 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell, outfitted with a bed, toilet, and basic amenities. Access to SCP-9862 is to be granted solely for medical purposes, as per its designation as a licensed veterinarian. Any interactions with SCP-9862 must be monitored by at least two (2) trained security personnel.

SCP-9862 is not to be allowed any form of communication with non-foundation personnel without prior approval from at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-9862 is a tiger anthropomorphic entity, estimated to be approximately 2.2 meters tall standing upright. SCP-9862 typically wears a red scarf and gold earring, along with a candy stick.

SCP-9862 possesses blue clown eyes and a unique two-color band on its forehead. It has a personality that exudes natural charm and charisma, making it the life of the party. SCP-9862 often tells jokes, enjoys doing silly dances, and overall seeks to create humorous situations that lead to laughter and enjoyment for all involved.

SCP-9862 is a licensed veterinarian, specializing in the care of both normal and anomalous animals. SCP-9862 has been specially trained to administer medical and surgical procedures on any animals within the Foundation's custody.

Addendum: SCP-9862 has been approved to provide medical care for any animal anomalies held within Foundation custody. In the instances where SCP-9862 is unable to provide care for an animal, authorization must be obtained from Level 3 personnel for alternative care options. SCP-9862 has shown above-average medical and surgical skills, and its anomalous properties provide significant advantages in the treatment of anomalous animals.
