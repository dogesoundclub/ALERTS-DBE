Item #: 5293

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5293 is to be monitored at all times and his movements are to be restricted to a specialized train platform designated for his use. All personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-5293 are required to wear specialized protective gear to avoid potential exposure to DSC-MATE-5293's anomalous properties.

Description: DSC-MATE-5293 is a human male whose face is characterized by several large spots, a clown mouth, and red clown eyes. The subject's head is that of a normal blonde male with a medical mask covering his mouth. DSC-MATE-5293's ears are adorned with a single green cross earring.

DSC-MATE-5293 has the ability to quickly change the atmosphere of any given situation to a lighthearted one, often through the use of humor, jokes, and silly impersonations. However, this ability has been shown to have a profound impact on those nearby, leading to uncontrolled laughing fits and states of euphoria. 

Due to their excessive laughter and uncontrollable behavior near DSC-MATE-5293, all personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-5293 are regularly checked for signs of long term psychological effects caused by DSC-MATE-5293's anomalous properties.

DSC-MATE-5293 is employed as a train driver at a specialized train platform fitted with additional safety measures and personnel to prevent the effects of DSC-MATE-5293's abilities on the general population.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5293 is a valuable asset for personnel morale and general psychological well-being. The use of the DSC-MATE-5293 in controlled settings is an approved part of the Foundation's ethics program.
