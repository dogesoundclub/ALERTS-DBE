DSC-MATE-9669 is the boss of a local mafia organization that operates in various urban areas. It exhibits remarkable problem-solving abilities, often fixing things in unconventional ways. DSC-MATE-9669 may use duct tape or paper clips to repair a broken object, or rig up a makeshift solution to a problem that others would find too complicated.

Despite its small size and apparently harmless appearance, DSC-MATE-9669 is highly skilled in combat and has been implicated in several murders and other criminal activities. Its connections with other criminal organizations make it a significant threat to society.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-9669 has been observed using a unique vocalization to call upon its subordinates. This sound is both loud and high-pitched, causing discomfort to those not accustomed to it. The exact mechanism behind this ability is unknown.
