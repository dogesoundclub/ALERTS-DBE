Item #: SCP-6647

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6647 is to be contained in a standard human containment chamber and monitored by security personnel at all times. SCP-6647's access to communication devices is restricted to prevent any communication beyond approved channels. Any attempts to enter SCP-6647's containment chamber without proper authorization will result in immediate disciplinary action.

Description: SCP-6647 is a female humanoid entity who identifies herself as a banker. SCP-6647 possesses unique facial features, including multiple spots on the face and purple lips, as well as blue clown eyes.
