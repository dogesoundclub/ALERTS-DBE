Item #: 5790

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Item 5790 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Item 5790 is allowed access to reading materials but is not to be given any legal documents.

Description: Item 5790, also referred to as "The Lawyer," is a male humanoid of the Homo sapiens species. Item 5790 is 6'1" in height and weighs 178 lbs. Item 5790's most notable features are its Mouth Spot and Clown Mouth, which are located on the left and right side of its mouth, respectively.

Item 5790's eyes are blue and resemble those of a clown. Item 5790's head is adorned with green hair and a purple cap. Item 5790 also displays a green cross-shaped earring in its left ear and a silver chain around its neck.

Personality: Item 5790 possesses a strong sense of duty and responsibility despite its quirky appearance. It has been known to take on more work than it can handle, resulting in humorous situations. Item 5790 has a tendency to try to carry too many things at once and has difficulty fitting all of its tasks into a single day.

Addendum: Item 5790 was recovered from a law firm in downtown ██████. Agents were alerted to its presence due to its odd appearance and strange behavior. Item 5790 was taken into custody without incident and has been in containment ever since. It is not known how Item 5790 acquired its anomalous properties.
