Item #: SCP-4031

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4031 is to be allowed to work at its current fast food job, but must return to its assigned apartment at the end of every shift. The apartment is to be furnished with basic amenities and monitored for any potential breaches. SCP-4031 is not to be allowed outside without proper clearance from Site personnel.

Description: SCP-4031 is a human female with distinctive orange stripes marking its face. SCP-4031 possesses an unusual physical feature in the form of a wing extending from its head. The wing has been determined to be non-functional, and SCP-4031 reports that it does not cause any discomfort. SCP-4031 also wears a jade earring in each ear and a choker around its neck.

While SCP-4031's physical features are unusual, its personality is what sets it apart. SCP-4031 is highly organized and enjoys planning ahead. However, SCP-4031 also possesses a spontaneous side that can lead to humorous situations. SCP-4031 has been known to surprise friends with impromptu adventures or last-minute party planning that turns out to be a hit.

While SCP-4031 has shown no signs of violent behavior, it is recommended that caution be exercised in order to avoid any potential breaches of containment.

Addendum: SCP-4031 has been observed smoking cigarettes on occasion. While this poses no threat to containment, SCP-4031 has been advised to quit smoking for its own health.
