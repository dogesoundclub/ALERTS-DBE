Item #: 2542

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2542 is to be kept within a standard containment chamber located within Site-██. The chamber is to be fitted with a ventilation system, as well as various enrichment items to aid in preventing the onset of boredom and depression. SCP-2542 is to be provided with a selection of fruits and vegetables for sustenance.

SCP-2542 is not to be exposed to any human personnel outside of the designated research and security teams. Any unauthorized individuals who come into contact with SCP-2542 must be immediately isolated and given appropriate amnestics.

Description: SCP-2542 is a male monkey of unknown species, roughly 58 cm in height and weighing roughly 7 kg. SCP-2542 currently works for the Foundation as a security guard, patrolling various sites across the globe.

SCP-2542 wears a bucket-style hat with a two-colored band around the forehead, a gold chain around the neck, and frequently holds a cigarette in its mouth. The eyes of SCP-2542 are currently unknown.

SCP-2542 exhibits a scatterbrained personality, with a tendency to get sidetracked easily or forgetful of things. However, SCP-2542 has proven to be a diligent and reliable employee, with a strong sense of loyalty towards the Foundation.

SCP-2542 has shown no signs of hostility towards human personnel, but it is recommended not to provoke or annoy SCP-2542, as it may turn hostile if it feels threatened or irritated.

Addendum: SCP-2542 has exhibited an odd phenomenon, in which it seems to have an innate sense of direction and can navigate through unfamiliar terrain with ease, which is believed to be part of an anomalous ability. Further testing is required to fully understand the extent of this ability.
