Item #: 8889

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 8889, also known as the "Tiger Fast Food Worker," is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell must be equipped with a standard ventilation system, two security cameras, and one steel door reinforced for maximum security. Any interaction with Subject 8889 must be carried out by Level 2 personnel or higher, after obtaining clearance from Site Director.

Description: Subject 8889 appears to be a male tiger of ████████ descent, standing at approximately 6 feet tall. It wears a blue police cap with a golden emblem, and a pair of goggles on its forehead. It is often seen wearing a silver chain around its neck, which it claims to have found on the streets. Subject 8889 is employed at the local fast food restaurant and is allowed to carry out work duties within the restaurant.

Personality: Despite its intimidating appearance, Subject 8889 exhibits an extremely friendly and approachable personality. It has a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards its job and performs all tasks to the best of its ability. However, it also knows how to have fun and often surprises others with its dance moves and silly antics on the dance floor at social events. Subject 8889 is also known for its love for burgers, often devouring them in a matter of seconds.

Addendum: Subject 8889 has been given access to a special room within the Site-██, where it can perform its dance routines and other performances for the entertainment of personnel. The room has been equipped with specialized speakers to handle the volume of its roaring, and the walls have been reinforced to withstand the impact of its jumps and maneuvers. Subject 8889 has expressed its contentment with this arrangement and is cooperative with containment procedures.
