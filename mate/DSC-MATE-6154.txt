SCP Foundation File: SCP-6154 "The Spot-Smiling Villain"

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6154 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times, with additional restraints if necessary. Any interactions with SCP-6154 must be supervised by at least two armed guards. Access to SCP-6154's personal effects, including the red scarf and gold earring, is strictly prohibited.

Description: SCP-6154 is a human male with facial features that include spots and a constant smile. SCP-6154's eyes appear to be those of a clown and are a bright blue color. SCP-6154's blonde hair is styled in a typical men's cut, and he wears a gold earring in his left ear. At all times, SCP-6154 wears a red scarf around his neck.

SCP-6154 has a strong and decisive personality that can come across as bossy or intimidating. SCP-6154 is determined to get things done efficiently and effectively, and this drive has led to their current villain status. SCP-6154's villainous activities include theft, extortion, and causing destruction to property.

SCP-6154's anomalous properties are currently unknown. However, it has been noted that SCP-6154's smile is constant, even in the presence of danger or fear. SCP Foundation researchers currently believe that SCP-6154 has the ability to influence individuals around him, but further testing is required to confirm this theory.

Addendum: Interview Log 6154-001

Interviewer: Dr. ██████

Interviewee: SCP-6154

Dr. ██████: Good morning, SCP-6154.

SCP-6154: Morning, doc.

Dr. ██████: Can you tell us about your villainous activities?

SCP-6154: Is that what they're calling it? I just see it as getting what I want, one way or another.

Dr. ██████: And what is it that you want?

SCP-6154: Power. Control. Money. The usual. You know how it is.

Dr. ██████: How do you feel about being contained by the SCP Foundation?

SCP-6154: It's just a temporary setback. I'll be back out there soon enough. And when I am, watch out. I'm going to be stronger than ever before.

Closing Statement: SCP-6154 remains a dangerous threat to society and must be contained indefinitely. Further research into SCP-6154's anomalous properties is ongoing.
