Item #: SCP-5553

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5553 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. It is permitted access to approved fashion design materials and magazines only. Any contact with SCP-5553 must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel prior to engagement. Any attempts by SCP-5553 to engage in playful arguments with personnel must be discouraged.

Description: SCP-5553 is a human female with orange striped facial features and a distinctive smile. It has green hair in a purple cap, with gold earrings and a silver chain as accessories. SCP-5553 is a fashion designer by trade, and is known for its exceptional taste and quick wit in the field.

SCP-5553's personality is that of a fun-loving, charismatic individual. It enjoys engaging in debates and arguments with friends and family members for entertainment, although it has been known to take things too far at times. SCP-5553 appears to be unaware of its own strength and persuasive abilities, which have caused issues in the past.

SCP-5553 possesses no anomalous abilities or traits, beyond its distinctive facial features and charisma. However, its tendency to engage in arguments and debates for entertainment has proven problematic in certain situations. SCP-5553 must be closely monitored at all times, particularly in social situations where it may attempt to engage in debate or argument with personnel.
