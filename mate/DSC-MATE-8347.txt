Item #: SCP-8347

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8347 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with a reinforced door and is to be fed a diet of raw meat twice daily. Any interactions with SCP-8347 are to be recorded for research purposes. Any unauthorized personnel caught attempting to interact with SCP-8347 will be dealt with accordingly.

Description: SCP-8347 is a male tiger approximately 2 meters tall, with blue eyes resembling clown makeup. SCP-8347 is a fashion designer with a no-nonsense attitude, but also shows a playful side manifesting in comedic situations. The most notable physical attributes of SCP-8347 are the jade earring in its left ear and the goggles on its forehead. SCP-8347 seems to value tradition and has a storytelling aspect to its personality, which adds to its playful nature.

Accessories: SCP-8347 carries a whistle, though its intended purpose is currently unknown.

Notes: SCP-8347 was brought to the foundation's attention after numerous reported sightings of a "talking tiger" in fashion shows around the world. Upon capture, SCP-8347 showed early signs of hostility towards foundation personnel. Subsequent psychological evaluations showed that SCP-8347 responds well to humor and storytelling, and now seems to enjoy interacting with foundation personnel.
