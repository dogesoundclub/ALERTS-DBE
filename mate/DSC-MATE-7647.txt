Item #: SCP-7647

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7647 is to be kept under surveillance at all times. Personnel are reminded to approach with caution due to SCP-7647's unpredictable behavior. SCP-7647 is to only be approached by designated personnel, who must wear protective gear at all times. Any unauthorized individuals who come into contact with SCP-7647 are to report to the Site Director immediately.

Description: SCP-7647 is a female human, approximately 26 years old, identified as a veterinarian. SCP-7647 has spots on her face and a permanent smile. SCP-7647's eyes are blue with clown-like makeup around them. SCP-7647 has a two-color band on her forehead and a goggle on her forehead. SCP-7647 wears a gold earring and is often seen carrying a cigarette.

SCP-7647 has a tendency to act before she thinks things through, which can lead to mishaps and accidents. She is known to show off and impress others, leading to dangerous situations. SCP-7647's personality is unpredictable, making her difficult to control.

SCP-7647 displays no anomalous abilities, but her unpredictable behavior and tendency to act recklessly make her a potential threat to both herself and those around her. Any attempts to correct SCP-7647's behavior have been unsuccessful.

Addendum: Incident Report 7647-01

On [REDACTED], SCP-7647 attempted to administer medicine to a large, aggressive animal without proper restraint. The animal attacked SCP-7647, causing severe injuries. SCP-7647 was taken to the medical facility for treatment, and the animal was contained without further incident. SCP-7647 was warned not to attempt similar actions in the future.
