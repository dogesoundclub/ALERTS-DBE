Item #: DSC-MATE-5900

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5900 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Due to DSC-MATE-5900's benign nature, no additional security measures are required. Interaction with DSC-MATE-5900 is permitted for research purposes, as long as it does not interfere with its job as a delivery driver.

Description: DSC-MATE-5900 appears as a human female with blue clown eyes and a contagious smile. Her head is adorned with a tiara, and a two-color band is present on her forehead. DSC-MATE-5900 is often seen wearing a silver chain as an accessory. Despite its anomalous traits, DSC-MATE-5900 otherwise appears to be a normal human.

DSC-MATE-5900 displays a remarkable sense of empathy, being highly attuned to the emotions of others. In many situations, DSC-MATE-5900's abilities can diffuse tense or awkward situations, as it always knows the perfect thing to say. DSC-MATE-5900's humorous disposition allows it to help others find humor in challenging situations, even in the face of danger.

It is currently unknown how DSC-MATE-5900 obtained its anomalous properties. DSC-MATE-5900 is highly cooperative, and willingly shares its knowledge and experiences with research personnel. Due to DSC-MATE-5900's gentle personality, Foundation personnel are encouraged to approach DSC-MATE-5900 in a friendly manner.
