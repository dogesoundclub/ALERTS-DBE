Item #: 9539

Object Class: Safe 

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 9539, also known as the "Train Driver", is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. The chamber is to be furnished with a comfortable bed, a small library, and various toys for play. Subject is to be provided with a steady supply of fresh fruits, vegetables and hay. 

Access to subject will be limited to Level 1 personnel and above, with visits being limited to a maximum of two hours. Any interaction with subject must be monitored and recorded. 

Description: Subject 9539 is a male rabbit, standing at approximately 2 feet tall when fully extended. He wears a bucket hat and nerd glasses, as well as a silver chain around his neck. 

Subject 9539's personality is highly empathetic and compassionate, often feeling responsible for the well-being of those around him. However, this also leads to a tendency for subject to take on the problems of others, often to the point of exhaustion. 

Subject 9539 has a special affinity for trains, having worked as a train driver prior to containment. He is highly knowledgeable on the subject of trains and often speaks of his love for them. 

Addendum 9539-1: Subject 9539 has been observed to display unusually high levels of intelligence and language comprehension. He has also shown an ability to communicate telepathically with other beings, a trait not commonly observed in rabbits. Further research is recommended. 

Addendum 9539-2: Subject 9539's love for trains has prompted the creation of a miniature train set in his containment chamber, which he spends many hours playing with. This has greatly improved his overall mood and well-being.
