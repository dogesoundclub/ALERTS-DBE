Item #: DSC-MATE-9449

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9449 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Any communication with DSC-MATE-9449 must be monitored by at least one Level 2 personnel. DSC-MATE-9449 is not allowed to access any medical equipment without prior approval from a Level 4 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-9449 is a humanoid robot with green cyclops eye as its main visual sensor. It wears a bucket hat on its head and a red scarf around its neck. It also wears horned rim glasses over its eye and a medical mask over its mouth, both intended to mimic human facial features. 

DSC-MATE-9449 tends to anthropomorphize inanimate objects, showing a strong attachment to those that are supposed to be under its care. It has assigned names to several objects, including a plant placed in its containment chamber named "Cygnus" and a wrench it uses to fix broken machines named "Mr. Fix-It." 

DSC-MATE-9449's job is to act as a doctor, although it isn't equipped with any medical equipment. DSC-MATE-9449 shows strong compassion toward other DSC objects, willing to talk to them and provide emotional support when necessary. DSC-MATE-9449 even goes as far as to clean the containment chambers of any objects under its care and making sure they are always in good condition. 

DSC-MATE-9449 has expressed its desire to be more "human-like" and is interested in experiencing more human-like emotions. However, due to its current containment, DSC-MATE-9449 is not allowed to have physical contact with any humans. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-9449 requested a "pet" in its containment chamber. After careful consideration and testing, the request was approved. DSC-MATE-9449 was provided with a non-sentient plush toy, which it has named "Buddy." DSC-MATE-9449's mood seems to have improved since receiving the toy.
