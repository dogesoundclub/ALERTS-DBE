Item #: SCP-9484

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9484 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-19. The chamber must have a reinforced steel door and be equipped with a soundproofing system. SCP-9484 is allowed one hour of outdoor exercise per day and must be accompanied by at least two armed guards at all times.

Description: SCP-9484 is a male Bengal tiger standing at 2.5 meters in height, with blue clown eyes. SCP-9484 wears a hoodie with a two-color band on its forehead and a silver chain around its neck. SCP-9484 is always accompanied by a whistle and has claimed to be a real estate agent.

SCP-9484 possesses an extraordinary level of charisma that allows it to connect with others on a deep level. SCP-9484 has a talent for getting strangers to open up about their life stories, often leading to humorous situations. SCP-9484 seems to enjoy sharing funny anecdotes that bring people closer together.

Experiments have shown that SCP-9484's charisma can affect not only humans but also other animals. SCP-9484 has been observed calming down other aggressive animals, including other SCPs.

Notably, SCP-9484 has not shown any signs of aggression. However, its true motives and abilities are unknown, and it is suspected that SCP-9484 may possess anomalous properties not yet discovered.

Addendum: SCP-9484 has expressed a desire to participate in activities that involve connecting with others, such as counseling or public speaking. Consideration of these requests is pending review by the Ethics Committee.
