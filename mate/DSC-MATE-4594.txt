Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4594 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-4594 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above.

Description: SCP-4594 is a male human identified as a reporter named ████████ █████████. SCP-4594 stands at 1.73 meters tall and weighs approximately 78 kilograms. The face of SCP-4594 displays a unique mouth spot of unknown origin and a perpetual smile. SCP-4594's eyes are concealed behind a pair of nerd glasses, and the head displays a metal purple hue.

SCP-4594 often wears a jade earring on the left ear and a gold chain, notably with a cross, around the neck area. Additionally, SCP-4594 is often seen with a cigarette in hand, which they will occasionally smoke. The subject's demeanor tends to be relaxed, and they have displayed no signs of hostility.

SCP-4594's personality is characterized by a caring and nurturing nature that extends to their friends and family, often treating them as if they were their own children. SCP-4594's conduct may lead to humorous situations, with the subject occasionally doting on everything and anything they come across.

Addendum: SCP-4594's anomalous properties are currently unknown. Testing is ongoing.
