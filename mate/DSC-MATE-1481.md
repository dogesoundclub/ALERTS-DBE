
Item #: 1481

Object Class: Euclid

Species: Rabbit (Oryctolagus cuniculus)

Gender: Male

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1481 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Any requests for materials or tools must be reviewed and approved by Level 3 personnel. DSC-MATE-1481 is to be monitored at all times for any attempts to create anomalous objects or devices.

Description: DSC-MATE-1481 has the appearance of a common domesticated rabbit, however, it is found wearing a doge hat on its head, a two-color band on its forehead, a gold earring on its left ear and carrying a candy stick as an accessory. DSC-MATE-1481 is a skilled mechanic with an aptitude for fixing almost any mechanical object.

DSC-MATE-1481 has a strong and decisive personality, with a bossy and intimidating demeanor. This is believed to be the result of its need to ensure that tasks are completed efficiently and effectively. Its attitude can make it somewhat difficult to approach or work with, but its talent as a mechanic makes it a valuable resource to the Foundation.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1481's personality is known to change upon prolonged exposure to certain anomalous objects. Personnel are to monitor its behavior when working around any potentially dangerous items.
