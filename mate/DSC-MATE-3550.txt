Item #: SCP-3550

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3550 is currently contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Due to SCP-3550's amiable nature, no specialized containment procedures have been deemed necessary at this time.

Description: SCP-3550 is a human male, appearing to be in his late twenties, with short, styled hair and donning a green cross earring in his left earlobe. SCP-3550's eyes are typically obscured by a pair of thick-rimmed nerd glasses and his mouth is often covered by a medical mask. The subject commonly accessorizes with a silver chain necklace and a short peak cap.

SCP-3550 displays a decisive and authoritative personality, often taking charge in group situations and exhibiting a bossy demeanor. Though this can come across as intimidating to some, SCP-3550's main objective appears to be accomplishing tasks in the most efficient and effective manner possible. It should be noted that SCP-3550 remains cooperative with Foundation personnel and does not attempt to escape confinement.

SCP-3550's occupation is that of a programmer, with proficiency in various programming languages and software development tools. The subject has not yet demonstrated any anomalous abilities in relation to his occupation, but further study is recommended.

Addendum: Research into SCP-3550's background reveals no prior criminal activity or other anomalous behavior. Investigation into the source of SCP-3550's out-of-the-ordinary persona is ongoing.
