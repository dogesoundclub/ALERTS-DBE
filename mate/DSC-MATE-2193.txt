Item #: SCP-2193

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2193 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. All interactions with SCP-2193 require the approval of at least one Level 2 personnel.

Description: SCP-2193 is a female human with distinct orange stripes on her face and red lips. The facial features are reminiscent of a 00s schoolgirl. SCP-2193 also has a two-color band on their forehead, gold earrings on their ears, and a medical mask over their mouth.

SCP-2193's personality exhibits deep empathy and understanding towards others, often moved to tears or emotional outbursts when witnessing someone else's pain or suffering. SCP-2193 also displays a fondness for animals and nature.

SCP-2193 has identified herself as a programmer, and her abilities to code and program have been confirmed by Foundation IT personnel. SCP-2193 has been cooperative during all interactions with Foundation staff.

Addendum: SCP-2193 has been observed displaying mild anomalous behavior during stressful situations, including the ability to temporarily immobilize electronic devices within a 5-meter radius. Further investigation is needed to determine if this behavior is a product of SCP-2193's abilities as a programmer or an inherent anomaly.
