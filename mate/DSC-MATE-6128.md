Item #: DSC-MATE-6128

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6128 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Due to its occupation as a fashion designer, it is permitted access to a limited amount of materials and tools for its fashion design work. However, any item brought within its chamber must be cleared by Level 2 personnel for potential anomalous properties.

Description: DSC-MATE-6128 is a male monkey of the genus Macaca with the ability to communicate fluently in English. DSC-MATE-6128 is typically observed wearing a hoodie and horn-rimmed glasses, and often adorns a gold chain. The monkey has a distinctive two-color band on its forehead that extends to its cheeks. 

DSC-MATE-6128's personality is indicative of high emotional sensitivity. It has a fondness for sentimental media and can be easily moved to tears. Additionally, DSC-MATE-6128 is particularly fond of art or music that elicits strong emotional responses, and has been observed incorporating these themes into its fashion designs.

DSC-MATE-6128's anomalous properties manifest in its designs. Any individual who wears DSC-MATE-6128's fashion designs will become the subject of increased emotional sensitivity. Subjects have reported heightened emotional states, which often lead to erratic behavior and emotional outbursts. DSC-MATE-6128 has no control over this effect.

DSC-MATE-6128 currently cooperates with the Foundation in containing its anomalous abilities. Nevertheless, personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-6128 in a professional capacity are advised to maintain a clear and professional demeanor.
