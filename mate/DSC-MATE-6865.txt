Item #: 6865

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6865 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. The subject is permitted access to a beautician's kit for their occupation; however, any other items or materials must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel. Any proposed changes to the subject's appearance must be presented to and authorized by a Level 4 personnel.

Description: SCP-6865 is a humanoid entity, biologically identified as female. The subject's facial features include orange stripes, a visible smile, and blue clown-like eyes. Additionally, they have a two-color band on their forehead, along with goggles on top. SCP-6865 is commonly seen sporting a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-6865 operates as a beautician, utilizing their unique appearance to attract clients. Their skillset primarily includes hairstyling, makeup application, and nail art. SCP-6865 has expressed a special interest in "transforming" their customers, using their humor and charisma to lift their spirits and help them feel more confident.

The subject's personality is noted for their sense of humor and talent for making others laugh. SCP-6865 is known to use funny anecdotes and comical impersonations to ease tense or uncomfortable situations. However, anyone experiencing prolonged exposure to the subject's humor may become subject to emotional manipulation.

Addendum: SCP-6865 has yet to show any signs of anomalous ability or behavior. As a precaution, regular psychological evaluations will be scheduled to ensure the containment of SCP-6865 remains manageable.
