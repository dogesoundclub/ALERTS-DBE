Item #: SCP-9891

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9891 is to be kept in a 5x5x5 meter containment chamber. The chamber must be equipped with a computer that is connected to the internet for SCP-9891's work-related tasks. SCP-9891 is allowed to wear its silver chain and jade earring but must remove the gas mask when not in use.

SCP-9891 is allowed access to its preferred music in order to maintain good morale. However, exposure to sudden loud noises can cause SCP-9891 to become agitated, so caution should be used when introducing new sounds to its environment.

SCP-9891 is allowed supervised socialization with other personnel or D-class subjects in order to prevent isolation and depression.

Description: SCP-9891 is a male monkey of the species Macaca fascicularis, standing approximately 50cm tall and weighing approximately 5kg. SCP-9891 is capable of speaking fluently in several human languages and has a proficiency in programming languages. SCP-9891's black hair on its head is normal and well-groomed.

SCP-9891 wears a jade earring in its left ear and a silver chain around its neck. SCP-9891 also wears an ordinary gas mask when not in its containment chamber. SCP-9891 has been observed using the mask as a prop during its programming work, pretending to be a biohazardous technician.

SCP-9891 has a strong sense of duty and responsibility, often taking on extra work without being asked. SCP-9891's work ethic is well-known and respected among personnel. SCP-9891 also has a playful side and enjoys socialization and entertaining others. SCP-9891's preferred method of entertainment is dancing and singing along with music. SCP-9891 has been known to break out into spontaneous karaoke sessions, much to the delight of its audience.

SCP-9891's intelligence and language skills are still being studied for their origins and potential applications in technology.

Addendum 9891-1: SCP-9891 has requested access to a drum set and has proposed a music room for personnel to use during off-duty hours. Request is pending approval from Site Director.
