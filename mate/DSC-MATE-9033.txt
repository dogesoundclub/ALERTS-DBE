Item #: 9033

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9033 is to be contained in standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. No direct contact or communication is allowed between SCP-9033 and any personnel without Level 2 clearance or higher. SCP-9033 is permitted access to mechanical equipment with direct supervision from designated personnel.

Description: SCP-9033 is a female human mechanic with unique physical features. She possesses orange stripes on her face, which extends from her left cheek to her right temple. Her head is predominantly orange, and her forehead features two-color bands with a goggle accessory located above it.

SCP-9033 has green cross earrings and wears a gold chain around her neck. Her gas mask appearance, while common for her profession, appears to be personalized with various symbols and markings.

SCP-9033’s personality is characterized by a tendency to be introspective, leading to moments of absent-mindedness. She is often lost in thought or daydreaming, which can result in humorous accidents such as walking into objects.

SCP-9033 exhibits no anomalous abilities or behavior, and its unique physical features are not expected to pose a significant threat to containment efforts. However, due to its eccentric and peculiar demeanor, personnel are warned to approach SCP-9033 with caution.

Addendum: SCP-9033 has been cooperative and compliant with containment procedures. Requests for specific mechanical equipment or tools have been approved on a case-by-case basis to ensure safety and security. Any changes to these procedures must be approved by Level 3 personnel or higher.  Overall, SCP-9033 is considered low-risk and low-priority for containment efforts.
