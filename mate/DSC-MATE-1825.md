Item #: DSC-MATE-1825

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1825 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-19. The subject is permitted to maintain their career as a lawyer, however all legal cases and clients must be approved by the Site Director. DSC-MATE-1825 is to wear a normal gas mask at all times when outside their containment chamber. Any requests for additional items or accessories must be approved by at least two Level-3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-1825 is a human male with spots across his face. Of particular note are the subject's blue clown-like eyes and jade earring. DSC-MATE-1825 wears a short peak cap and displays a 2 color band on their forehead. When outside of containment, DSC-MATE-1825 appears to be a normal individual, save for the aforementioned features. 

DSC-MATE-1825 displays a strong sense of duty and responsibility, which has resulted in them overextending themselves on multiple occasions. DSC-MATE-1825 is prone to trying to accomplish too much in a single day and restricting themselves from asking for assistance. This has resulted in numerous humorous situations, which has led to personnel referring to DSC-MATE-1825 as "the clown lawyer". 

DSC-MATE-1825 has displayed no anomalous effects beyond their physical appearance and personality traits. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1825 has requested that they be allowed to wear a more ornate gas mask and carry several case files with them at all times. The request has been denied.
