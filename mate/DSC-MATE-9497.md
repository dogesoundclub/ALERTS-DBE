Item #: DSC-MATE-9497

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9497 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell. All communication with DSC-MATE-9497 must be monitored for any potential anomalies.

Description: DSC-MATE-9497 is a human male with multiple spots on his face and a frightened mouth. The subject's head is adorned with a mohawk and a jade earring on each ear. DSC-MATE-9497 frequently wears a gold chain and a normal gas mask as accessories.

DSC-MATE-9497 is an electrician by profession, and exhibits no anomalous abilities or traits. However, the subject possesses a highly observant and attentive personality, often spending a considerable amount of time and effort in selecting the "perfect" gift for someone. DSC-MATE-9497's preoccupation with giving the perfect gift has been observed to cause significant distress and anxiety.

Despite being a mundane human, DSC-MATE-9497's meticulousness in gift-giving raises concerns about potential mind-altering properties that may not have been detected. Further research on DSC-MATE-9497's behavior is ongoing.
