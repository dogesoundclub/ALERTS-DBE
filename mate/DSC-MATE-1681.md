Item #: DSC-MATE-1681

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1681 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. It is not allowed to smoke its cigarette inside the chamber. Two members of staff are to be present at all times during interaction with DSC-MATE-1681. The subject is to receive weekly psychological evaluations to monitor its empathic abilities.

Description: DSC-MATE-1681 is a humanoid rabbit approximately 1.5 meters tall, wearing a gray hoodie over its head. It appears to be a furry creature with slightly pointed ears and whiskers. The subject is male and is often seen smoking a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-1681 has an extremely strong sense of empathy, often being able to feel the emotions of others around it. This ability can lead to humorous situations, as DSC-MATE-1681 has an almost human sense of humor and often knows just the right thing to say to cheer someone up. It is also able to help others see the humor in difficult situations.

DSC-MATE-1681 works as a beautician and is known to be skilled at the application of makeup and hair styling. It has been observed to use its empathic abilities to make customers feel more comfortable and relaxed during appointments.

DSC-MATE-1681 is generally cooperative with staff and has not shown any signs of aggression. However, it has been known to become overwhelmed by the emotions of others and requires regular psychological evaluations to ensure its mental stability.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1681 was brought to the attention of the Foundation after several reports were made about a talking rabbit that could manipulate emotions. Foundation agents were able to contain DSC-MATE-1681 without incident, and it has since been used in various projects involving the study of empathic abilities.
