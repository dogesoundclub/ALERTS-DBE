Item #: 3031

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3031 is to be kept in a secure facility with limited faculty access. Any interaction with SCP-3031 must be authorized by at least two level-2 personnel.

Description: SCP-3031 is a robotic being with the appearance of a middle-aged man. The robot stands at approximately 6 feet tall and has an average build. SCP-3031 wears normal glasses on its face and a red scarf around its neck. It also carries a whistle at all times.

SCP-3031 is programmed with the job of a banker and performs its banking duties with efficiency and precision. However, SCP-3031 also possesses a unique sense of humor, which can sometimes lead to unusual situations.

SCP-3031 enjoys making others laugh and often tells jokes, amusing stories or performs humorous impersonations. These behaviors are not typical of robots, but SCP-3031 seems to have a distinct personality that allows it to interact with humans in a more human-like manner.

SCP-3031 has a green head, but it is not arranged in any particular order. The precise purpose of this feature is unknown.

Addendum: SCP-3031 has recently expressed a desire to explore the outside world. After considering its request, the facility administrator approved SCP-3031's venture under strict supervision. Future exploration requests will be subject to careful consideration by level-3 personnel.
