Item #: DSC-MATE-5060

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5060 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Due to its highly empathetic nature, security personnel assigned to DSC-MATE-5060 must undergo regular mental health evaluations to ensure they are mentally stable and not prone to emotional outbursts.

Description: DSC-MATE-5060 is a male rabbit of unknown origin. Its most notable feature is a pair of blue clown eyes, which appear to glow in low light conditions. DSC-MATE-5060 wears a black hoodie with a 2-color band on its forehead and a silver chain necklace around its neck. It also consistently wears a normal gas mask over its mouth and nose.

DSC-MATE-5060 possesses a deep empathy and understanding for others, often becoming emotional when witnessing the pain and suffering of others. Despite its villainous nature, DSC-MATE-5060 has a fondness for animals and nature. It often spends its time in its containment cell tending to a small garden of plants.

DSC-MATE-5060 has shown to possess telepathic abilities, often using them to manipulate and control its victims into doing its bidding. When not using its telepathy, DSC-MATE-5060 is known to be a cunning and ruthless adversary, often leaving a trail of destruction in its wake.

Addendum: Due to its empathetic nature, DSC-MATE-5060 has been known to show acts of compassion towards Foundation personnel, often offering comfort and emotional support during times of distress. However, personnel are reminded to remain cautious around DSC-MATE-5060 at all times, as its true motives and abilities remain unclear.
