Item #: DSC-MATE-4470

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4470 is to be kept under strict surveillance at all times. DSC-MATE-4470 is to be contained in a standard D-Class cell, modified to accommodate a mini-fridge and microwave for food and drink. Personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-4470 must wear a medical mask for their own safety, and must follow standard containment procedures for humanoid DSCs. DSC-MATE-4470 is not to be allowed to leave Foundation property.

Description: DSC-MATE-4470 is a human female police officer with unusual facial features including spots and purple lips. Its most notable feature is its orange eyebrow, which is believed to have anomalous properties. DSC-MATE-4470 has a metal blue head and a pair of green cross earrings. DSC-MATE-4470 is typically seen wearing a gold chain accessory.

DSC-MATE-4470 is very social and outgoing, and enjoys being the center of attention. It has a tendency to tell jokes and stories that are entertaining and has caused security breaches in the past weeks. DSC-MATE-4470's personality is contagious, and it can cause others to behave in atypical ways.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-4470 came to Foundation attention after a containment breach incident. DSC-MATE-4470 displayed unusual immune system characteristics that allowed it to survive being exposed to an anomalous virus. DSC-MATE-4470's immune system was studied, and it was discovered that DSC-MATE-4470's anomalous eyebrow produces a hormone that can fight anomalous viruses.

DSC-MATE-4470 is currently being used for research purposes, as the compound produced by its eyebrow may be a potential cure for various DSCs. Reasons for its pension include trespassing in unauthorized areas, trying to contact anomalies, and making other personnel laugh.
