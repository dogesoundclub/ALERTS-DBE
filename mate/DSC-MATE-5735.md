Item #: DSC-MATE-5735

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5735 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber with additional ventilation systems to support its species. Access to the chamber is to be limited to Level 2 personnel and above. DSC-MATE-5735 is not to be allowed access to music or any equipment that may lead to its entertainment activities. Any instances of DSC-MATE-5735 attempting to organize social gatherings or events outside of its designated duties are to be reported to Level 3 personnel immediately.

Description: DSC-MATE-5735 is a humanoid rabbit of the species Oryctolagus cuniculus, standing at approximately 1.5 meters tall. Its most prominent feature is a two-color band on its forehead, typically white and black. DSC-MATE-5735 is male and often seen wearing a red scarf and a gold earring on its left ear. It also frequently dons a normal gas mask, despite not requiring one for breathing.

DSC-MATE-5735 is a theater manager and displays a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards its position. However, this has not stopped it from displaying a playful personality, often leading to humorous situations. DSC-MATE-5735 seems to enjoy entertaining others and frequently hosts social gatherings with its subordinates. It has been observed to be particularly fond of music and often gets the entire crowd to dance or sing along to its favorite songs.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5735 has been observed attempting to organize unauthorized parties and events outside of its designated theater. Any instances of this behavior are to be reported to Level 3 personnel immediately for disciplinary action. Under no circumstances are personnel to comply with DSC-MATE-5735's requests to organize events outside of the theater, as this has the potential to compromise Foundation secrecy.
