Item #: 1989

Object Class: Anomalous

Special Containment Procedures: 1989 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell within Site-19. Any requests for cosmetic procedures are to be strictly monitored and approved by at least two level-3 personnel.

Description: 1989 is a male Monkey of unidentified origin, measuring approximately 1.5 meters in height. Its most distinguishing features include a bucket hat, gold chain around its neck, and a cigarette constantly held between its teeth. Although its eye color is unknown, 1989 has been observed to have a piercing gaze.

Personality: 1989 exhibits a behavior that can be described as impulsive and rash. It often acts on its emotions instead of taking the time to think things through, which can lead to unpredictable outcomes. Its spontaneity can, however, be quite humorous, often resulting in unexpected but amusing situations.

In particular, 1989 has honed its skills as a beautician, specializing in haircuts and other aesthetic procedures. Despite its impulsive nature, it delivers exceptional results, pleasing both clients and observers.

Overall, 1989 is a valuable asset to the Foundation. Its abilities and skills could be harnessed for the benefit of the organization, as long as they are carefully monitored and contained.
