Item #: SCP-8941

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8941 is to be contained in a humanoid containment chamber with reinforced walls and door locks. The chamber must be fitted with a high-quality ventilation system to avoid the risk of airborne transmission. SCP-8941 is allowed to engage in recreational activities as long as they remain within the containment facility. Furthermore, it is forbidden to expose SCP-8941 to any new risky behaviors, as it has been observed that SCP-8941 always seeks to try new experiences.

Description: SCP-8941 is a male monkey that stands upright and reaches a height of approximately 1.3 meters. SCP-8941's face features include a permanent frightened mouth expression and its eyes are always covered with horned rim glasses. SCP-8941 wears a retro cap on its head, with two color bands on its forehead and a goggle on its forehead. It always carries a gold earring on one of its ears. SCP-8941 displays a strong love of adventure and enjoys taking risks or trying new things, often resulting in humorous stories of near-death experiences or unexpected situations.

SCP-8941 retains high intelligence and has demonstrated an excellent memory for theatrical productions. SCP-8941's current position is theater manager, where it is responsible for the logistics and staging of theater performances.

SCP-8941 has shown no signs of hostility towards humans and is friendly to anyone who approaches it. However, SCP-8941 can sometimes be difficult to control when exposed to new environments or situations. SCP-8941 requires constant monitoring to prevent it from engaging in risky behavior or exploring new places, which may result in damage to containment or escape attempts. As a result, it is essential to monitor SCP-8941's whereabouts using containment tracking devices.

Addendum: SCP-8941 has demonstrated an ability to learn new skills and communicate with humans using sign language. SCP-8941's intelligence and curiosity make it a fascinating creature to observe and study. However, due to its unpredictable nature, all interactions with SCP-8941 should be conducted with extreme caution.
