Item #: DSC-MATE-1342

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1342 (aka "Dr. Adventure") is to be contained in an urban setting with access to medical facilities. Dr. Adventure is to be monitored at all times while on site for both his safety and the safety of others. Any requests from Dr. Adventure for dangerous or high-risk activities must be reviewed and approved by the Site Director.

Description: DSC-MATE-1342 is a 35-year-old male human known to many as "Dr. Adventure." Dr. Adventure stands at 6 foot, has an average build and has orange stripes on his face. He is often seen with a smile and wears normal glasses which have been seen to have no anomalous effects.

Dr. Adventure's head is an unusual metallic purple color, and he wears a green cross earring on one of his ears. He is often seen wearing a medical mask due to his profession, and he is adorned with a red scarf around his neck as his designated accessory.

Dr. Adventure is known to have a love for adventure and taking risks. His eagerness to try new things often leads to humorous stories of near-death experiences, such as surviving bungee jumping or accidentally getting lost in the wilderness. However, despite his love for adventure, Dr. Adventure is a qualified medical professional and prioritizes the safety of himself and others.

Addendum: Dr. Adventure has been deemed a valuable asset in retrieving anomalous objects due to his ability to think on his feet and his proficiency in medical procedures. Consideration for his assistance should be made in future expeditions.
