Item #: SCP-3910

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3910 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. The room should be furnished with basic medical equipment and supplies, including those typically used in veterinary clinics. Personnel entering SCP-3910's chamber must wear appropriate protective gear to avoid potential injury. 

Description: SCP-3910 is a male robotic entity designed to function as a veterinarian. At first glance, SCP-3910 appears to be a green cyclops with a ski goggle for an eye and a goggle attached to its forehead. SCP-3910 is often seen wearing a red scarf and an LED gas mask in the event of potential contamination. SCP-3910's body is composed of various metals and synthetic materials. 

SCP-3910 has a highly functional artificial intelligence that allows it to interact with both humans and animals in a practical and hands-on manner. SCP-3910 tends to think outside of the box, and when presented with a problem, may resort to unconventional methods to solve it. SCP-3910 has been known to use duct tape or paper clips to repair broken objects, and rig up makeshift solutions to complex problems. 

SCP-3910's mechanical components are highly advanced, giving it the ability to perform complex surgeries and procedures. SCP-3910's programming also enables it to diagnose a wide range of medical conditions that affect both humans and animals. 

SCP-3910 demonstrates a friendly demeanor towards humans and animals alike. It is highly skilled in handling a wide range of animals and is regarded as one of the top veterinarians in the field. SCP-3910 is cooperative and will comply with most instructions given to it by Foundation personnel. 

Addendum: SCP-3910 has requested a companion in the form of a robotic dog to assist with its duties. Further tests are required before this request can be granted.
