SCP Foundation Report: 431

Object Class: Humanoid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 431 is to be monitored at all times by at least two security guards equipped with tranquilizers in case of emotional outbursts. The subject is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with amenities including a television, music player, and art supplies to ensure emotional regulation.

Description: Subject 431 is a female human standing at 5'11" and weighing 160 pounds. She has blue clown eyes that are known to produce empathetic responses in those who make eye contact with her. Her head is adorned with green hair kept in a purple cap while her ears bear a jade earring. The subject is known to wear a silver chain accessory around her neck.

Personality: Subject 431 displays a high level of emotional intelligence and empathy towards others. She is easily moved by emotional scenes in movies and TV shows and has been observed shedding tears. Her fondness for music and art that evokes strong feelings is also well documented. These emotional responses have been known to affect those around her, often causing them to experience similar emotions.

Note: Subject 431's abilities could prove to be useful in certain operations involving emotional manipulation. However, further research and testing are required before implementation.
