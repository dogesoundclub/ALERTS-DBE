Item #: SCP-4574

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4574 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-32. SCP-4574 is allowed to keep its bucket hat and red victory band, but all other accessories are to be removed and kept in storage. SCP-4574 is to be monitored for any abnormalities to its behavior or physical appearance. Any changes are to be reported immediately to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-4574 is a human male traffic officer with a unique appearance. SCP-4574 has a distinct mouth spot and wears nerd glasses. SCP-4574 also wears a red scarf and smokes cigarettes. SCP-4574's most notable features are its bucket hat and jade earring. SCP-4574 can be identified by its forehead's red victory band.

SCP-4574 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. SCP-4574 will tell funny stories and do silly impersonations to lighten the mood. SCP-4574's humor has been reported to have an anomalous effect on individuals, causing them to feel happy and relaxed.

SCP-4574 was brought to the Foundation's attention when several individuals reported feeling an uncontrollable urge to laugh and follow SCP-4574's directions, even against their better judgment. Foundation personnel quickly apprehended SCP-4574 and contained it in a humanoid containment chamber at Site-32.

Addendum: SCP-4574 has caused no harm to Foundation personnel or the general public. SCP-4574 has complied with all containment procedures and has shown no signs of attempting to breach containment. SCP-4574's unique appearance and personality make it a valuable asset to the Foundation's research into anomalous mental effects.
