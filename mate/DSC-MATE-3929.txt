SCP Foundation Containment Data Sheet

Identification Number: SCP-3929

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3929 is to be contained within a standard indoor animal habitat equipped with exercise, climbing, and enrichment opportunities. SCP-3929 is to be provided with a variety of fruits, vegetables, and nuts as well as sugary treats for positive reinforcement during research and testing.

All personnel interacting with SCP-3929 must possess Level 2 clearance and have completed advanced animal behavior training. SCP-3929 is to be monitored via CCTV at all times and any significant changes in behavior or health are to be reported immediately to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-3929 is a male, anthropomorphic monkey that exhibits advanced intelligence and verbal communication abilities. SCP-3929 often wears nerdy glasses, a two-color band on their forehead, a jade earring on their left ear, a gold chain, and a candy stick.

SCP-3929 possesses a dry sense of humor and frequently delivers deadpan jokes that can be surprisingly funny. SCP-3929 is a trained veterinarian with extensive knowledge of animal behavior and is capable of providing medical treatment to Foundation animals.

SCP-3929 has demonstrated a cooperative and friendly disposition towards Foundation personnel and is often considered a valuable asset for animal care and research. SCP-3929's intelligence and communication abilities, however, present a potential security risk and all communication with SCP-3929 must be monitored to prevent the release of classified information.

Addendum 3929-01: SCP-3929 has expressed interest in conducting animal behavior research with other SCP creatures, specifically those containing animalistic characteristics. Further research is pending approval from Site Director.
