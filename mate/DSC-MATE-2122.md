Item #: DSC-MATE-2122

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2122 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Due to their occupation as theater manager, DSC-MATE-2122 is permitted to engage in theatrical productions approved by Foundation personnel. All scripts must be reviewed and cleared of any potential anomalous properties.

Description: DSC-MATE-2122 appears to be a middle-aged human male with a smile as his primary facial feature. Their eyes are often obscured by nerd glasses, and their head is equipped with a headset with LED lights. A red victory band adorns their forehead, along with a goggle. Two accessories are consistently worn; a silver chain and an LED gas mask.

DSC-MATE-2122 exhibits a dry sense of humor that can catch people off guard. Their deadpan delivery provides a comedic element that has been noted to surprise individuals. DSC-MATE-2122 does not consider themselves the life of the party, but their personality nonetheless draws people towards them.

DSC-MATE-2122 claims to have no anomalous abilities or properties, but the Foundation has noted unique properties manifesting within the theatrical productions that DSC-MATE-2122 oversees. These properties have included temporary alterations to audience members and changes in the surrounding environment.

Addendum 2122-01: DSC-MATE-2122 has requested to oversee an experimental theatrical production involving D-class personnel. The Foundation is currently considering the proposal, pending further review.
