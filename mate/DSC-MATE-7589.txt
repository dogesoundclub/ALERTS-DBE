Item #: SCP-7589

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7589 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell. Testing requests must be submitted by appropriately ranked personnel and approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-7589 is permitted to work as a delivery driver for [REDACTED], provided that personnel are present to monitor and protect against any potential incidents. SCP-7589 is not permitted to partake in any adventurous activities within the vicinity of the containment area.

Description: SCP-7589 is a human male of average height and build, with numerous spots dotting his body. He typically wears normal glasses over his eyes and sports a green cross earring on one ear. His most notable feature is the mohawk on his head, which is dyed in various colors. SCP-7589 often wears a red scarf around his neck and carries a cigarette, although he is not permitted to smoke within the containment area.

SCP-7589 possesses a personality that is highly adventurous and enjoys taking risks. He often has humorous stories to recount, including surviving near-death experiences or getting lost in the wilderness. SCP-7589 possesses a high level of physical endurance and is notable for his ability to remain calm in dangerous situations.

Addendum: During Incident-7589-A, SCP-7589 was able to outrun and evade several members of Site-[REDACTED] security personnel in an attempted escape. Following a containment breach, SCP-7589 managed to evade capture for several days before turning himself in. As a result, SCP-7589's containment procedures have been increased and monitoring has been intensified.
