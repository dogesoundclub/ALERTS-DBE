Item #: DSC-MATE-2799

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2799 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber with basic amenities. All interactions with DSC-MATE-2799 must be monitored and approved by at least one Level 2 personnel. DSC-MATE-2799 is not allowed to leave its containment chamber without prior permission from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-2799 is a humanoid entity of the female sex designated as a human species, identified as a beautician by profession. DSC-MATE-2799 displays abnormal features such as purple lips, blue clown eyes, and a pink rabbit head, suggesting that it may have undergone significant genetic mutations. Additionally, DSC-MATE-2799 is adorned with a red cross earring, a gold chain, and almost always carries a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-2799 possesses a unique personality characterized by a strong sense of idealism, and a desire to make the world a better place. DSC-MATE-2799 often hosts various protests and boycotts against entities that it believes go against its values. This has resulted in some amusing situations.

DSC-MATE-2799's anomalous properties manifest in its ability to provide beautification services to human beings. These services range from hairstyling, makeup, nail polishing, and other grooming needs. DSC-MATE-2799's services have been reported to deliver anomalous effects, resulting in clients experiencing significant changes to their personalities and physical appearances lasting for extended periods. Subjects who have received these effects have displayed increased levels of attractiveness, confidence, and charisma.

DSC-MATE-2799's existence was discovered during a police arrest involving a robbery at a local beauty salon in New York City. DSC-MATE-2799 was found among the accused and detained following reports from its fellow accused of its ability to provide cosmetic enhancements with eerie effects.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-2799 has requested to provide beautification services to Site personnel as a request made to the Ethics Committee. Given its Safe classification and cooperative behavior, the request has been granted under tight regulation.
