Item #: SCP-9401

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9401 is to be contained at all times within a reinforced cell in a standard humanoid containment unit. No communication with SCP-9401 is permitted without the prior approval of at least one Level 3 researcher.

Description: SCP-9401 appears to be a female humanoid with spots on their face, a distinctive smile, and bright orange eyebrows. They are often seen wearing a silver chain and smoking a cigarette. SCP-9401 is identified as a high-ranking member of a mafia family, and as such, they possess a no-nonsense attitude and value tradition above all else.

Despite their serious demeanor, SCP-9401 also exhibits a playful side, often telling jokes and stories that have been passed down through their family or community. This unpredictable behavior has been known to create humorous situations and erratic behavior.

SCP-9401's head is often adorned with a blonde ushanka and goggles on their forehead. Their appearance gives them an undeniable aura of authority and intimidation.

Further research is ongoing to determine the full extent of SCP-9401's abilities and potential threats to foundation security.

Addendum: SCP-9401 has repeatedly shown a willingness to cooperate with foundation researchers in exchange for leniency in their criminal activities. It is recommended that SCP-9401's requests be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and with appropriate caution.
