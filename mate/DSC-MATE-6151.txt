Item #: 6151

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6151 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to SCP-6151 is only permitted with Level 2 clearance or above. SCP-6151 is to undergo monthly physical and psychological evaluation, and no outside items are allowed to be brought into its containment chamber without approval from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-6151 is a male human of African descent aged 42 years. SCP-6151 wears a bucket hat and has a goggle over his forehead, commonly seen wearing a gold chain necklace and an LED gas mask. The most notable feature of SCP-6151 is a spot on its mouth that appears as a dark patch.

SCP-6151 has shown to be very organized and enjoys planning ahead. However, SCP-6151 also has a spontaneous side, which can lead to unpredictable situations. SCP-6151 has been known to surprise its friends with impromptu adventures or last-minute party plans that have successful outcomes.

SCP-6151's occupation is that of a train driver. Despite SCP-6151's organized persona, it has displayed a lack of professionalism at work. SCP-6151 has been reprimanded several times for performing stunts and presenting itself in an unprofessional manner.

SCP-6151's motives or intentions are currently unknown, and further testing is required to better understand its capabilities and potential dangers if left unmonitored. SCP-6151 is to be monitored closely, and any changes to its behavior should be reported immediately to the overseeing Level 3 personnel.

Addendum: SCP-6151 has requested entertainment in the form of books and board games. Requests must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel before being granted. SCP-6151 is to be closely observed during the persuasion process.
