Item #: DSC-MATE-9802

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9802 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Access to DSC-MATE-9802 is limited to Level 2 personnel or higher and must be approved by the Site Director. Any communication with DSC-MATE-9802 must be recorded and analyzed by Foundation linguists for potential anomalous effects.

Description: DSC-MATE-9802 is a male human of average height and weight, with spots covering the majority of his face. DSC-MATE-9802's mouth is almost always contorted into a frightened expression, even when appearing to be happy or excited. DSC-MATE-9802's eyes resemble ski goggles, and he frequently wears a green cross earring in his left ear. Additionally, he is known for consistently wearing a silver chain and carrying a candy stick with him at all times.

DSC-MATE-9802's personality is characterized by high levels of energy and enthusiasm. He appears to enjoy getting others excited about mundane or trivial subjects, occasionally to the point of annoyance. DSC-MATE-9802 often becomes overly enthusiastic about subjects that others find uninteresting or unimportant, resulting in awkward or humorous situations.

DSC-MATE-9802's anomalous effects are not fully understood. Subjects who spend extended periods of time with DSC-MATE-9802 report feeling a heightened sense of happiness and excitement, regardless of the subject matter being discussed. DSC-MATE-9802's candy stick also appears to have a cognitohazardous effect on individuals who consume it, resulting in heightened emotional responses and increased suggestibility.

Addendum: Following Incident-9802-B, DSC-MATE-9802 is prohibited from interacting with D-class personnel or any individual under the influence of Class-B or higher amnestics. All communication with DSC-MATE-9802 must be conducted through a secure communication channel and any physical contact with DSC-MATE-9802 must be approved by the Site Director.
