Item #: SCP-9348

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9348 is to be monitored at all times while on duty as a train driver. Any deviation from their routine or behavior must be reported to the Site Director immediately. SCP-9348 is to be provided with accommodations suitable for human occupancy during any extended layovers.

Description: SCP-9348 is a human male, currently employed as a train driver. SCP-9348's most notable feature is his constant smile. They accessorize with a purple cap with a headset and a gold chain. SCP-9348 wears normal glasses, and no anomalous properties have been identified in their eyes.

SCP-9348's personality is characterized by reliability and organization, but also by stubbornness. SCP-9348 tends to adhere to their personal routines without regard to external environmental factors. SCP-9348's rigid mindset has led to unanticipated humorous situations in the past.

Addendum: Incident Report 9348-A

On [REDACTED], SCP-9348 encountered a minor delay in their routine. As a result, SCP-9348 refused to follow the new schedule and departed from the station 20 minutes late. SCP-9348 ignored signals from the control center, forcing SCP Foundation personnel to intervene.

Following consultation with Site Director [REDACTED], SCP-9348 has been advised to remain flexible to avoid similar incidents in the future. SCP-9348 has agreed to adjust their personal routine accordingly. Monitoring of SCP-9348 will continue as normal.
