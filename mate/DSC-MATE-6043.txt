Item #: 6043

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Item #6043 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell in Site-██. The subject should be provided with a suitable workstation and computer to pursue their programming skills. Item #6043 can be given occasional access to objects in its containment cell, such as candy, to encourage positive behavior. Any malfunctioning equipment shall be removed from the cell immediately in case of any accidents.


Description: 

Item #6043 is a male monkey, standing at approximately 1.2 meters tall. The subject has a frightened mouth and wears normal glasses on its eyes. The subject often wears a Doge hat on its head and a jade earring on its ear. In addition, Item #6043 often wears a red scarf and carries a candy stick as one of its several accessories. 

Personality:

 Item #6043 has a reckless streak, often acting before thinking. This can often lead to comedic situations such as minor mishaps and accidents, which can make the subject popular among staff members. It is believed that this behavior stems from the subject's desires to impress others. Despite this, Item #6043 excels at its programming job and has a high success rate in completing assigned tasks. 

Addendum:

Item #6043 discovered a coding error that aided the containment of SCP-███, resulting in the upgrade of its security clearance to Level 2. As a reward, Item #6043 was allowed access to a variety of candies and an extra accessory – a plastic bow tie.
