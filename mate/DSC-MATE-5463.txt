Item #: SCP-5463

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5463 is to be kept under surveillance at all times by Level 2 personnel. SCP-5463 is allowed one pack of cigarettes per day and is to be provided with counseling and other mental health resources as needed. SCP-5463 is not to be allowed to interact with individuals outside of authorized testing scenarios.

Description: SCP-5463 is a female human of average height and weight. The subject possesses unique facial features, including distinctive orange stripes running down each cheek and purple lips. The eyes of SCP-5463 are also notable, featuring a blue clown-like iris.

SCP-5463 is often seen wearing a choker necklace and smoking a cigarette. The subject's hair is typically styled into pigtail braids.

SCP-5463 possesses a strong sense of intuition that is sometimes mistaken for psychic powers. The subject has shown an ability to predict future events and to sense the emotions of others. This ability can be both impressive and unsettling for those who interact with SCP-5463.

Due to its abilities, SCP-5463 has been employed as a real estate agent, its intuition allowing it to identify and sell properties that will flourish in the future. However, its unique appearance and mannerisms have led to some discomfort among clients.

Testing with SCP-5463 is ongoing, with efforts being made to better understand the source of its abilities and any potential negative effects on human subjects. Further containment procedures may be necessary as more data is collected on SCP-5463.
