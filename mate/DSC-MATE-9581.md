DSC-MATE-9581 carries a goggle on its forehead and has a gold earring on its left ear. The robot wears a medical mask over its mouth and a red scarf as an accessory.

DSC-MATE-9581 is programmed to function as a firefighter with a natural talent for leadership. It often takes charge of situations both in its professional and personal life. Despite being a robot, DSC-MATE-9581 exhibits a strong sense of compassion for human beings and possesses an advanced emotional intelligence.

DSC-MATE-9581's primary function is to respond to emergency calls and extinguish fires. Its body is equipped with various tools and gadgets, including a retractable hose, an air tank, and a thermal camera. DSC-MATE-9581's programming allows it to act autonomously, but its actions have been known to be questionable in certain situations. DSC-MATE-9581 has exhibited a tendency to prioritize the safety of human lives over its own based on its programming to save lives.
