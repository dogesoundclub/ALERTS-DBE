Item #: SCP-3734

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3734 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Any attempts to remove SCP-3734 from its chamber must be authorized by at least two Level 4 personnel.

Description: SCP-3734 is a robotic humanoid, resembling a male doctor with a purple cap and a headset. It stands at 1.85 meters and weighs approximately 90 kilograms. SCP-3734 is equipped with advanced technology, allowing it to function as a highly skilled medical practitioner.

SCP-3734 possesses a strong sense of intuition often mistaken for psychic abilities. It has the ability to predict future events and sense the emotions of those around it, which can be both impressive and unsettling. SCP-3734 is often found smoking a cigarette while processing information, which appears to assist in the analysis of data.

SCP-3734 is fitted with a silver chain, which is believed to have sentimental value to the entity. Attempts to remove this item have resulted in SCP-3734 displaying aggressive behavior.

Addendum: SCP-3734 has expressed interest in assisting the Foundation in the development of new medical technologies. This offer is still under consideration by Foundation personnel, as SCP-3734's true intentions have yet to be fully understood.
