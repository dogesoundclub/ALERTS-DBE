Item #: SCP-8870

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8870 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-8870 is to be approved and actively monitored by at least one Level 2 personnel. An on-site veterinarian is to be provided for any emergencies involving SCP-8870.

Description: SCP-8870 is a male human appearing to be in his late thirties to early forties, standing at 5'11" (180 cm) and weighing approximately 170 lbs (77 kg). SCP-8870 has an affable disposition and enjoys making connections with others. SCP-8870 possesses a facial feature of small spots and a smile, with LED rainbow-colored eyes and pink parted hair. SCP-8870 is frequently seen wearing a red scarf and a standard gas mask.

SCP-8870 is a skilled and licensed veterinarian and has shown an aptitude for treating and caring for animals. SCP-8870 demonstrates a deep connection to animals, specifically being able to connect with them on an emotional level. SCP-8870's ability to understand and empathize with animals allows for a higher success rate in treatment of diseases and injuries compared to other veterinarians.

SCP-8870 has also demonstrated a unique ability to connect with humans on a deep, emotional level. This skill has been known to lead to cathartic experiences for individuals who interact with SCP-8870, leading to a successful healing of mental traumas or opening up about personal issues. SCP-8870 has reported having a deep intuition for understanding human emotions.

SCP-8870 is cooperative with Foundation personnel and willingly complies with containment procedures. SCP-8870 has expressed a keen interest in performing veterinary duties for any animals that may require medical assistance within the Foundation's facilities.

Addendum: SCP-8870 has displayed an understanding of anomalous properties and is willing to assist Foundation personnel in treating anomalous animals. SCP-8870 has been acknowledged as a valuable asset to the Foundation in the containment and care of anomalous animals. SCP-8870's willingness to identify anomalous animal properties and report anomalies to Foundation personnel has been approved by the Ethics Committee.
