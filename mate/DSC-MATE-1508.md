Item #: DSC-MATE-1508

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1508 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Access to DSC-MATE-1508 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above. A medical mask is to be worn at all times when interacting with DSC-MATE-1508, and its eyes are to be covered with special tinted goggles. Any laughter or amusement by DSC-MATE-1508 is to be reported immediately.

Description: DSC-MATE-1508 is a female human, identified as a veterinarian. It is visually distinct due to the presence of multiple spots on its face, as well as a constant smile. Its eyes are bright red, and resemble that of a clown. DSC-MATE-1508's head is covered by a black hoodie, with a two-color band across its forehead.

Due to the nature of its personality, DSC-MATE-1508 is often seen telling jokes or amusing stories to Foundation personnel. While its humor is harmless, it is advised to avoid laughing or engaging in conversation with DSC-MATE-1508, as it has been known to cause physical and psychological effects on those around it. DSC-MATE-1508 has demonstrated the ability to induce uncontrollable laughter in those around it, as well as temporarily reducing the pain of nearby individuals.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1508 was recovered from a local zoo, where it had been working as a veterinarian. Reports indicate that its abilities had caused disturbances among zoo visitors, leading to the discovery of its anomalous properties. Further testing is ongoing to determine the exact nature of DSC-MATE-1508's abilities.
