Item #: SCP-3155

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3155 is to be contained at Site-██ and is only allowed to leave with permission from at least two level-3 personnel. SCP-3155 must always be accompanied by at least one armed security officer while outside of its containment area.

Description: SCP-3155 is a male tiger, standing at approximately 2.5 meters tall and weighing around 250 kg. SCP-3155’s head is adorned with a black police cap, with two colored bands on its forehead. SCP-3155 wears a pair of horn-rimmed glasses over its eyes and a red cross earring on each ear. SCP-3155's mouth appears to be in a frightened expression.

SCP-3155 has shown to have a playful side and values tradition. However, it also displays a no-nonsense attitude and is known to tell jokes and stories that have been passed down through its family or community. SCP-3155 is a skilled doctor and has been observed using medical equipment and supplies to treat injuries of both humans and animals.

SCP-3155 is often seen with a red scarf and is known to carry a whistle at all times. The use of the whistle is currently unknown.

Addendum: SCP-3155 was discovered living in the forests of [REDACTED], where it had reportedly been treating the injuries of animals and humans for several years. Foundation personnel were alerted to its existence by local authorities after reports of an unusual, talking tiger reached their attention.

SCP-3155 has been cooperative with the Foundation since its containment, but its true motivations and capabilities are still unknown. Ongoing research on SCP-3155 is currently underway.
