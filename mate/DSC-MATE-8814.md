Item #: DSC-MATE-8814

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8814 is to be contained in a standard humanoid cell at Site-██. In order to prevent escape attempts, all electronic communications in the vicinity of DSC-MATE-8814 are to be strictly monitored. Additionally, a team of two guards is to be stationed outside of the cell for the entirety of DSC-MATE-8814's containment period.

Description: DSC-MATE-8814 is a female human, identified as a banker prior to containment. DSC-MATE-8814's face features orange stripes and a red lip, as well as blue clown eyes. DSC-MATE-8814's head is consistent with that of a witch's short haircut, and they are observed wearing a single green cross earring and a gold chain. The entity also wears an LED-filled gas mask, which is always aglow.

DSC-MATE-8814 exhibits a highly observant and attentive personality, with a propensity for gift-giving. They are highly concerned with finding the perfect gift to give and can spend a significant amount of time attempting to do so. DSC-MATE-8814's highly adaptable nature makes them adept at mimicking and observing human behavior as well.

Addendum: Incident Log 8814-1 -
On 08/08/20██, DSC-MATE-8814 managed to escape its containment cell, using its highly mimicking and adaptive behavior to deceive the guards. Foundation staff were able to track and recontain DSC-MATE-8814 with ease, but the incident highlights the need for strict electronic communication monitoring and vigilance around DSC-MATE-8814 at all times.
