Item #: SCP-2288

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2288 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Access to SCP-2288 is limited to Level 2 personnel and above. Any interaction with SCP-2288 must be supervised by at least two security personnel. SCP-2288 is to be provided with appropriate clothing and accessories as its preference dictates.

Description: SCP-2288 is a human male identified as a car salesman by occupation. SCP-2288's anomalous properties manifest in its physical appearance. Its face displays orange stripes, and its mouth appears to be perpetually in a frightened position. SCP-2288's eyes resemble ski goggles, and its head is adorned with green hair in a purple cap. SCP-2288 sports a green cross earring and a gold chain as an accessory. The most notable item in SCP-2288's possession is a LED gas mask that it wears when interacting with others.

SCP-2288's personality exhibits a strong sense of duty and responsibility, combined with a propensity for fun. SCP-2288 has been known to entertain its peers with humor and creative performances, making it the life of the party. However, SCP-2288's eagerness to please others has resulted in inappropriate discussions and behaviors on occasion, leading to anxiety and stress in social situations.

Addendum 1: SCP-2288's anomalous effects have been observed to cause a memetic effect on others. Individuals who interact with SCP-2288 tend to express a positive outlook and a willingness to engage in entertaining activities, as well as develop a liking to SCP-2288.

Addendum 2: SCP-2288 has exhibited an unusual ability to move through solid materials without visible signs of resistance. The mechanics behind this ability are currently unknown, and research is ongoing. SCP-2288 is cooperating with Foundation researchers in exploring the limits and effects of this ability.
