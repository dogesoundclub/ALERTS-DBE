Item #: 3527

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: 3527 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-19. Access to 3527 is granted to personnel with level 2 clearance or above. 3527 is permitted to continue his occupation as a news anchor for the public news network.

Description: 3527 is a human male with orange stripes running along the length of his face. He also possesses a pair of goggles positioned atop his forehead, and a jade earring in his left ear. When not on air, he is often seen wearing a red scarf around his neck and smoking a cigarette.

3527's most notable trait is his charisma, allowing him to easily connect with people on a deep level. Through his power of persuasion, strangers are compelled to share their life stories and intimate details with him. He is also known for his quick wit and humorous anecdotes, which helps bring people together.

3527's abilities are not inherently harmful, though caution should still be exercised in regards to the information he has access to. It is believed that his abilities are a result of unknown anomalous influence.

Addendum: 3527's broadcasting abilities have proven highly valuable in gathering information for various Foundation operations. Permission has been granted for limited use of 3527's abilities in order to gain insight into certain subjects or individuals of interest.
