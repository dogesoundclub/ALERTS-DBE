SCP Foundation Containment Report #6627

Item #: SCP-6627

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-6627 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber lined with reinforced steel walls. Under no circumstances should anyone enter SCP-6627's chamber without the expressed permission of an O5 Council member. Any personnel within 50 meters of SCP-6627 must maintain minimal noise levels and remain as still as possible. Any sudden movements or loud noises may provoke SCP-6627's aggressive behavior.

Description:

SCP-6627 is a male tiger standing at approximately 6 feet tall. SCP-6627 is highly intelligent and has shown a natural talent for leadership, often taking charge of situations. SCP-6627 often wears a doge hat and nerd glasses, adorned with a gold chain and candy stick.

SCP-6627's heightened intelligence and natural leadership abilities have made him highly dangerous. SCP-6627 has been known to manipulate and control those around him, forcing them to do his bidding. SCP-6627's personality makes him highly unpredictable and difficult to contain.

SCP-6627 has shown the ability to summon a swarm of bees at will, using them to attack and subdue his target. SCP-6627 has also been known to create large-scale illusions, confusing and disorienting those around him.

SCP-6627 is highly aggressive and should be approached with extreme caution. Any attempt to engage or provoke SCP-6627 will result in a swift and brutal response. 

Addendum:

SCP-6627 was brought to the attention of the Foundation after local authorities reported a series of unusual incidents involving a fashion designer named Tony. Witnesses reported that Tony, who was later identified as SCP-6627, was able to control people's thoughts and actions, using them to carry out his every whim. Foundation agents were dispatched to contain SCP-6627, and he was successfully apprehended without incident. SCP-6627 is to be monitored at all times for any signs of escape or potential breach.
