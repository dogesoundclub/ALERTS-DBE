It has also been noted to smoke cigarettes, which can lead to health complications.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5612’s origins remain unknown, but it was recovered in the city of ██████ where it was working as a delivery driver. It is believed to have gained its human-like abilities through anomalous means.
