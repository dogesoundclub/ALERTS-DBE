The LED Gas Mask worn by DSC-MATE-4758 is to be removed during testing and interviews, but otherwise left on. The use of amnestics is approved for all individuals directly exposed to DSC-MATE-4758.

DSC-MATE-4758's head is adorned with green hair in a purple cap, with a two-colored band on their forehead. They also possess a jade earring, a silver chain, and an LED gas mask-like accessory.

DSC-MATE-4758 has displayed a highly energetic and enthusiastic personality, which can at times border on mania. DSC-MATE-4758 may attempt to excite and engage others in their activities, sometimes with humorous and sometimes with uncomfortable results. Despite this, DSC-MATE-4758 shows no signs of being dangerous or hostile towards others.

DSC-MATE-4758 has reported working at a local fast-food establishment, with no knowledge of their anomalous nature. Foundation agents were able to interview DSC-MATE-4758 under the guise of market research, with amnestics administered upon completion.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-4758's LED gas mask is a unique piece of technology, providing varying levels of light and sound. Further study and testing are required to determine its nature and any additional anomalous properties it may possess.
