Item #: SCP-9653

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9653 is to be kept in a maximum-security facility at all times. All personnel must wear safety gear when interacting with SCP-9653. Any communication with SCP-9653 must be monitored and approved by at least two Level 4 personnel.

Description: SCP-9653 is a humanoid male, identified as a Mafia Boss in his previous life. SCP-9653 displays various physical abnormalities, such as random spots on his skin, and a persistent smile on his face. SCP-9653's eyes are artificial and emit a rainbow-colored LED light at all times.

SCP-9653's head always dons a retro-style cap, while his ears are adorned with a golden earring. SCP-9653 frequently wears a red scarf and carries a candy stick as an accessory.

SCP-9653's personality is indicative of an imaginative and creative individual. The subject is interested in writing poetry, creating elaborate cosplay costumes, and collecting unusual objects. SCP-9653 also displays exceptional leadership skills and has an innate ability to manipulate individuals through his charm and charisma.

Addendum:

SCP-9653 is considered an anomalous entity as it displays a potent ability to influence individuals' thoughts and actions. Despite the subject's jovial demeanor, personnel are reminded that SCP-9653 is an expert in his craft and should not be underestimated.
