Item #: SCP-4984

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4984, a human veterinarian with extraordinary abilities, must be kept in a designated containment chamber at Site-19. SCP-4984 must be monitored around the clock for any signs of anomalous behavior or activity.

Description: SCP-4984 is a male human, approximately 35 years of age. A unique feature of SCP-4984's face is the presence of bright orange stripes on his cheeks. SCP-4984 is often seen wearing a retro cap and nerd glasses, with a gold earring in his left ear. His mouth is almost always covered by a medical mask, as is common in his profession.

SCP-4984 is highly intelligent and is known for his love of debate and quick wit. This trait can lead to humorous situations, where SCP-4984 will engage in playful arguments with friends or family members just for fun. SCP-4984 is highly skilled in veterinary medicine and is known for his ability to communicate with animals. 

SCP-4984 also seems to possess some level of telepathy, allowing him to communicate with specific individuals regardless of distance or language barriers. However, this ability has yet to be fully understood or documented.

SCP-4984's demeanor is mostly cooperative and cooperative when not agitated, often showing a friendly smile. However, under stress or confrontation, SCP-4984 can become unpredictable and dangerous. SCP-4984 is known to exhibit anomalous abilities when agitated, including the sudden appearance of claws and fangs.

Addendum: SCP-4984 was discovered in a small veterinary clinic in ████████, where he was working as a veterinarian. Foundation personnel were alerted to unusual animal behavior in the area, leading to the discovery of SCP-4984's anomalous abilities. Further investigation is ongoing.
