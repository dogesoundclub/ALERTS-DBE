Item #: SCP-7292

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7292 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber with access to a small collection of newspapers and a computer for writing reports. Contact with SCP-7292 is to be limited to Foundation personnel with Level 2 clearance or higher.

Description: SCP-7292 is a male monkey with a frightened mouth and eyes behind nerd glasses. Its head fur is not arranged, appearing green in color. SCP-7292 has a pair of jade earrings, wears a red scarf and a gas mask commonly found in urban areas.

SCP-7292 is a reporter by profession and is known for its tendencies to behave impulsively, often making spontaneous decisions that may seem amusing or surprising. Despite its overall friendly demeanor, SCP-7292 seems to possess glimmers of paranoia, often fearing for its own safety.

SCP-7292's journalistic skills have brought it to the attention of the Foundation, after it infiltrated several restricted areas in search of a story. The Foundation, upon discovering SCP-7292's anomalous nature, has contained it, while providing it with a stable environment for work.

Addendum: SCP-7292's writings have been under observation for any anomalous activity or information leaks about the Foundation. So far, SCP-7292 has not disclosed any confidential information within its writings or to other individuals. Nonetheless, additional monitoring is required.
