Item #: 7167

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 7167 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. Any instances of her aforementioned accessories are to be stored in a secure locker in Site-██. Experimentation with 7167 is permitted for research purposes only under the supervision of at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC 7167 appears to be a human female of average height and weight, with blue clown eyes and red lipstick. Her head is often concealed by a hoodie with a two color band on her forehead. She is often seen wearing a choker and an LED gas mask. DSC 7167 works as a beautician in a local salon.

While DSC 7167 appears to be a normal human, she possesses an abnormal level of care and nurturing. She treats those close to her, including friends and family, as if they were her own children. This can lead to humorous situations, such as insisting on tucking in a grown man or reminding someone to wear a jacket on a chilly day.

DSC 7167 is also known to possess a strong will and a sharp tongue, often using her skills in cosmetics to intimidate her opponents. She has been observed to possess expert knowledge in the application of makeup and has been known to use it for various effects.

Addendum: DSC 7167 has been noted to have a particular interest in anomalous beauty products. These products appear to have been tampered with or possessed some sort of memetic effect. Research into the effects of these products is ongoing. It is advised that caution be taken in the use of any beauty products associated with DSC 7167.
