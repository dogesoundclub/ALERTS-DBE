Item #: SCP-2604

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2604 must be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with heavy duty restraints. It must remain under constant video surveillance and be guarded by at least two trained security personnel at all times. Under no circumstances should SCP-2604 be allowed to make physical contact with any staff member or attempt to escape its containment.

Description: SCP-2604 is a human female of unknown origin, with orange stripes on its face that resemble tiger markings. It wears normal glasses over its eyes, a bucket hat on its head, and a gold earring on its left ear. SCP-2604 also wears a silver chain as an accessory. It is known to exhibit a volatile personality, displaying a great sense of humor that often leads to inappropriate behavior and violence.

SCP-2604 enjoys making others laugh, even when it involves disturbing or dangerous methods. It often makes use of its talent for impersonations and storytelling, which it uses to manipulate others and escape from dangerous situations. SCP-2604 seems to possess an innate talent for deception and destruction, using its humor as a cover for its malicious intent.

SCP-2604 was captured after numerous reports of unexplained incidents involving humor gone wrong. The Foundation obtained SCP-2604 on a raid on a known villain hideout. SCP-2604 resisted capture multiple times with aggressive tactics that resulted in the deaths of several personnel.

Personnel coming into contact with SCP-2604 are advised to approach with caution and assume nothing. SCP-2604 is a trained villain, capable of using any means necessary to escape and should be considered dangerous at all times.
