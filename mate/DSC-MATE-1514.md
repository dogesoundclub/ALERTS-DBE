Item #: DSC-MATE-1514

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-1514 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber with a minimum area of 30 square meters. The door to the chamber must be composed of reinforced steel with a locking mechanism that is only accessible by Level 3 personnel and above. DSC-MATE-1514 is to be fed a diet of raw meat twice a day and is to be monitored at all times via remote camera.

Description: DSC-MATE-1514 is a male Siberian tiger that previously worked as a banker prior to its containment. DSC-MATE-1514 measures approximately 2 meters in length and displays a variety of physical features including a purple cap with a headset, two color bands on its forehead, jade earring, and a gold chain. DSC-MATE-1514 is known to wear nerd glasses.

DSC-MATE-1514 displays a high level of empathy and compassion and often tries to fix everything and everyone around them. However, DSC-MATE-1514's tendency to take on other people's problems as their own can be both admirable and exhausting. DSC-MATE-1514 has shown signs of irritation when it is unable to help others, leading to emotional distress.

DSC-MATE-1514 is not to be approached by anyone outside of authorized personnel due to its unpredictable behavior. DSC-MATE-1514 has been known to become aggressive when it is unable to help others and any interaction with DSC-MATE-1514 must be approved by a Level 3 personnel or above.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-1514 was contained after reports of a tiger attacking and attempting to "fix" individuals in public spaces. A team of Foundation agents were dispatched and were able to contain DSC-MATE-1514 without further incident. DSC-MATE-1514 was subsequently brought back to the Foundation for containment and observation.
