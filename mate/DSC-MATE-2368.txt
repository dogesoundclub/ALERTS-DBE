Item #: SCP-2368

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2368 has been designated as Safe and is to be kept in a standard human containment cell. Any interactions with SCP-2368 must be monitored by security personnel at all times. SCP-2368 is allowed low-risk recreational activities such as reading and watching movies, but all other requests must be approved by the Site Director.

Description: SCP-2368 refers to a male theater manager with green hair and a purple cap. SCP-2368 can often be seen wearing a medical mask and a gold chain. SCP-2368 values harmony and social connections, which makes them particularly amiable towards others.

SCP-2368 has a goofy side and has been known to engage in playful banter with others, which can lead to humorous situations. SCP-2368 enjoys telling jokes and is particularly enthusiastic about making others laugh.

SCP-2368's behavior is indicative of a healthy and stable individual; however, it is unknown whether SCP-2368 has any anomalous abilities. Through extensive evaluation, there is no evidence to support any studies of SCP-2368 beyond regular human behavior and personality. 

Addendum 2368-1: SCP-2368’s containment procedures have been updated due to ongoing research. As a gesture of appreciation for their compliance, SCP-2368 has been allowed to continue with their work at the Site's theatre, with additional supervisor support. There is currently no evidence of risk to SCP-2368 within their job position. 

Addendum 2368-2: SCP-2368 has expressed appreciation for the efforts made in accommodating their job positions. SCP-2368 continues to entertain residents at the Site’s Theatre, with supervised controls as per approved protocols.
