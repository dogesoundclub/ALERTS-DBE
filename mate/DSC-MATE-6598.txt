Item #: SCP-6598

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6598 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. SCP-6598 is permitted to wear their standard police uniform during waking hours and is permitted to read and access the internet for entertainment purposes only. Any attempts to contact SCP-6598's family must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-6598 is a human female with numerous spots on her face and an orange eyebrow. SCP-6598 wears a hoodie and a medical mask, with their smile being the only visible facial expression. SCP-6598 is a police officer with a passion for social connections and harmony. However, SCP-6598 also possesses a playful, goofy side that often manifests through humor and playful banter. The combination of their social and humorous nature has garnered SCP-6598 popularity within their community.

SCP-6598 was brought to the attention of the Foundation due to an incident in which SCP-6598's sense of humor led to a containment breach. Despite initial concerns, SCP-6598 proved to be compliant with containment and has shown no signs of attempting to breach containment again.

Addendum: SCP-6598 has expressed interest in participating in Foundation operations that require social interaction or infiltration. While this request has been denied, it has been noted for future reference.
