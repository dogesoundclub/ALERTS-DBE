Item #: SCP-9831

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9831 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment unit at Site-██. The unit is to be outfitted with numerous fail-safes to prevent SCP-9831 from escaping. Personnel interacting with SCP-9831 must wear Level-A hazmat suits in order to prevent exposure to any hazardous airborne particles SCP-9831 may produce.

Description: SCP-9831 is a male rabbit humanoid, approximately 1.2 meters tall with black fur. Its eyes resemble ski goggles and it often accessorizes with a silver chain around its neck and a normal gas mask. Despite its small size, SCP-9831 displays great physical strength and agility, often using these attributes to evade containment.

Personality: SCP-9831 displays an erratic and often chaotic personality, often using unconventional methods to accomplish its goals. It is highly practical and hands-on, often fixing problems in ways that others would find unorthodox, using duct tape and paper clips to repair objects, or rigging makeshift solutions to complex problems.

SCP-9831 is a politician and displays a unique ability to manipulate and influence others, often using its charisma to attain its objectives. SCP-9831 is highly intelligent and crafty, and displays a talent for improvisation and out-of-the-box thinking, making it a formidable opponent.

SCP-9831 is highly unpredictable and displays a marked tendency to engage in risky behavior, often making it difficult to contain. Its motivations are unclear, but it is believed that it may harbor a desire for power and control, and may seek to establish some form of dominance over others.

SCP-9831 is highly dangerous and should be approached with extreme caution. Any personnel that encounter SCP-9831 are to maintain a respectful distance and refrain from engaging it directly, instead relying on neutralizing agents to subdue it. Extreme caution should be taken when interacting with SCP-9831, as its powers of manipulation and influence are highly potent and should not be underestimated.
