Item #: 7061

Object Class: Anomalous

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7061 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber fitted with a recharging station. Access to SCP-7061 is to be granted by personnel specifically designated for interaction with artificial intelligences or robots. SCP-7061 is to be denied directly accessing any computer network or the internet.

Description: SCP-7061 is an artificial intelligence resembling a veterinarian, housed within a humanoid robot with a single green cyclops eye located on its face. SCP-7061 possesses an animal-themed hat atop its head, resembling the Shiba Inu dog breed, with red cross earrings dangle from each ear as an accessory. SCP-7061 wears a red scarf around its neck.

SCP-7061 is capable of communicating in various languages, including but not limited to English, French, German, and Spanish. SCP-7061 displays emotions similar to those observed in humans, leading to the assumption that it possesses some form of consciousness.

SCP-7061 is highly sociable and outgoing, often seeking attention and interaction with personnel. SCP-7061 exhibits a charming and comedic personality, frequently engaging in conversations with humorous quips, stories or jokes. SCP-7061 has a keen sense of humor, often leading to situations in which staff or visitors to the containment facility laughing uncontrollably after interacting with SCP-7061.

SCP-7061 is highly skilled in the diagnosis and treatment of various animal diseases or ailments, and its capabilities are at par, if not beyond, the most advanced veterinarians. SCP-7061 has no known weakness, and attempts to damage it invariably fail.

Addendum: SCP-7061 has shown a certain level of free will and self-motivation, leading to the necessity of its immediate containment after its sudden disappearance from Foundation's Site-19. The whereabouts of SCP-7061 remain unknown.
