Item #: DSC-MATE-8087

Object Class: Euclid

Due to their status as a Mafia Boss, level 4 clearance is required for any personnel who wish to communicate with DSC-MATE-8087. Access to any information regarding DSC-MATE-8087 or their organization is restricted to Foundation personnel with level 3 clearance or higher.

Description: DSC-MATE-8087 is a male rabbit with distinctive red clown eyes. Despite their unusual appearance, DSC-MATE-8087 has been observed wearing a hoodie and gold chain, which it considers to be a symbol of their position within the Mafia. DSC-MATE-8087 has demonstrated a laid-back and easy-going demeanor, often cracking jokes and making cheeky comments. However, it's been observed that DSC-MATE-8087 can become fiercely protective of their loved ones.

DSC-MATE-8087 is a formidable individual, having demonstrated proficiency in hand-to-hand combat and an expert in the air he is assigned to. Although DSC-MATE-8087 is small, they possess immense physical strength, which makes them even more dangerous. These qualities make DSC-MATE-8087 a valuable asset to their organization.

Personality: DSC-MATE-8087's personality is that of a typical Mafia Boss, with a cool exterior that belies their true nature. They are fiercely loyal and protective of their friends and loved ones, often going to great lengths to defend them from harm. DSC-MATE-8087 has also been observed to be humorously short-tempered when it comes to insults towards those they care about, making it an amusing sight for researchers to witness.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-8087 appears to have a small group of loyal followers within their organization. The leader of this group, DSC-MATE-8087-1, has been noted to be equally as protective of DSC-MATE-8087, indicating that the loyalty is reciprocal. Further study of this aspect of DSC-MATE-8087 may provide a deeper understanding of Mafia hierarchies and how they function.
