Item #: SCP-2465

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2465 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. The cell must be monitored at all times via CCTV, and guards must be present during any interactions with SCP-2465. Any personnel assigned to SCP-2465 must undergo extensive psychological screenings and be trained in non-lethal combat techniques.

Description: SCP-2465 is a male human of indeterminate age, approximately 6 feet tall with a lean build. SCP-2465 has a distinct mouth spot on the right side of his lips and a frightened mouth expression. His normal glasses and retro cap covers his eyes and head, respectively. SCP-2465 has an earring that spans a green cross and carries a cigarette as his principal accessory.

SCP-2465's most notable trait is his introspective and daydreamy nature. His tendency to get lost in thought can cause humorous situations where he trips or crashes into various obstacles. SCP-2465 has an unsettling obsession with causing chaos and destruction, leading him to become known as a villain.

SCP-2465 possesses above-average combat skills; any interaction must be done with caution. His multiple escape attempts and dangerous behavior suggest that he can create plans and makes schemes capable of harming individuals he dislikes. 

In conclusion, it is imperative to keep SCP-2465 securely contained on-site for the safety of the facility and the public at large. Any changes in behavior or attempts to escape must be immediately reported to the containment team.
