Item #: SCP-3490

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3490 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Access to SCP-3490 is restricted unless authorized by Level 3 or higher personnel. SCP-3490’s medical mask is to be inspected regularly to ensure it remains secure and functional.

Description: SCP-3490 is a human female of average height and build with orange stripes on their face and red lips. They possess orange eyebrows, 00s school girl hairstyle, and wear a silver chain around their neck. On their forehead, SCP-3490 wears a goggle, and they wear a single jade earring on their right ear.

SCP-3490’s personality is characterized by rebelliousness and a tendency to push boundaries. They enjoy breaking rules and testing boundaries, sometimes just to see what happens. SCP-3490 has displayed a nonchalant and playful attitude towards many of the Foundation’s attempts at containing them.

SCP-3490 has a notable penchant for law and has identified themselves as a lawyer. It is currently unknown how SCP-3490 obtained their law degree or if they possess a legal license.

SCP-3490 has not exhibited any anomalous abilities or properties. However, given their tendency towards disobedience, it is recommended that they be contained under close observation.

Addendum-3490-1: Incident Log

On ██/██/20██, SCP-3490 was found attempting to persuade two security guards to let them leave their containment chamber. SCP-3490 claimed they were simply testing security measures and was not acting maliciously. After several minutes of discussion, SCP-3490 agreed to return to their containment chamber with minimal resistance.

On ██/██/20██, SCP-3490 was found in possession of a Foundation document that outlined a containment breach procedure. SCP-3490 claimed they were conducting research on Foundation protocols and meant no harm. Following the confiscation of the document, SCP-3490 was placed under closer observation.
