Item #: SCP-3009

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3009 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. They are to be provided with standard amenities and given access to an exercise yard for one hour per day. SCP-3009 is to be monitored at all times through CCTV cameras and audio equipment.

Description: SCP-3009 is a human female in her mid-30s. She has purple lips, blue clown eyes, and a green, curly-edged head of hair. SCP-3009 also wears a single gold earring in her left ear. They are a firefighter by profession.

SCP-3009's personality can be described as reliable and organized, with a tendency towards stubbornness. They have a hard time accepting new ideas or changes to their routine, which can lead to humorous situations when they encounter unexpected challenges. SCP-3009 has shown no signs of anomalous behavior or abilities.

Addendum: SCP-3009 was discovered following reports of a fire in a residential building in ████, ██. During the rescue operation, SCP-3009 displayed no signs of panic or fear while carrying out their duties. Foundation personnel were alerted to SCP-3009's anomalous appearance and behavior during interviews with witnesses.

SCP-3009 was taken into custody and transported to Site-██ for containment and study. Further investigation revealed no other anomalous effects aside from their appearance and personality traits. SCP-3009 is considered to be a stable and low-risk object.
