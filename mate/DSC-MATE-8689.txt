Item #: SCP-8689

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8689 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. SCP-8689 is to be provided with standard-issue clothing and given access to any recreational or educational material available upon request.

Description: 

SCP-8689 is a female human of average height and build, with orange stripes on their face and red lips. SCP-8689's eyes are the most unique feature, being VR Jade LED. SCP-8689's hair is green and is usually combed into a purple cap. A pair of goggles rests upon the forehead, and SCP-8689 wears gold earrings and a choker necklace.

SCP-8689 is a firefighter by trade, who displays a deep desire to make the world a better place. SCP-8689 is affable and cooperative, and has shown no significant anomalous behavior or abilities.

Addendum 8689-1:

Despite SCP-8689's portrayal of a cooperative and helpful individual, it is to be noted that SCP-8689 displays tendencies towards activism and protesting when they believe that a company conflicts with their values. As such, vetting of companies and events that SCP-8689 is allowed to attend is recommended.
