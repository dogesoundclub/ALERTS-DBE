Item #: SCP-8898

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8898 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times, with access to basic grooming supplies. SCP-8898 is not to be allowed to leave its containment chamber unless authorized by at least two Level 3 personnel. Anywho interact with SCP-8898 is to wear earplugs and eye protection at all times.

Description: SCP-8898 appears as a human male, standing at 1.67 meters in height. The subject has dark skin with several light spots scattered across its body. The tail of a small mouse is attached to the back of the subject's peak cap, and multiple rainbow LED lights are visible within the subject's eyes. SCP-8898 often wears a medical mask and has a pair of goggles resting on its forehead. One gold earring is visible on each ear, and the subject wears a silver chain around its neck.

SCP-8898 has shown to be extremely outgoing and social, often attempting to be the center of attention. Its humorous and lighthearted nature can lead to it telling jokes or stories that result in uncontrollable laughter from those around it. However, SCP-8898's presence often causes individuals to experience disorientation and hearing loss, which has resulted in severe injuries in some cases.

SCP-8898 is a beautician by trade, often using its abilities to enhance the beauty of individuals that are permitted to interact with it. SCP-8898's abilities are not of anomalous origin, however its presence and physical appearance have resulted in its classification as an SCP entity.

Addendum: SCP-8898 has expressed interest in pursuing beauty treatments on other SCP objects within Foundation containment. This request has been denied, as it could result in potential breaches of containment protocols.
