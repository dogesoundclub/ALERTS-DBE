Item #: SCP-6466

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6466 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Due to the nature of SCP-6466’s job as a fast food worker, they are allowed to leave containment for scheduled shifts at the ████ ███████████ restaurant with the understanding that they are to return immediately following their shift.

SCP-6466's LED gas mask is to be regulated and monitored by Site-██’s technical staff in order to prevent any hacking or tampering of the device.

Description: SCP-6466 is a Caucasian male, standing at approximately 5'11" and weighing 185 lbs. Their most distinctive features include a short peak cap, normal glasses, a red victory band, a jade earring in their left ear, a goggle on their forehead, a red scarf, and an LED gas mask.

SCP-6466's abilities have not yet been determined, although it is suspected that they possess a vivid imagination and a high level of creativity. SCP-6466 has expressed interest in activities such as poetry, cosplay, and object collection, which may indicate a pre-disposition towards creative expression.

Addendum: SCP-6466 has displayed no anomalous properties during their time in containment. Experimentation on SCP-6466 is still ongoing.
