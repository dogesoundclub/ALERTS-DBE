Item #: SCP-7425

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7425 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber with access to medical equipment and supplies. SCP-7425 may leave their containment chamber for medical consultations authorized by the Site Director.

Description: SCP-7425 is a human female with orange stripes on her face, a smile, and blue clown eyes. SCP-7425's head is adorned with various rainbow-colored accessories frequently seen at festivals. They willingly wear a medical mask over their mouth and a choker around their neck.

SCP-7425 is a medical professional and has displayed exceptional knowledge in the field of medicine. They have been cooperative and enthusiastic towards Foundation personnel during their stay at Site-███, often engaging in friendly conversations and light-hearted jokes with staff.

SCP-7425's outgoing and sociable personality has proven to be an asset in their field of study. They tend to enjoy being the center of attention and often tells humorous stories and jokes. This trait, coupled with their medical proficiency, has made SCP-7425 a favorite among Foundation personnel. 

SCP-7425's origin is currently unknown, however, their ability to perform medical procedures is confirmed. Further research into SCP-7425's medical knowledge and abilities is ongoing.
