Item #: SCP-8115

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8115 is to be kept under supervised observation at all times while on duty as a delivery driver. Due to SCP-8115's fondness for thrill-seeking activities, it is strictly forbidden from engaging in any activities that could potentially result in harm to civilians or SCP Foundation personnel.

Description: SCP-8115 is a male human with several unique physical features. Its eyes resemble ski goggles, positioned over the skin and functioning with no known mechanism of support. On its forehead, SCP-8115 wears a goggle uncovered at all times. SCP-8115 also has a jade earring attached to its left earlobe. During delivery shifts, SCP-8115 wears a medical mask to cover its mouth. SCP-8115 is often seen with a silver chain as an accessory.

SCP-8115 demonstrates a love for adventure and a tendency towards taking risks. This trait has led to several humorous stories, including surviving a near-death experience while bungee jumping and getting lost in the wilderness due to overzealous exploration. SCP-8115's risky behavior often leads to chaos and puts other SCP Foundation personnel in danger. As a result, SCP-8115's movements have been restricted and closely monitored.

Addendum 8115-1: Incident Report

On [REDACTED], SCP-8115 deviated from its delivery route, stating that it was following a "better, more exciting" path. SCP-8115's deviation led to a confused bystander being seriously injured. After reviewing the incident, SCP-8115 was terminated from delivery duties until further notice. It is deemed a suitable candidate for further exploration and SCiP classification.
