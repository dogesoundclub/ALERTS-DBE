Item #: SCP-2956

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2956 is to be monitored at all times by at least two security personnel, who are to wear protective gear for their own safety. SCP-2956 must reside in a specially constructed cell within Site-██ that is made of reinforced steel and lead. Upon entry, SCP-2956 must be sedated and restrained before any interactions take place.

Description: SCP-2956 is a human female of average height and weight, with distinct orange stripes on her cheeks and a vibrant red lip. Her head is blonde and features a single wing that protrudes from her left side. SCP-2956's eyes are replaced with VR Rainbow LED lenses that constantly emit a bright glowing light. She also wears a medical mask on her mouth at all times, which is believed to be a filter to prevent foreign entities from entering her respiratory system.

SCP-2956 is often seen wearing a gold chain around her neck, which is thought to be an integral part of her tradition and culture. SCP-2956 is a train driver by profession and is known to operate locomotives in the suburbs of ███████.

SCP-2956 has a personality that values tradition and often displays a no-nonsense attitude when dealing with others. However, she does possess a playful side, which can lead to humorous situations. SCP-2956 often tells jokes and stories that have been passed down through her family or community, causing her to be a favorite among her colleagues.

SCP-2956's history is largely unknown, but it is believed that her unique appearance and abilities can be attributed to experimentation and manipulation by an unknown organization. Further research is needed to determine the full extent of SCP-2956's abilities and any potential threats she may pose.
