Item #: 6173

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6173 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. The chamber is to be equipped with a computer with internet access to allow SCP-6173 to continue its programming work.

SCP-6173 is to be provided with an LED gas mask at all times to prevent any adverse reactions to harmful gases. Any requests for additional accessories should be approved by the Site Director.

Description: SCP-6173 appears to be a male rabbit with red clown eyes and a frightened mouth. It wears a purple cap with a headset on top and a goggle on its forehead. It has gold earrings and a gold chain around its neck.

SCP-6173's personality is laid-back and easy-going, but it is fiercely loyal and protective of its loved ones. This can lead to some humorous situations, such as getting into a heated argument with someone who insults a friend or family member.

SCP-6173 is a skilled programmer and has created numerous software programs for both personal and professional use. It has been known to collaborate with Foundation personnel on various technological projects.

Addendum: SCP-6173's programming skills have been invaluable to the Foundation in developing new security systems and improving existing ones. As such, it is to be treated with respect and given the necessary resources to continue its work.
