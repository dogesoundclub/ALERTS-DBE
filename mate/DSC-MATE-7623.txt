Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7623 is to be monitored at all times by Foundation personnel wearing appropriate protective gear. SCP-7623 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Any requests for additional reading or research material should be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-7623 is a male human of average height and build, with orange stripes on his face and a clown mouth. Their distinctive feature is their blue clown eyes and mohawk. SCP-7623 wears a red scarf as an accessory. SCP-7623 works as a real estate agent, primarily targeting properties that are rumored or known to be haunted or otherwise associated with paranormal activity.

SCP-7623 has a remarkable thirst for knowledge, and their interests often extend into dark and obscure territories. Their passion for collecting books or other educational material could provide useful insights into the supernatural, although their motives may not always be altruistic.

SCP-7623 is a charismatic individual, and they are known to enjoy sharing their knowledge with others, especially when discussing paranormal phenomena. Their contagious enthusiasm may draw in individuals who may not have otherwise shown an interest in the occult.

SCP-7623 has shown no signs of aggression, but due to its anomalous nature, it should be carefully monitored at all times.

Addendum: SCP-7623 has requested additional research materials on the topic of possession and exorcism. All requests have been approved with caution.
