Item #: SCP-5865

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5865 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell in Site-██. It is not to be allowed contact with any individuals outside of research purposes. Any requests made by SCP-5865 for items or materials are to be approved on a case-by-case basis.

Description: SCP-5865 appears to be a human male in his late 20s to early 30s, approximately 178cm in height and weighing 87.5kg. SCP-5865 has multiple spots on his face and red clown eyes, which are believed to be a result of anomalous means. SCP-5865 is typically seen wearing a purple cap with a headset and a jade earring, as well as a gold chain.

SCP-5865 works as a real estate agent, and is known for his ability to observe and attend to small details. SCP-5865 is an excellent gift-giver, although his desire to give the "perfect" gift has caused a great deal of anguish and preoccupation for them. Despite these quirks, SCP-5865 is generally cooperative with Foundation staff.

Addendum: Given its abilities, SCP-5865 could be utilized for reconnaissance missions in the future. This concept is under consideration, pending further testing and evaluation.
