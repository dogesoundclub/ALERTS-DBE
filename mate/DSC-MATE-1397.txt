Item #: SCP-1397

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1397 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times when not actively working as a Theater Manager. SCP-1397 is not to be allowed any contact with the outside world without prior approval from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-1397 is a humanoid creature bearing the appearance of a tiger. SCP-1397 stands approximately 6 feet tall and weighs approximately 300 pounds. SCP-1397's head is adorned with a red beanie with a 2-color band around the forehead. SCP-1397's ears are pierced and sport a gold earring on each side. SCP-1397 also wears a gold chain around its neck and an LED gas mask as a mouth covering.

SCP-1397 exhibits a strong desire to make the world a better place, often leading to irrational and humorous situations. SCP-1397 has been known to protest or boycott companies or causes that it feels go against its values, even when such action is not necessary or reasonable. Due to this behavior, SCP-1397 often draws unwanted attention to itself, and special care must be taken when providing SCP-1397 with any opportunities for activism.

SCP-1397 is currently employed as a Theater Manager at [REDACTED]. SCP-1397 has thus far exhibited no unusual behavior while performing its job duties.

Addendum: Due to SCP-1397's unusual appearance and behavior, it is recommended that any interactions should be supervised by at least two Level 3 personnel to avoid any unexpected outcomes.
