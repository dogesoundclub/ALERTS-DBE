Item #: SCP-9288

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9288 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-17. Access to SCP-9288 is restricted to Level 2 personnel or higher. SCP-9288's veterinary practice is to be monitored at all times for the possibility of anomalous animal activity.

Description: SCP-9288 is a human male with distinctive facial features including orange stripes and a permanent smile. SCP-9288's eyes are composed of VR jade LED lights and their head is adorned with a "Doge" hat. SCP-9288 also possesses green cross earrings, a gold chain, and a normal gas mask. It is unknown how SCP-9288 acquired these accessory items.

SCP-9288 displays a heightened sensitivity to emotions and has been known to become emotional during sentimental moments in films or TV shows. SCP-9288 also shows a strong attachment to music and art that evokes intense feelings. SCP-9288 works as a veterinarian, and there have been reports of anomalous animal activity in the vicinity of their practice.

SCP-9288 has not displayed any anomalous abilities or behaviors beyond their heightened emotional sensitivity and attraction to anomalous animals. Further research into SCP-9288's possible connection to the anomalous animal activity reported around their practice is ongoing.

Addendum: SCP-9288 has expressed an interest in working with anomalous animals contained by the Foundation. This request is currently under review by Site-17 personnel.
