Item #: SCP-9160

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9160 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. He must be provided with appropriate facilities to continue his job as a train driver with the supervision of a Level 3 personnel. SCP-9160's access to Foundation databases must be monitored at all times.

Description: SCP-9160 is a human male train driver, identified as ██████. The subject has orange stripes on both sides of his face, with a purple cap adorned with a headset on his head. He also has nerd glasses covering his eyes, a gold earring on each ear, and a silver chain around his neck. 

SCP-9160 wears a gas mask as an accessory, which is not required due to any anomalous properties but for personal reasons. The subject's forehead features a two-color band and also has goggles placed on top of his forehead.

SCP-9160 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. He often tells funny stories and does silly impersonations. His humorous nature has led to some incidents within Foundation facilities, including but not limited to causing personnel to break protocol to laugh at his jokes. 

SCP-9160 does not exhibit any anomalous properties except for his appearance. However, as per the Foundation protocol, all individuals that display any unusual appearance or behavior are to be contained and monitored until proven to be safe for release.

Addendum: SCP-9160 was detained after a civilian reported a strange-looking train driver in their local train station. Upon the arrival of the Mobile Task Force, SCP-9160 was taken into Foundation custody without incident. Further investigation into SCP-9160's origins is ongoing.
