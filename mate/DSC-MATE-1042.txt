Item #: SCP-1042

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1042 is to be contained in a standard living quarters at Site-17. Access to SCP-1042 is to be granted only with Level 2 clearance and above, and interactions with SCP-1042 are to be monitored at all times.

SCP-1042 is allowed to carry out her job as a theater manager, but must not make any attempts to use her anomalous charisma to manipulate or control individuals, especially Foundation personnel.

Any instances of SCP-1042's anomalous abilities must be reported to the Site Director immediately.

Description: SCP-1042 appears as a Caucasian female with red lipstick, blue clown eyes, and a retro cap. SCP-1042 wears a choker as an accessory, which is believed to have no anomalous properties.

SCP-1042's anomalous abilities revolve around her exceptional charisma and ability to connect with others on a deeply personal level. Individuals who come into contact with SCP-1042 are immediately drawn to her, and are often willing to share intimate details about their lives.

SCP-1042's abilities have the potential to be harmful, as she could potentially manipulate and control individuals to carry out her own desires. Thus, caution must be exercised when interacting with SCP-1042.

Personality: SCP-1042 is a personable, outgoing individual who is well-liked by most people. She enjoys meeting new people and making connections, and is considered a natural born entertainer.

Despite her abilities, SCP-1042 is generally cooperative with Foundation personnel and is willing to follow proper protocol. She is aware of the potential dangers her abilities pose, and actively works to avoid any situations that may cause harm.
