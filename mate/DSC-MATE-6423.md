DSC-MATE-6423's head is adorned with a bucket hat, and it wears a golden chain around its neck. Its face is equipped with a green cyclops eye and a frightened mouth. It has horned rimmed glasses which serve as its eyes.

DSC-MATE-6423's primary function is that of a security guard, and it is equipped with various gadgets and weaponry to protect its assigned location. Despite its programmed objective, DSC-MATE-6423 has demonstrated a high level of empathy and compassion, indicating that it has an advanced AI system. DSC-MATE-6423 tends to take on other people's problems as its own and feels responsible for fixing everything and everyone around it. Although this trait is admirable, it can be exhausting for DSC-MATE-6423.

DSC-MATE-6423's code has been updated to enable it to use its empathetic and compassionate nature to calm and subdue potential hostile entities. This programming makes it an effective line of defense in operations where highly aggressive and unsociable DSCs are present.
