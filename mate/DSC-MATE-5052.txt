Item #: SCP-5052

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5052 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to any forms of adventurist activities is to be prohibited at all times. Any instances of unauthorized activities conducted by SCP-5052 must be immediately reported to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-5052 is a humanoid entity approximately 1.8 meters in height and of male gender. Its head is covered in a purple metallic material with distinctive orange stripes on its face. The entity frequently accessorizes with a silver chain and can often be found with a lit cigarette.

SCP-5052 is a professional mechanic and has a vast knowledge of mechanics and engineering. It enjoys exploring new possibilities and often takes risks in its personal life. SCP-5052 has survived several near-death experiences, including bungee jumping and getting lost in the wilderness.

Despite being a highly skilled mechanic, SCP-5052 has demonstrated a disregard for its own safety and frequently engages in dangerous activities. SCP-5052 is also known for its reckless behavior, which can have disastrous consequences.

SCP-5052 has displayed resistance to most forms of psychological manipulation and is highly resilient to interrogation.

Addendum: SCP-5052 has expressed an interest in exploring the limits of its physical endurance. These requests are to be denied at all times as it may pose a risk of compromising the security of the Foundation.
