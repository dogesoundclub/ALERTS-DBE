Item #: SCP-1416

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1416 is to be monitored through routine check-ins and any sudden absences are to be reported immediately. SCP-1416 is to wear a standard-issue headset with an LED to enable communication with the foundation. Restricted access to SCP-1416's traffic post is to be enforced in order to avoid unintended disclosure of anomalous activities.

Description: SCP-1416 is a human male with orange stripes on his face. SCP-1416's eyes are blue clown-like eyes that have a tendency to draw the attention of others. The forehead of SCP-1416 has a goggle, a device that serves an unknown function at this time. SCP-1416 wears a red scarf and is often seen smoking a cigarette.

SCP-1416's personality can lead to a forgetful nature, which is why it is essential to keep track of their movements at all times. SCP-1416 has been known to lose focus easily and can become distracted while working on a project, which can lead to humorous situations.

SCP-1416 currently works as a traffic officer, and his expertise in directing traffic has been used to assist in the containment of other SCP anomalies.

SCP-1416's abilities and any possible connections with other SCPs continue to be investigated. All Foundation personnel must take caution when interacting with SCP-1416 in order to avoid any potential complications.
