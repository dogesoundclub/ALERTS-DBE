Item #: 3180

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3180 is to be housed in a standard human living quarters at Site-██. The area is to be monitored by security cameras at all times, and SCP-3180 may be allowed limited interaction with personnel. Any unusual behavior from SCP-3180 should be reported to the site director immediately.

Description: SCP-3180 appears to be a human female, with distinct facial features. Her face contains spotted skin, a broad smile, and blue clown eyes. SCP-3180's head is typically adorned with a retro cap, and her forehead features a two-color band. Her accessories include a choker and a normal gas mask.

SCP-3180 has shown a tendency to approach situations in a logical and analytical manner, demonstrating a high level of competence in problem-solving. However, SCP-3180 has also displayed a quirky sense of humor, often making witty observations or deadpan jokes.

Despite SCP-3180's seemingly harmless demeanor, caution is advised. SCP-3180's possession of a gas mask may indicate involvement in illicit activities or dealings with anomalous entities. It is recommended that personnel do not engage in personal interactions with SCP-3180 without proper authorization.

Addendum: SCP-3180 was discovered in rural ██████, where she maintained a small farm. She was taken into Foundation custody following reports of abnormal livestock behavior and confirmed possession of knowledge regarding ████-███████████ anomalies. Interrogation has revealed significant knowledge regarding SCP entities and their interactions with non-anomalous individuals. SCP-3180's background and current location are being investigated for any further anomalies or anomalies of interest.
