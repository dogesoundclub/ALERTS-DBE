DSC-MATE-1126 also has 90s brown hair and a pair of goggles on their forehead. A gold earring on each ear and a gold chain completes DSC-MATE-1126's ensemble.

DSC-MATE-1126 is a doctor and has been observed to be very introspective, often getting lost in thought. DSC-MATE-1126 is prone to accidents caused by their lack of attention to their surroundings. This information suggests a level of mental instability may be present.

DSC-MATE-1126's anomalous abilities manifest mainly within their medical practice. Patients DSC-MATE-1126 has treated have reported feeling better and even experiencing miraculous recoveries beyond what should be possible with standard medical procedures. The source of this power is currently unknown.
