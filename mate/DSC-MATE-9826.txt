Item #: SCP-9826

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9826 will remain under surveillance at all times to ensure their adherence to Foundation protocols. SCP-9826 is to be assigned to train routes that run through low-population areas to minimize civilian exposure. SCP-9826 is to be outfitted with a standard gas mask for control in case of emergencies.  

Description: SCP-9826 is a male human train driver with unique identifying features. SCP-9826 sports a retro cap and frequently wears a silver chain around their neck. SCP-9826's face is characterized by spots and a constant smile. SCP-9826 is noteworthy for their uninhibited personality, which often leads to comic situations involving silly dance moves or antics.

SCP-9826 has a strong sense of duty and responsibility and fulfills their job with unwavering effort. However, SCP-9826 knows how to have fun and lets loose when appropriate. It's important to note, this behavior doesn’t impact SCP-9826’s employment duties.

SCP-9826’s safety-focused nature and adherence to our protocols makes SCP-9826 a valuable asset to the Foundation. 

Additional Note: SCP-9826's gas mask has been modified with LED lights to improve visibility during emergencies. SCP-9826 was cooperative in modifying their mask for safety purposes.
