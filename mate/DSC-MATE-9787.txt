Item #: 9787

Object Class: Humanoid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 9787 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber, lined with reinforced steel and monitored via surveillance cameras 24/7. Any attempt to remove SCP-9787 from its containment must be approved by at least two Level-4 personnel.

Description: SCP-9787 appears to be a male human of average height and weight, with orange stripes running across its face and green layers covering its head. The most notable feature of SCP-9787 are its blue clown eyes and green cross earring. SCP-9787 is often seen wearing a red scarf and a normal gas mask.

SCP-9787 is employed as a police officer, and its personality reflects its career choice. SCP-9787 possesses a no-nonsense attitude and values tradition. However, there is also a playful side to SCP-9787, often telling jokes and stories from its family or community.

SCP-9787's anomalous properties manifest in its ability to withstand extreme environments and toxins, even without the use of its gas mask. SCP-9787 has exhibited the ability to heal from injuries at a faster rate than a typical human. Testing is ongoing to determine the full extent of its abilities.

Addendum: SCP-9787 has requested permission to participate in a charity event for hospitalized children. Following a thorough risk assessment, permission has been granted for SCP-9787 to participate under close supervision.
