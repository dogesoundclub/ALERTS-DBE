Item #: 546

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 546 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. The subject is to be monitored constantly for any signs of deviation from its baseline behavior. Any deviations are to be reported immediately to the Site Director.

Description: Subject 546 is a female human with distinctive orange stripes on its face. It has VR Jade LED eyes and a not-arranged green head. The subject wears a medical mask to cover its mouth and has a silver chain as an accessory. Its most notable feature is the red cross earring on its left ear.

Subject 546 is highly intelligent and possesses a strong sense of duty and responsibility. It has been observed to actively pursue villainous activities while exhibiting a calm and controlled demeanor. The subject's unique sense of humor and ability to have fun can lead to unexpected humorous situations.

Despite its villainous tendencies, Subject 546 has shown a preference for socializing and being the life of the party. It has the ability to get others to dance and sing along to its favorite songs, making it a charismatic and engaging individual.

Addendum: The subject has displayed an unusual resistance to physical and chemical stimuli, making it highly dangerous in close combat situations. Extreme caution is advised when in the presence of Subject 546.
