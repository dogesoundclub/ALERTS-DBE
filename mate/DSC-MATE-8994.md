When DSC-MATE-8994 is unable to help with a situation, it reflects on them as feelings of failure, often taking the responsibility to fix everything they come across.

DSC-MATE-8994 is highly regarded among the staff members who appreciate its positive attitude and keen observations of activities in the environment.
