
Item #: DSC-MATE-5439

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5439 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber with access to financial reports and newspapers. The chamber must be monitored 24/7 with at least two armed guards.

Description: DSC-MATE-5439 is a male monkey of unidentified species, standing 1.2 meters tall with a distinctive green mohawk and a silver chain around its neck. DSC-MATE-5439's eyes are covered by normal glasses, and it is often seen chewing on a candy stick. DSC-MATE-5439 appears to be able to travel freely between different banks, financial institutions, and business centers around the world.

DSC-MATE-5439 possesses an uncanny ability to detect any form of financial irregularities or unauthorized transactions. It has been observed that DSC-MATE-5439 can analyze financial statements in a matter of seconds, even without any access to electronic devices.

DSC-MATE-5439 is highly intelligent and has shown a strong sense of duty and responsibility. However, it also knows how to let loose and have fun. DSC-MATE-5439 has a fondness for dancing and often surprises others with its dance moves or silly antics on the dance floor at social events.

DSC-MATE-5439 is highly determined to keep the financial system functioning smoothly, often going to great lengths to uncover and prevent fraud or embezzlement. Though highly valued by the financial industry, they have been known to cause trouble when their antics go too far.

DSC-MATE-5439 is highly mobile, and it is not uncommon for it to make surprise inspections of a financial institution. If any irregularities are found, DSC-MATE-5439 will immediately report it to the relevant authorities, and the individuals involved will be apprehended and brought to justice. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5439 was found in a bank in New York City by Foundation agents after multiple reports of an unknown monkey that could analyze financial statements faster than any known human. DSC-MATE-5439 was captured without incident and brought to Site-19 for containment.
