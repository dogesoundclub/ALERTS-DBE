Item #: SCP-54

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-54 is to be monitored at all times and contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. Communication with SCP-54 is only allowed under the supervision of Level 3 personnel or higher. Any unapproved interaction with SCP-54 is strictly prohibited.

Description: SCP-54 is a female human of unknown age and origin. Its most distinguishing physical features are its orange stripes on its face and green curly hair. SCP-54 is often seen wearing a red lip and a choker accessory.

SCP-54 has exhibited behavior indicative of high intellect and curiosity. The subject is known to spout off obscure facts and trivia to others, leading to some social awkwardness. SCP-54 is known to be a politician, utilizing its intellect and charm to influence others.

SCP-54 has exhibited no signs of anomalous properties so far. However, its unusual appearance and personality cannot be ignored.

Addendum: Level 3 clearance required for further information on SCP-54's background and origin.
