Item #: 2408

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2408 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber, with standard amenities provided. All interactions with SCP-2408 must be conducted with caution, as they have a tendency to daydream and may not always be aware of their surroundings. SCP-2408 is to be monitored at all times to ensure their safety and the safety of Foundation staff.

Description: SCP-2408 is a male human of average height and build. They work as a beautician, though they have shown no anomalous abilities in this field. SCP-2408 has spots on their face and a wide smile, which is often seen even when they are not actively amused. Their eyes are blue and have been described as clown-like in appearance, and their head is colored purple metal. They wear a green cross earring, gold chains, and are frequently seen smoking cigarettes.

SCP-2408 has a highly introspective personality and often appears lost in thought. They have been observed walking into poles and tripping over objects due to their preoccupation with their own thoughts. Despite their absent-mindedness, SCP-2408 is cooperative with Foundation staff and has not shown any hostile tendencies.

Addendum: SCP-2408 is not to be used for any experimental purposes that may affect their mental state or ability to perform their job as a beautician. Any testing must be approved by Site Director and monitored by experienced staff at all times.
