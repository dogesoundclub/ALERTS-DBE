Item #: SCP-8098

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8098 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber with additional provisions for accommodating his tiger form. Access to the chamber is to be strictly limited to authorized personnel only. SCP-8098 is to be provided with a regular supply of meat, and any requests for additional reading material are to be approved by Site Director.

Description: SCP-8098 is a male Siberian tiger who works as a delivery driver for [REDACTED] courier service. Despite his intimidating appearance, SCP-8098 displays a range of emotions from extreme agitation to abject fear.

SCP-8098 has normal glasses, a goggle on the forehead, and a gold earring. It wears a gold chain around its neck and a whistle around its neck.

SCP-8098 exhibits a deep curiosity about the world, which can lead to some humorous situations as he randomly spouts off obscure facts or trivia to others. 

SCP-8098 seems to suffer from frequent bouts of anxiety, and displays a frightened mouth whenever it is placed in stressful situations.

SCP-8098 is an efficient delivery driver, but is known to go off course on occasion when something catches his interest. If left unchecked, he may lose track of his assigned tasks altogether in favor of exploring and investigating the world around him. 

SCP-8098 is amicable and non-threatening towards humans and other SCP subjects, and displays an eagerness to please. However, caution must be exercised when working with this subject, as his tiger instincts can be triggered by sudden movements or loud noises.

Addendum: SCP-8098 has requested access to a specialized library containing obscure and unusual topics. Upon approval, this library has been made available to SCP-8098 in accordance with containment protocol.
