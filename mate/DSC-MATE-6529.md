Item #: 6529

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Item 6529 is to be contained within a secure facility with access limited to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any attempts to communicate or interact with item 6529 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel. Any information obtained from item 6529 must be documented and submitted to the Foundation archives.

Description: Item 6529 appears to be a male European rabbit with the physical ability of speech and wearing human clothing. The item is often seen wearing a bucket hat, a pair of horn-rimmed glasses, a jade earring, a gold chain, and carrying a whistle. Although the item appears to possess normal rabbit characteristics, its intellect and communication skills exceed that of its species. Item 6529 has identified itself as a politician and has exhibited knowledge of governmental procedures and policies. 

The item's personality is described as organized and efficient with controlling tendencies. It is known to delegate tasks to others in a matter-of-fact way, often leading to a humorous situation. The item tends to be easily frightened and has exhibited this through its mouth posture.

Addendum: Item 6529 was recovered during a political campaign rally where it was observed speaking to a large crowd. Foundation agents were able to retrieve the item and contain it without incident. Interviews with the item have revealed minimal knowledge of its origin and purpose, leading to further investigation.
