Item #: DSC-MATE-6445

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6445 is currently contained within Site-██ in a standard human containment cell. Physical contact with DSC-MATE-6445 is strictly prohibited, as it poses a potential danger to personnel due to its unusual and unexplained physical features.

Description: DSC-MATE-6445 is a human female who works as a beautician. It possesses orange stripes on its face and has blue clown eyes that appear to be permanently painted on. DSC-MATE-6445 also wears a tiara on its head and has a two-color band on its forehead, which glows intermittently. It further complements its appearance with a choker around its neck.

DSC-MATE-6445's personality has been observed to be highly analytical and logical. It possesses excellent problem-solving skills and can quickly identify flaws in any given situation. DSC-MATE-6445 also has a quirky sense of humor and enjoys making witty observations or teasing people in a dry, deadpan way.

Despite its seemingly harmless exterior, DSC-MATE-6445 has caused concern among Foundation personnel due to its ability to manipulate physical matter. Objects that come into contact with DSC-MATE-6445 will undergo various transformations, ranging from subtle to extreme. For example, DSC-MATE-6445 transformed a pencil into a live rat on one occasion.

DSC-MATE-6445's anomaly appears to be self-aware, and it has demonstrated a determination to keep its abilities hidden from the outside world. It has been known to destroy any evidence that exposes its anomaly, making containment and study of DSC-MATE-6445 a priority of the Foundation.
