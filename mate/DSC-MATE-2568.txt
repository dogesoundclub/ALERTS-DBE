Item #: SCP-2568

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2568 is to be monitored at all times and is to be placed in a standard humanoid containment cell. Any contact with SCP-2568 must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-2568 is allowed one hour of supervised outdoor time per day. During this time, SCP-2568 must wear a specially designed suit to hide its physical anomalies from public view.

Description: SCP-2568 is a female human with orange stripes on its face and has blue clown eyes. SCP-2568 possesses a two color band and goggles over its forehead as well as gold earrings on its ears. SCP-2568 has a choker on its neck at all times. According to reports, SCP-2568 works as a firefighter in the local town.

SCP-2568 has a deep empathy for others and may experience intense emotional outbursts when witnessing someone else's pain or suffering. SCP-2568 has also demonstrated an affinity for animals and nature. During SCP-2568's outdoor time, it has been observed interacting with birds, insects, and plants in a manner that is unusual for a human.

SCP-2568's physical appearance and emotional tendencies suggest that SCP-2568 may have undergone extensive experimentation or augmentation. Further investigations into SCP-2568's background are ongoing. Until conclusive evidence is obtained as to SCP-2568's origins and abilities, SCP-2568 remains classified as an Euclid class anomaly.
