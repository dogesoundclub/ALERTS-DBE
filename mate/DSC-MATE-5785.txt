Item #: SCP-5785

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5785 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber outfitted with surveillance cameras and reinforced restraints. Due to SCP-5785's connections within the criminal underworld, monitoring of communication channels for any mention of SCP-5785's name is to be conducted by Foundation agents assigned to the case. Any attempts at rescue or extraction of SCP-5785 by external parties are to be thwarted by any means necessary.

Description: SCP-5785 is a male monkey of the genus Macaca, standing at 1.5 meters tall and weighing approximately 50 kilograms. SCP-5785 wears a red beanie and shade glasses at all times, with a 2-color band and goggles on its forehead. SCP-5785 possesses a gold earring in its left ear, and typically accessorizes with a red scarf.

SCP-5785 is the leader of a prominent criminal syndicate in [REDACTED]. The extent of SCP-5785's operations is unclear, but it is known to deal in narcotic substances and illegal firearms. SCP-5785 is known for its organizational prowess and efficiency, which have earned it a reputation for being a shrewd and controlling leader.

SCP-5785's personality is described as authoritative and domineering. It is prone to taking charge of any situation and delegating tasks to its subordinates in a direct and matter-of-fact manner. SCP-5785 appears to have no qualms with resorting to violence to achieve its goals and has demonstrated a high level of tactical acumen.

Addendum: [REDACTED] reports indicate that SCP-5785 may have developed an interest in anomalous artifacts as potential tools for its criminal enterprise. Investigations into this matter are ongoing.
