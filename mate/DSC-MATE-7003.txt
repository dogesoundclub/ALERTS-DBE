Item #: SCP-7003

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7003 is to be housed in a private room in Site-██ with standard human accommodations. Personnel are not to make physical contact with SCP-7003 without proper protective measures due to its rebellious nature. SCP-7003 is allowed to smoke only within designated smoking areas and is provided cigarettes upon request.

Description: SCP-7003 is a human female of average height and weight, with orange stripes on her face and purple lips. SCP-7003 wears normal glasses and has a rainbow festival hairstyle. On its forehead are two color bands, and it wears gold earrings and a choker as its accessories. SCP-7003’s most distinguishable accessory is a cigarette.

SCP-7003’s personality is characterized by its rebellious nature, often breaking or bending rules for its amusement. Due to its disregard for authority, it is capable of creating humorous situations. Although SCP-7003’s behavior may be perceived as playful, caution must be exercised to prevent any potential danger.

SCP-7003 claims to be a doctor, although no evidence has been found to support this claim. The Foundation continues to monitor SCP-7003 and attempts to determine any potential anomalies.
