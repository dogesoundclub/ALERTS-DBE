Item #: SCP-3096

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3096 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with reinforced walls and security measures. SCP-3096 is to be sedated at all times when being transported for visits to authorized personnel. Any interactions with SCP-3096 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel.

Description: SCP-3096 is a humanoid individual of female sex and human species. SCP-3096 is the current boss of a criminal organization based in [REDACTED]. SCP-3096 has distinctive purple lips and blue clown eyes. SCP-3096's head is colored in metallic blue, and SCP-3096 often wears a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-3096 has displayed a creative and artistic side that they may express through various hobbies or interests. SCP-3096 enjoys painting, drawing, writing, or other forms of artistic expression. SCP-3096 shows no signs of remorse or guilt for their actions as a Mafia member.

SCP-3096 possesses an unusual charisma that enables them to command respect and loyalty from their associates. SCP-3096's strategies are successful, and their organization has significantly increased in size and influence. SCP-3096 is suspected to have access to mystical and occult forces, although no definitive evidence supports this hypothesis.

SCP-3096 has avoided justice on several occasions, leaving no evidence that could link them to their criminal activities. Investigations into SCP-3096's whereabouts have often resulted in the sudden disappearance of law enforcement officials. Foundation personnel assigned to SCP-3096 are repeatedly warned to maintain vigilant surveillance at all times.

Addendum: Recently, SCP-3096 has expressed interest in collaborating with other criminal organizations worldwide to expand its reach and influence. Foundation personnel should monitor any potential collaborations for signs of anomalous activity.
