Item #: DSC-MATE-7351

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7351 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber. All interactions with DSC-MATE-7351 must be supervised by at least two armed guards. DSC-MATE-7351 is allowed access to cigarettes as part of its reward system.

Description: DSC-MATE-7351 is a human female with blonde hair and red clown eyes. DSC-MATE-7351 possesses spots on its face, and one wing sprouting from its head. DSC-MATE-7351 also wears a jade earring on its left ear. DSC-MATE-7351 has been identified as a veterinarian by public records.

DSC-MATE-7351 has a tendency to forget important appointments and events due to its absent-minded personality. Its forgetfulness is occasionally humorous but must be monitored as it compromises the Foundation's procedures.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-7351 was acquired by the Foundation after reports of a strange creature with wings were received from an animal shelter. DSC-MATE-7351 was observed sedating animals and conducting unauthorized medical procedures, leading to concerns for public safety. After capture, DSC-MATE-7351 was deemed sentient and cooperative towards containment procedures.
