Item #: SCP-6229

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6229 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-███. Daily psychological evaluations are to be conducted to monitor any changes in behavior or thought patterns. Interaction with SCP-6229 is prohibited unless authorized by at least one Level 2 personnel.

Description: SCP-6229 appears to be a human male of average height and build. He has distinctive orange stripes on his face and wears a retro cap on his head. His eyes resemble ski goggles, and he wears a green cross earring on one ear. SCP-6229 is known to sport a wide smile at all times, making him easily recognizable.

SCP-6229's behavior is characterized by an extreme level of attention to detail. He is highly organized and methodical in his daily routine, which includes alphabetizing his spice rack and color-coding his closet. This trait has been observed to manifest in unexpected ways, making SCP-6229 unpredictable in his actions.

SCP-6229 is highly intelligent and has a keen eye for detail. He is a crafty villain, known for his clever schemes and ability to think on his feet. Despite initial impressions, he is incredibly dangerous, and should be approached with extreme caution.

Addendum: SCP-6229 was brought into Foundation custody after reports of a string of elaborate thefts in the city. He has proven to be highly resistant to traditional interrogation techniques, and it is believed that he has information that could be harmful to the Foundation.

Note: SCP-6229 is not to be underestimated. While he may appear harmless, his sharp intellect and meticulous planning make him a force to be reckoned with.
