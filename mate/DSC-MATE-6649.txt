Item #: SCP-6649

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6649 is to be housed within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Access is only permitted to Level 2 personnel and above with prior approval from at least two Level 3 personnel. SCP-6649 is to be provided with basic amenities, including a bed, bathroom, and a variety of art supplies. SCP-6649 is allowed to continue working as a fashion designer, however access to public events is to be restricted to prevent potential breaches of confidentiality.

Description: SCP-6649 is a human female standing at 170 cm tall and weighing 56 kg. SCP-6649 possesses facial features including spots and a smile, as well as orange eyebrows and two color bands across the forehead. SCP-6649 has green hair and wears a purple cap, red cross earrings, and a silver chain. SCP-6649 is often seen wearing an LED gas mask, which it claims is to help protect against pollution when working.

SCP-6649 possesses the ability to connect with others on a deep level, often leading to humorous situations. It has been known to get strangers to open up about their life stories or share funny anecdotes that bring people closer together. SCP-6649 is known for its charisma and is highly respected within the fashion industry.

SCP-6649's fashion designs are highly sought after, with several high-profile celebrities ordering outfits from SCP-6649's brand. SCP-6649 uses a unique combination of colors and fabrics to create outfits that are unlike any others in the industry.

Addendum: SCP-6649 has expressed interest in using its fashion designs as a way to raise awareness of environmental issues. Research into this possible avenue of SCP-6649's containment is currently underway.
