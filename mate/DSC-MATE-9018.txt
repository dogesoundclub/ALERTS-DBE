Item #: SCP-9018

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9018 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber with appropriate amenities for their well-being. Any communication with SCP-9018 must be kept to a minimum, and any sensitive information must be censored before being shared with them.

Description: SCP-9018 is a humanoid entity with distinctive physical features. Their face is decorated with spots, and they have purple lips. Their eyes are red and resemble those of a clown. SCP-9018 has a pair of pig-tails on their head, and a goggle is regularly seen on their forehead. They have a red cross earring on one ear, and a choker around their neck.

SCP-9018 is a human and goes by the name of [REDACTED]. They are employed as a reporter and are well-known for their investigative skills. SCP-9018's laid-back and easy-going demeanour often leads people to underestimate them, but they can be fiercely loyal and protective of their loved ones.

SCP-9018's personality has caused humorous situations, such as getting into a heated argument with someone who insults a friend or family member. While SCP-9018 may seem harmless, caution is advised when interacting with them as they have shown to be capable of protecting their own.

Addendum: SCP-9018 was discovered following an incident in which they uncovered sensitive information related to the Foundation's activities. [REDACTED] was immediately detained and recruited into the Foundation. SCP-9018 has since cooperated with the Foundation, providing valuable intel concerning paranormal activities.
