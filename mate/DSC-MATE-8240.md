Item #: DSC-MATE-8240

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8240 must be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber and provided with basic human necessities. The subject must be monitored at all times for any unusual behavior, especially any attempts to escape containment.

DSC-MATE-8240's job as a reporter has been terminated, and she is not allowed to use any form of media to disseminate any information. Any attempts to do so will result in disciplinary action.

Description: DSC-MATE-8240 is a female human with orange stripes on her face and a perpetual smile. Her blue clown eyes make her appearance unnerving and leads individuals to question her emotional state. DSC-MATE-8240 often wears a hoodie and a silver chain around her neck. Her personality shows a sense of responsibility to her duty while enjoying life.

DSC-MATE-8240 has displayed an unnatural ability to influence individuals' behavior through the use of her favorite songs. Affected individuals become unusually euphoric and may participate in uncharacteristic behavior, such as suddenly dancing in public or joining in on the singing.

DSC-MATE-8240 has also exhibited high-level observation and reporting skills. Her journalistic background may have contributed to this behavior. However, any information disseminated by DSC-MATE-8240 has been deemed unreliable and unverifiable.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-8240 is currently undergoing evaluation for potential recruitment into the Foundation's media department, where her unique abilities may be utilized effectively under direct supervision.
