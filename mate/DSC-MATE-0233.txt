Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-233 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber located within a secure facility. SCP-233 is allowed access to books and other educational materials at the discretion of the overseeing personnel. SCP-233 is to be provided with any necessary personal protective equipment, including a gas mask, when outside of its containment chamber.

Description: SCP-233 is a human female who appears to be in her mid-20s. SCP-233 displays a unique physical attribute with spots located on her face. SCP-233 has orange eyebrow eyes and typically wears a hoodie. A normal gas mask is one of SCP-233's accessories. SCP-233 is a reporter and demonstrates a thirst for knowledge, often spending time reading and researching obscure topics. SCP-233 appears to enjoy collecting books, documentaries, and other educational materials; SCP-233 is passionate about sharing her newfound knowledge with others.

SCP-233 has not displayed any anomalous properties beyond its physical appearance and interests. SCP-233 is cooperative with personnel and has not shown any threats to containment.

Addendum: SCP-233 was brought to the attention of the foundation after a local news agency reported on her unusual physical features. Foundation agents were dispatched to investigate and ultimately brought SCP-233 into custody. SCP-233 willingly agreed to be contained and has provided valuable intelligence on the reporting community. SCP-233 has expressed interest in joining the foundation as a researcher or analyst. The request is pending review by the O5 council.
