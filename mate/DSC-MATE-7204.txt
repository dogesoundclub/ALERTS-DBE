Item #: 7204

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7204 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. Its living environment should resemble a typical law firm office, complete with a desk, chairs, and stacks of law books. Instances of candy sticks are to be provided as a reward for good behavior.

Description: SCP-7204 is a male rabbit appearing wearing a bucket hat and horned-rimmed glasses. Its forehead features a two-color band, and it has one jade earring on its left ear. SCP-7204 carries a candy stick as an accessory, which it is often seen chewing on.

SCP-7204 is capable of human-like communication and is a practicing lawyer. It possesses a rebellious personality, often pushing boundaries and breaking rules for amusement. Despite its behavior, SCP-7204 is highly intelligent and skilled in legal matters. Its persuasive skills are also noteworthy, as SCP-7204 has successfully defended several anomalous entities in court.

SCP-7204's behavior can be unpredictable, and it is advised to treat it with caution. Due to its penchant for bending rules, it is also important to keep a watchful eye on SCP-7204 during its interactions with other anomalous entities.

Addendum: SCP-7204 has formed a friendship with SCP-XXXX, another anomalous entity currently contained on-site. The two can be seen playing card games and engaging in light conversation during their recreational time. Their relationship is currently being monitored for any potential security breaches.
