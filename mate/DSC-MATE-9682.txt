Item #: SCP-9682

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9682 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber equipped with organic materials for their use. Standard rabbit food pellets are to be provided daily, along with fresh vegetables and fruits. SCP-9682's silver chain and whistle are to be placed in secure storage, accessible only with Level 2 clearance.

Description: SCP-9682 is a male rabbit measuring 28 centimeters in height and 35 centimeters in length. Their eyes are a bright blue, featuring a clown-like appearance. SCP-9682's head is adorned with green hair in a purple cap, with a dual-color band on their forehead. One notable feature of SCP-9682 is a jade earring in their left ear.

SCP-9682's personality is characterized by a strong sense of duty and responsibility, sometimes leading to comical results when they take on more than they can handle. SCP-9682 is a skilled mechanic, able to repair and maintain machinery with ease. They are also able to operate heavy machinery such as forklifts and cranes, despite their small size.

SCP-9682 is fond of collecting small trinkets, which they carry on their person using their silver chain. They are rarely seen without their whistle, which they use to call for attention or to indicate completion of a task.

Addendum: SCP-9682 has expressed a desire to assist in the repair and maintenance of Foundation equipment. While this request is under consideration, it is important to note that SCP-9682's containment takes priority over any requests for assistance.
