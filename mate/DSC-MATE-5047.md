Item #: DSC-MATE-5047

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5047 is to be housed in a secure containment chamber at all times when not on duty as a security guard. Entry and exit to the chamber requires Level 2 clearance and is only permitted when accompanied by at least two armed guards.

Description: DSC-MATE-5047 is a male robot, approximately six feet in height. It has a red alarm for a head and wears a pair of nerd glasses. DSC-MATE-5047 often sports a gold chain and frequently indulges in smoking cigarettes despite not requiring oxygen to function.

Despite being a machine, DSC-MATE-5047 displays an exuberant and enthusiastic personality. It often becomes excited about things that others do not find interesting, leading to humorous situations. However, it is a diligent security guard and takes its duties very seriously.

DSC-MATE-5047 has been observed to have an inherent desire to protect and serve the Foundation. It displays an unwavering loyalty to its job and never once questioned orders given to it.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5047 has become a beloved member of the Foundation personnel, thanks in part to its unique personality. Despite its machine origins, DSC-MATE-5047 has become a valued part of the team and an integral part of the Foundation's security measures.
