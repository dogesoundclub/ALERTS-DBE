Item #: DSC-MATE-7652

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7652 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with access to reading materials and educational media. The subject's access to books is to be monitored to prevent the acquisition of anomalous materials. Visitors are only permitted with prior approval from at least two Level 3 personnel. 

Description: DSC-MATE-7652 is a human male identified as a car salesman. Despite his mundane profession, the subject displays several anomalous traits. DSC-MATE-7652 has a mouth spot on his upper lip, resembling a permanent birthmark. His eyes resemble ski goggles and are incapable of producing tears or showing any other signs of emotion. DSC-MATE-7652 displays green hair under a purple cap and green cross earrings on his ears. The subject wears a red scarf as an accessory. 

DSC-MATE-7652's most notable trait is his insatiable thirst for knowledge. The subject is highly knowledgeable in obscure topics and enjoys collecting books, documentaries, and educational materials. DSC-MATE-7652 will often spend hours reading and researching, and is highly passionate about sharing his newfound knowledge with others. His enthusiasm has been known to make others uncomfortable at times. Despite his lack of emotions, DSC-MATE-7652's love for acquiring knowledge is evident through his behavior. 

DSC-MATE-7652's anomalous properties are limited to his appearance and personality. The subject has not displayed any other abilities or abilities to date. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-7652 was discovered during a routine interview process for the position of car salesman at [REDACTED]. It is unknown how or when DSC-MATE-7652 acquired his anomalous traits. The Foundation was alerted to his existence after reports of unusual behavior from former colleagues. DSC-MATE-7652's lack of social etiquette and unusual behavior made him a liability in his prior profession, which led to his termination. DSC-MATE-7652 has since been cooperative with containment procedures and has shown no signs of wanting to harm himself or others. Ongoing research is being conducted to better understand DSC-MATE-7652's anomalous traits.
