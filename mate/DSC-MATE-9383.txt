Item #: SCP-9383

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9383 is to be kept under standard humanoid containment in a secured facility. Access is granted only to personnel with Level 2 clearance or higher. SCP-9383 is to wear his LED gas mask at all times when outside of its cell. Any inappropriate behavior is to be reported to the Site Director for potential reassignment.

Description: SCP-9383 is a male human with blonde hair and striking orange stripes on his face. He has rainbow LED eyes and a gold earring on his left ear. SCP-9383 wears a silver chain around his neck and LED gas mask to filter out hazardous particulates.

Personality traits of SCP-9383 include a strong proclivity towards debate, an analytical and logical approach to discussion, and a tendency to argue for fun. SCP-9383 can get into heated debates on trivial topics, often resulting in humorous situations. In serious or hostile situations, SCP-9383 displays a frightened expression, his mouth trembling with fear.

SCP-9383 works as a security guard for the Foundation, tasked with keeping the Foundation's secrets safe. Despite his quirks, SCP-9383 is a reliable and dedicated employee.

Addendum: SCP-9383 has expressed an interest in debating with other SCPs, which could prove useful in future containment procedures. This request is currently under review.
