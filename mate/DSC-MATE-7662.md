Item #: DSC-MATE-7662

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7662 must maintain a normal lifestyle and is not to be approached by any staff members during live broadcasting. DSC-MATE-7662 also requires a VIP parking slot outside of the Site-19 premises. DSC-MATE-7662 is to be observed through CCTV cameras and must submit daily reports on unusual behaviors or symptoms.

Description: DSC-MATE-7662 is a male tiger with a unique physical appearance. DSC-MATE-7662 has ski goggle-like eyes and a jade earring on each ear. DSC-MATE-7662 is always wearing a silver chain around his neck, which he has refused to take off since containment. DSC-MATE-7662 is locally known as the "golden voice" of the news channel and has become a household name in the city.

DSC-MATE-7662's personality is characterized by being highly observant and attentive to details. DSC-MATE-7662 is an excellent gift-giver but can become overly concerned with giving the "perfect" gift. DSC-MATE-7662's attention to detail can lead to extensive agonizing over finding the ideal gift, which has been observed to frustrate DSC-MATE-7662.

DSC-MATE-7662's anomaly is unknown, but it is assumed to be related to its unique physical appearance. It has been observed that DSC-MATE-7662's eyes can generate a powerful mesmerizing effect when in direct eye-contact with live spectators or viewers. Reports on possible connection between DSC-MATE-7662 and other anomalous beings in the city are currently under investigation.

Addendum: On ██/██/████, DSC-MATE-7662 reported having a dream about being part of a large feline community outside the city limits. DSC-MATE-7662 claimed he had a feeling of not belonging in the city and longed for being part of his own kind. Research teams are communicating with DSC-MATE-7662 to monitor this behavior closely.
