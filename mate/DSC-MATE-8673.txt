Item #: SCP-8673

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8673 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Access to SCP-8673 is limited to personnel with Level 2 clearance or above. Testing on SCP-8673 must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel prior to conducting any experiments.

Description: SCP-8673 is a humanoid entity that appears to be a middle-aged male of European descent. The entity has a parted pink hair and is adorned with a gold earring, a silver chain, and often seen with a cigarette in hand. SCP-8673 identifies itself as a human lawyer.

SCP-8673 presents with a charming and disarming smile, although it may also display a no-nonsense attitude when dealing with legal matters. SCP-8673's playful side is evident in its mannerisms, often telling jokes or sharing stories that have been passed down through their family or community. SCP-8673 highly values traditions.

SCP-8673 has not demonstrated any anomalous properties; however, due to its unusual appearance and behavior, it has been classified as SCP-8673. Further testing will be needed to ascertain the entity's true nature and determine the extent of its capabilities.

Addendum - Interview Log:

Interviewer: Dr. ██████

Interviewee: SCP-8673

<Begin Log>

Dr. ██████: Good morning, SCP-8673. How are you feeling today?

SCP-8673: I'm feeling just fantastic, doctor. How about yourself?

Dr. ██████: I'm doing well, thank you. So, SCP-8673, can you tell me a little bit about yourself?

SCP-8673: Of course! I'm just a humble lawyer, doing my best to uphold the law and the traditions that come with it.

Dr. ██████: What kind of traditions do you value?

SCP-8673: Oh, you know, the usual - respect for one's elders, honesty, hard work. And of course, storytelling! I come from a long line of storytellers, and I love sharing those stories with others.

Dr. ██████: That's interesting. Do you have any stories you'd like to share?

SCP-8673: I certainly do! Would you like to hear about the time my grandfather defended a group of faeries in court?

<End Log>

Closing Statement: Further interviews with SCP-8673 will be needed to determine the extent of its knowledge and abilities. Its attachment to traditional values may prove useful in future containment procedures, but caution is advised when interacting with SCP-8673 as its true nature remains unknown.
