Item #: SCP-9846

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9846 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell equipped with a bookshelf stocked with educational materials to satisfy its thirst for knowledge. Any requests for additional materials must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel. SCP-9846 is to be provided with a jungle gym and other standard monkey amenities. Access to the whistle is strictly prohibited.

Description: SCP-9846 is a male monkey of indeterminate species, approximately 1.5 meters in height when standing. It wears a blue bandana on its head and normal eyeglasses over its eyes. SCP-9846 also wears a gold earring in its right ear, a silver chain around its neck, and carries a whistle. SCP-9846 is highly intelligent and has a thirst for knowledge, often spending hours reading or researching obscure topics. It has been observed collecting books and documentaries, and frequently attempts to share its newfound knowledge with others.

Personality: SCP-9846 is highly curious and enjoys learning new things. It has a particular interest in science and history, and is often seen poring over books on these subjects. SCP-9846 can be sociable and enjoys interacting with its handlers and other researchers, although it can also be mischievous at times. SCP-9846 has been known to use the whistle to summon other primates to assist it in escaping containment.

Addendum: Due to SCP-9846's intelligence and potential capabilities for escape, all personnel are reminded to exercise caution when interacting with this entity. While it may appear harmless, SCP-9846 is still dangerous and should not be underestimated.
