Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7260 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Personnel should avoid direct eye contact with DSC-MATE-7260 at all times. All communication with DSC-MATE-7260 must be conducted through a video recorder to ensure safety.

DSC-MATE-7260's face features a frightened mouth and red clown eyes. Its head is not arranged green and has a goggle on its forehead. It is wearing a jade earring and a gold chain as accessories. DSC-MATE-7260 can often be seen with a cigarette in its hand. 

DSC-MATE-7260 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. Its personality can lead to humorous situations, such as telling funny stories or doing silly impersonations to lighten the mood. Despite its friendly nature, personnel are advised to maintain a cautious attitude when interacting with DSC-MATE-7260. DSC-MATE-7260 has been observed to use its charisma and humorous personality to manipulate its surroundings and escape its containment chamber. 

DSC-MATE-7260's anomalous properties allow it to produce a distorted version of its news broadcasts capable of inducing temporary madness in individuals who view it directly. The effects of DSC-MATE-7260's broadcasts are not permanent, and subjects typically return to their baseline mental state within 24 hours. 

Addendum: DSC-MATE-7260 is a highly trained and experienced news anchor that has gained the trust and admiration of many viewers. The Foundation has determined that the potential risk of DSC-MATE-7260's anomalous broadcasting abilities far outweigh the benefits of its newscasting career. Efforts are ongoing to prevent DSC-MATE-7260 from broadcasting its distorted news.
