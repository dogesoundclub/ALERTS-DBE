Item #: SCP-9685

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9685 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber, with additional security measures taken due to its status as a known Mafia boss. All interactions with SCP-9685 are to be conducted with utmost care, as it has demonstrated high levels of cunning and manipulation.

Description: SCP-9685 is a male rabbit standing at approximately 1.5 meters tall. It is often seen wearing a retro cap and horned rim glasses, as well as a gold earring and chain. SCP-9685 is known to smoke cigarettes regularly.

SCP-9685 possesses the ability to control and coordinate various criminal activities, making it a highly feared and respected figure within the criminal underworld. Despite its occupation, SCP-9685 has demonstrated a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards its subordinates and mission, and will not hesitate to eliminate threats to its organization.

Despite its serious demeanor, SCP-9685 also exhibits a jovial and playful attitude. It is known to enjoy social events, often being the life of the party and convincing others to dance and sing along to its favorite songs.

SCP-9685's motives and goals are currently unknown, and further research is required to fully understand its capabilities and intentions.

Additional Notes: Agents are advised to exercise caution and avoid engaging in any illegal activities proposed by SCP-9685 during interactions. Any breaches of protocol will result in disciplinary action.
