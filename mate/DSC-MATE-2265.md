Item #: DSC-MATE-2265

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2265 is to be contained within a standard human containment cell at Site-██. The subject is to be allowed to continue their career as a real estate agent under close surveillance by Foundation agents posing as potential clients.

Description: DSC-MATE-2265 is a male human with a frightened expression on their mouth. The subject frequently wears a police cap and a jade earring, along with a gold chain and nerd glasses. DSC-MATE-2265 also possesses an LED gas mask, which they claim is a fashion statement.

DSC-MATE-2265 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. This personality trait can lead to humorous situations, but it can also be used to manipulate those around them. DSC-MATE-2265 has been observed utilizing jokes and impersonations to disarm individuals during potential negotiations.

Further investigation into DSC-MATE-2265's background has revealed that they possess a significant amount of knowledge regarding paranormal properties and entities. It is currently unknown how DSC-MATE-2265 acquired this knowledge.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-2265 has recently begun to express interest in selling properties with a history of paranormal activity. Foundation agents are to closely monitor any potential breaches of containment resulting from the sale of such properties.
