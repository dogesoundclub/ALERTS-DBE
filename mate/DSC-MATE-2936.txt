Food intake must be monitored and strictly limited to a diet that primarily consists of vegetables, fruits, and hay. Access to any kind of candy is strictly prohibited, except during testing or as a reward for good behavior.

The rabbit's fur is predominantly white with a 2-color band on its forehead that seems to change color depending on the rabbit's mood. Item 2936 has also been observed wearing a gold earring that is not removable. Eye color is currently unknown, as item 2936 has not opened its eyes since containment.

Item 2936 is known for its charismatic personality and strong ability to connect with others on a deep emotional level. This can lead to humorous situations, such as getting strangers to open up about their life stories or sharing funny anecdotes that bring people closer together. Despite its small size, Item 2936 has proven to be an effective lawyer and advocate for animal rights in court cases.

Addendum: It has been observed that Item 2936 has a strong attachment to candy sticks and will go to great lengths to obtain them. Extreme caution must be taken when offering Item 2936 candy sticks, as it has been known to manipulate individuals into giving them more than they should. It is recommended that only approved personnel are allowed to provide candy sticks to Item 2936.
