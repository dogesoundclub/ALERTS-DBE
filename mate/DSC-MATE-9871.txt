Item #: SCP-9871

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9871 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell, with no access to any form of media or artistic expression. No personnel without Level 3 clearance are allowed to interact with SCP-9871 under any circumstances.

Description: SCP-9871 is a male human of indeterminate age, standing at 5'10" and weighing approximately 170 pounds. SCP-9871 possesses a distinctive spot on his mouth, and often appears to be frightened or anxious. SCP-9871 wears a short peak cap and glasses, along with a red scarf and a candy stick.

SCP-9871 exhibits an extremely emotional nature, with a tendency to be moved to tears by sentimental moments in movies and television shows. SCP-9871 also displays a strong appreciation for music and art that elicits powerful emotions. It is believed that SCP-9871 has a heightened empathic ability, which may contribute to his emotional sensitivity.

SCP-9871 is employed as a car salesman, and has exhibited a talent for persuading potential buyers through his emotional appeals. Testimony from former customers suggests that SCP-9871 is able to create an instant connection with his clients, and has an uncanny ability to anticipate their emotional needs.

SCP-9871 was brought to the Foundation's attention after reports of strange emotional phenomena associated with his car dealership. Witnesses reported feeling intense emotional responses while interacting with SCP-9871 or anywhere on the dealership's premises. Investigation by Foundation agents discovered that SCP-9871 was the origin of these emotional disturbances.

Addendum: Due to SCP-9871's emotional volatility, all personnel are advised to maintain a professional demeanor at all times when in its presence. SCP-9871 is currently undergoing testing to determine the full extent of its empathic abilities.
