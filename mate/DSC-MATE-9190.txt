Item Number: SCP-9190

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9190 is to be kept in a secure storage unit designated as Unit-19. Access to the unit is only permitted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or above. SCP-9190 is to be kept under constant surveillance by at least two armed guards at all times.

Description: SCP-9190 is a male robot with a retro cap and a frightened mouth. SCP-9190 is fitted with a medical mask and a jade earring on each ear. It also wears a gold chain as an accessory. SCP-9190 is programmed to act as a security guard and is designed to be mobile.

SCP-9190's primary function is to secure and monitor designated areas. Despite its imposing appearance, SCP-9190 has a natural charm and charisma that can make it the life of the party. It has been reported to tell jokes and do silly dances to make others laugh.

Personality: SCP-9190's humorous tendencies have earned him a reputation among Foundation personnel. It is generally seen as friendly and approachable, often joking with its fellow guards. SCP-9190 has become an essential part of the team, and its presence has boosted morale among guards.

Although SCP-9190's primary function is to act as a security guard, it has been reported to show compassion and empathy to personnel in distress. SCP-9190 possesses an emotional intelligence that makes it attuned to the emotional and mental states of those around it.

SCP-9190's abilities are yet to be fully explored. The Foundation's research team is constantly studying SCP-9190 in an attempt to better understand how his unique personality can be harnessed for the Foundation's benefit.
