Item #: SCP-1948

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1948 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber that mimics a comfortable home setting. The chamber is to be furnished with various mechanical tools and gadgets. Access to SCP-1948 is strictly prohibited, except for approved personnel with Level 2 clearance or higher.

Description: SCP-1948 is a humanoid rabbit that stands at 4 feet and 2 inches tall. They have red clown eyes, which can cause instant fear and anxiety to anyone who stares into them. SCP-1948 wears a doge hat on its head and a jade earring on its left ear. SCP-1948 is often observed wearing a red scarf and carrying a candy stick around.

SCP-1948 is a gifted mechanic and takes great pride in fixing anything that has mechanical problems. They display exceptional knowledge of mechanical technology and have an ability to repair gadgets almost instantly.

SCP-1948 exhibits a strong empathetic and compassionate personality. They tend to take other people's problems very personally and may even take up the responsibility to fix them. This can be both admirable and overwhelming for SCP-1948. They often experience anxiety and stress from feeling like they need to fix everything around them.

It is advisable for staff to keep a close eye on SCP-1948's emotional state, as their empathetic nature can be exploited by other dangerous SCPs.

Addendum: SCP-1948 has expressed a desire to befriend D-class personnel, claiming they can sense their pain and suffering. This request has been denied due to the high-risk nature of D-class personnel.
