Item #: 1404

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 1404 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell, fitted with a tempered steel door, electrified flooring, and reinforced walls. The LED gas mask accessory must be worn at all times and should not be removed under any circumstances. Any interaction with personnel must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel and is to be conducted under video surveillance.

Description: Subject 1404 is a male tiger humanoid, standing at 6'3" with an athletic build. His physical appearance includes red clown eyes, a red beanie, and a red cross earring. He also wears a gold chain around his neck. The subject's most distinctive feature is the LED gas mask, which emits a constant humming sound and illuminates his surroundings in a green hue. Despite the mask's appearance, there is no gas being emitted.

Subject 1404's personality is highly adaptable, making him an expert car salesman.  He is very observant and attentive to details, which makes him an excellent gift-giver. However, he is also overly concerned with giving the perfect gift and can spend a lot of time agonizing over what to get someone.

In addition to his gift-giving skills, subject 1404 has developed a keen ability to read human emotions and habits. He uses this knowledge to sell his cars to people, making him extremely persuasive. Subject 1404 has demonstrated that he is willing to use his skills to manipulate others to his advantage.

Addendum: Subject 1404 has displayed unpredictable behavior, even for a tiger humanoid. It would be wise to interact with caution and follow all safety protocols when in his presence.
