Item #: SCP-7653

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7653 is to be contained in a standard animal containment unit and guarded by at least two armed personnel at all times. Any attempt to communicate with SCP-7653 should only be done by trained personnel wearing reinforced protective gear. SCP-7653's containment unit should be cleaned and inspected for damages regularly by authorized personnel. Feeding and medical care are to be administered by trained personnel only.

Description: SCP-7653 appears to be a tiger of indeterminate subspecies, fitted with red clown eyes and wearing a green headpiece arranged in a non-standard fashion. The tiger's forehead has two color bands of red and gold and two gold earrings are present on either side of its head. SCP-7653 frequently wears a medical mask on its mouth area and a silver chain around its neck.

SCP-7653 displays a personality that values tradition and discipline with a no-nonsense attitude. Despite this, SCP-7653 has displayed a playful side which manifests itself through jokes and stories passed down through its family or community. SCP-7653 is capable of understanding human language and has displayed a degree of cooperation during medical and feeding procedures.

Addendum: SCP-7653 was discovered residing in a circus in Siberia, Russia. The tiger was recovered by Foundation personnel during an operation to secure anomalous entities in the area. SCP-7653 was contained due to its abilities and potential for causing harm if not properly handled. The origin of SCP-7653's anomalous traits is still under investigation.
