Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1448 is to be contained within a maximum security prison facility for the duration of his natural life. Any and all interaction with SCP-1448 is to be carried out by trained and authorized personnel only. Any unapproved interactions should be reported immediately to on-duty staff.

Description: SCP-1448 is a male human of Italian descent, standing at approximately 185 cm tall and weighing 85 kg. SCP-1448 is the leader of an organized crime syndicate, known as the “Bloody Angels”, operating primarily within the tri-state area. SCP-1448 is known for his aggressive tactics, as well as his tendency to take risks that often put others in danger.

SCP-1448’s most distinctive features include a mouth spot on the left side of his lower lip, a mohawk hairstyle, and a two-color band across his forehead. SCP-1448 is often seen wearing a medical mask over his mouth, a gold chain around his neck, and a pair of gold earrings in both of his ears.

SCP-1448’s personality is characterized by a tendency to act on impulse, often without thinking through the consequences of his actions. This can lead to situations where SCP-1448 puts himself, as well as those around him, in danger. Despite his reckless nature, SCP-1448 is highly intelligent and has a talent for manipulating others to achieve his goals.

Addendum: Incident Report 1448-01: On [REDACTED], SCP-1448 attempted to escape from containment using a makeshift explosive device. SCP personnel were able to contain the situation, and SCP-1448 was placed in solitary confinement as punishment for his actions. It is recommended that all interactions with SCP-1448 be monitored closely, and that he be restrained at all times when outside of his containment cell.
