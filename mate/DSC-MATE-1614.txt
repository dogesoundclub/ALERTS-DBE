Item #: SCP-1614

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1614 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber in Site-██. Access to SCP-1614 is only permitted with Level 2 clearance and must be escorted by at least two armed guards at all times.

SCP-1614 is permitted to continue their profession as a fashion designer under Foundation supervision. Any creations made by SCP-1614 must be inspected before being released for public use.

Description: SCP-1614 is a human male of average height and build. The most notable feature is the orange stripes that run down the face and neck. SCP-1614's eyes are replaced with VR Rainbow LED, which appears functional and acts as a normal pair of eyes. The subject's hair is parted pink, styled in a voluminous manner. SCP-1614 also wears a green cross earring on their right ear, a gold chain around their neck, and a normal gas mask.

SCP-1614 has a strong sense of responsibility and duty, which contrasts with their ability to have fun. This often results in humorous situations where SCP-1614 becomes the life of the party, engaging in dancing or singing with others.

SCP-1614 has not displayed any anomalous effects on individuals around them. However, the subject has shown to have an exceptional proficiency in fashion design, often creating unique pieces that surpass current fashion trends.

Addendum: SCP-1614 has expressed a desire to design specialized clothing for other SCPs. This request is currently under review.
