DSC-MATE-7465 - The Panicked Primate Delivery Driver

It also carries a whistle as an accessory, which it frequently uses to signal nearby personnel.

However, this behavior can also result in DSC-MATE-7465 failing to complete its delivery tasks on time or forgetting important appointments. DSC-MATE-7465 is easily stressed and fearful, which is manifested by its frightened mouth expression and its tendency to use its whistle when feeling threatened.

Biography: DSC-MATE-7465 was discovered wandering aimlessly in a residential area in [REDACTED]. Its owner, who was identified as a traveling circus performer, reported its disappearance a day later. Agents were dispatched to retrieve DSC-MATE-7465 and discovered that it had somehow acquired the ability to speak fluent English. Further analysis revealed that DSC-MATE-7465's brain structure had been altered by an unknown anomaly, resulting in its intelligence and language learning capabilities.

Despite its forgetful nature, DSC-MATE-7465 remains dedicated to its job and is always willing to learn new things.
