Item #: 5985

Object Class: [[Euclid]]

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 5985 is to be monitored at all times by Foundation personnel. Any public appearance must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel. The subject is to be outfitted with a GPS tracking device when outside of Foundation facilities. In the event that the subject is spotted by a civilian outside of a Foundation-approved appearance, witnesses are to be given Class-A amnestics and a cover story is to be established.

Description: DSC-MATE-5985 is a male tiger of unknown origin. Despite their appearance, the subject is capable of speech and possesses a highly developed intellect. DSC-MATE-5985 frequently wears a purple cap with a headset, normal glasses, and a gold earring. The subject also displays a silver chain and is frequently seen carrying a candy stick.

DSC-MATE-5985 is a reporter for an unknown news outlet. The subject's emotional sensitivity has contributed to their success as a journalist; they are often moved to tears by sentimental moments in movies or TV shows and have a fondness for music and art that evokes strong feelings. However, the subject's outgoing nature and desire to connect with others can potentially jeopardize Foundation containment procedures.

DSC-MATE-5985's tiger physiology has granted them increased strength and agility, making them a potential danger should containment be breached. Despite their amicable personality, the subject has shown the potential for violent outbursts in certain situations.

Interviewer: Dr. Patton

Subject: DSC-MATE-5985

DSC-MATE-5985: (laughs) Maybe a little. But really, it's all about being in the right place at the right time, you know? And having a good nose for a story.

Dr. Patton: Thank you for your time, DSC-MATE-5985.

DSC-MATE-5985: Anytime, doc. Always happy to chat.
