Item #: SCP-3541

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3541 is to be monitored at all times by at least two armed security personnel. SCP-3541's political activities are to be tracked and monitored by Foundation intelligence. Physical contact with SCP-3541 is restricted to authorized personnel only. Any requests for access to SCP-3541 must be submitted in writing and approved by at least Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-3541 is a Caucasian male politician in his mid-50s, standing at approximately 6 feet tall. SCP-3541's most notable feature is the presence of orange stripes on the sides of his face. SCP-3541's eyes are blue with enlarged pupils resembling that of a clown. SCP-3541 is often seen wearing a short peak cap and a medical mask, and sports a gold earring in his right ear. SCP-3541 also wears a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-3541's personality is characterized by an abnormally great sense of humor, often using it as a tool to disarm others or diffuse tense situations. SCP-3541 has been known to tell jokes and funny stories, and may also do impersonations or mock performances. It is believed that SCP-3541 achieves psychological satisfaction from making others laugh.

Addendum: Given SCP-3541's position as a prominent politician, it is believed that he possesses significant influence and potential danger to Foundation interests. Further investigation into SCP-3541's background is ongoing.
