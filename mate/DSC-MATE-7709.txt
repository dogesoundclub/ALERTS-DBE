SCP Foundation Description:

Item #: SCP-7709

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7709 is to be housed in a standard containment chamber designed for primate species, and provided with a diet of fruits and vegetables. Access to SCP-7709 is limited to authorized personnel only. Any interaction with SCP-7709 must be overseen by a Foundation security detail.

Description: SCP-7709 is a male monkey of the Cebus genus, measuring approximately 30 cm in height and weighing 5 kg. SCP-7709 has a distinctive appearance, wearing a blue bandana around its head, a pair of normal glasses, and a jade earring in its left ear. SCP-7709 also wears a gold chain around its neck and often carries a candy stick.

SCP-7709 exhibits a friendly and outgoing personality, valuing social connections and harmony among its peers. SCP-7709 is often seen engaging in playful banter and telling jokes, allowing it to form positive relationships with others quickly. However, SCP-7709's behavior can also be erratic at times, leading to unpredictable outcomes during interactions with others.

SCP-7709 currently works as a security guard for the Foundation, responsible for monitoring access to critical areas and ensuring the safety of Foundation personnel. Despite its playful demeanor, SCP-7709 takes its role as a security guard seriously, and is often praised for its vigilance and dedication to the job.

Addendum: SCP-7709 has displayed an unusual affinity for solving puzzles and completing tasks that require critical thinking. This skill has been noted to be above average for a primate of SCP-7709's species, indicating possible anomalous properties. Further study is required to determine the extent of SCP-7709's abilities.
