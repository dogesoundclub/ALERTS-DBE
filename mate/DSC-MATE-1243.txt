Item #: SCP-1243

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1243 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██, with all communication monitored and strictly limited. Personnel are advised to remain at a safe distance from SCP-1243. It is crucial to maintain strict surveillance to prevent any unauthorized communication with SCP-1243.

Description: SCP-1243 is a 5 foot 6 inch human female, who appears to be in her mid-thirties, however its origins are unknown. SCP-1243's face features orange stripes and red clown eyes, with a black hoodie covering its head. SCP-1243 wears a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-1243 is known to be a prominent Mafia Boss in the criminal underworld with a great sense of humor. SCP-1243 enjoys making others laugh, and frequently engages in humor to lighten the mood. SCP-1243's jokes have been known to lead to humorous situations, often involving telling funny stories or doing silly impersonations. However, SCP-1243's humor can be used as a tool for manipulation and control.

It has been reported that individuals who engage in conversation with SCP-1243 are often left with a long-lasting euphoric feeling, which can transcend the conversation with SCP-1243. Nonetheless, it is advised that interaction with SCP-1243 be avoided, as it is unknown what additional abilities or anomalies SCP-1243 might possess.

Addendum: SCP-1243's whereabouts were unknown until it was discovered in the city center of ██,███, where it has been reported originally from. Investigation into the origins of SCP-1243 is ongoing.
