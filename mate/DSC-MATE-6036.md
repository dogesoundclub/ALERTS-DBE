Item #: DSC-MATE-6036

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6036 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Due to its political position, it is to have limited interaction with the general public and is to be accompanied by security officers at all times. Any requests made by DSC-MATE-6036 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-6036 is a male tiger with unusual facial features. Its mouth appears to be in a constant state of fear, and its eyes resemble those of a clown, blue in color and with overly exaggerated pupils. DSC-MATE-6036 also possesses a 2-color band across its forehead and a gold earring in each ear.

Additionally, DSC-MATE-6036 is regularly seen wearing a medical mask and a red scarf. Its fur is well-groomed, and it appears to take great pride in its appearance.

DSC-MATE-6036 is a politician in its home country and displays a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards its role. However, this often leads to it taking on more responsibilities than it can handle, resulting in various humorous situations. Despite its position, DSC-MATE-6036 has exhibited a cooperative attitude towards Foundation personnel and has complied with all containment procedures.

Addendum: On ██/██/████, DSC-MATE-6036 requested to speak to Foundation personnel about a potential threat to its life. Although no evidence of such a threat was found, the request was granted and a Level 4 personnel was sent to meet with DSC-MATE-6036. The meeting concluded with DSC-MATE-6036 expressing its appreciation for the Foundation's assistance and its willingness to continue cooperation.
