Item #: SCP-7176

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7176 is to not be removed from their current job as a fast-food worker. Any attempts to relocate at the request of the subject are to be denied. Regular psychological evaluations are to be conducted to confirm continued contentment within their occupation.

Description: SCP-7176 is a 25-year-old human female with red lipstick and normal glasses, typically seen wearing a metal blue headband with two-color bands and goggles on her forehead. SCP-7176 has green cross earrings and a choker around her neck, which are believed to have no anomalous properties.

SCP-7176's personality is generally idealistic, with a strong desire to make the world a better place. They may engage in humorous situations such as protesting against corporations or causes that they perceive to go against their values.

SCP-7176's anomalous properties manifest in their ability to maintain contentment with their current employment as a fast-food worker, despite having a higher education and skill level. Attempts to offer or relocate SCP-7176 to a more prestigious job have resulted in rejection by SCP-7176.

Addendum: SCP-7176 has voiced their desire to join the Foundation as a researcher to help make the world a better place. Request denied at this time.
