Item #: DSC-MATE-7976

As a train driver, DSC-MATE-7976's employment is to be maintained by the Foundation in order to keep tabs on his activities. DSC-MATE-7976's car, household objects, and plants are to be monitored for any anomalous properties.

DSC-MATE-7976 has a large mouth spot that extends from the corner of his lips to his left cheek. He wears normal glasses over his brown eyes and a blue bandana on his head. DSC-MATE-7976 also sports a jade earring in his right ear and a gold chain around his neck. He is often seen with a candy stick in his mouth.

DSC-MATE-7976's most notable trait is his nurturing personality. He tends to care for his belongings and frequently anthropomorphizes inanimate objects around him. For example, he may give his car a name, talk to his plants, or assign personalities to his household appliances. It is currently unknown whether these behaviors are indicative of any anomalous properties.

DSC-MATE-7976 was discovered by the Foundation when several train passengers reported strange occurrences during their trips. Interviews with the passengers indicated that DSC-MATE-7976 was present during each event. Further investigation revealed that DSC-MATE-7976 was influencing these anomalies with his unique personality.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-7976 has expressed a desire to name his assigned train "Thomas." This request has been approved by the Site Director. Any anomalous activity surrounding DSC-MATE-7976's assigned train is to be reported immediately.
