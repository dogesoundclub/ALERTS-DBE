Item #: SCP-3900

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3900 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Due to their profession, SCP-3900 is permitted to have access to electrical equipment under supervision. SCP-3900's requests for cigarettes are to be granted in moderation. Physical and psychological evaluations are to be conducted bi-annually.

Description: SCP-3900 is a human female, identified as an electrician named [REDACTED]. SCP-3900 stands 167cm tall and weighs approximately 61kg. SCP-3900 has spots on their face and a noticeable smile. Their eyes are a unique shade of red, resembling clown makeup. SCP-3900's head is covered by green hair in a purple cap. They wear goggles on their forehead and a red cross earring in their left ear. SCP-3900 wears a silver chain around their neck and carries cigarettes on their person.

SCP-3900's most notable feature is their strong and decisive personality. They can come across as bossy or intimidating but claim that their assertiveness is solely to ensure efficiency and effectiveness. SCP-3900 is highly skilled in various fields of electrical work and often requests to assist in repairing equipment in the facility.

SCP-3900 has displayed no anomalous properties or behavior thus far. Further investigation is ongoing.

Addendum: SCP-3900 has expressed a desire to assist in creating and designing equipment for the Foundation. Request denied for safety reasons.
