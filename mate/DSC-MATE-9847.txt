Item Number: SCP-9847

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9847 is to be employed as a delivery driver for Foundation personnel only. SCP-9847 is to be allowed contact only with others possessing Level 2 clearance or higher. SCP-9847 is not to be granted access to any confidential documents or sensitive areas.

Description: SCP-9847 appears to be a male human, measuring 1.8 meters in height and weighing approximately 86 kilograms. SCP-9847's most notable feature is his choice in attire; he wears a purple metal helmet with gold earring and nerd glasses over his eyes. He also dons a silver chain and is frequently seen smoking cigarettes.

SCP-9847's personality is best described as energetic and enthusiastic. He enjoys rallying others into his passions, some of which are not well perceived as interesting by his audience. His enthusiastic nature has occasionally led to clashes with Foundation personnel.

SCP-9847's anomalous capabilities are not known at this time, and more research is needed. However, preliminary investigations have found no abnormalities other than his unconventional appearance and extroverted personality.

Addendum: Dr. ███████ has requested that SCP-9847 be kept under careful observation due to his interest in connecting with the paranormal. Until further research is conducted, SCP-9847's status will remain Safe.
