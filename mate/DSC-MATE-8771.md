Item #: DSC-MATE-8771

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8771 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times, with access to food and water as well as personal hygiene products. DSC-MATE-8771 is permitted to leave its containment cell for the purposes of performing deliveries, provided that it is accompanied by no fewer than two armed security personnel at all times. DSC-MATE-8771 is to be monitored at all times, including during any instances in which it interacts with non-foundation personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-8771 is a male rabbit identified as a delivery driver. DSC-MATE-8771 possesses blue clown eyes, which seem to be a natural part of its anatomy. Its head is adorned with a headset connected to an LED system, which seems to be a critical component in its ability to communicate with others. Two unique features are present on its forehead: a red victory band and a goggle.

DSC-MATE-8771 typically dons two accessories: a silver chain and an LED gas mask. These accessories seem to be mostly for show, but it cannot be established definitively. DSC-MATE-8771's personality is characterized by its exceptional charisma, which enables it to interact with others on a deep level, unraveling their emotions and feelings in the process.

DSC-MATE-8771 also has a remarkable sense of humor, and its amusing anecdotes can bring people closer together. DSC-MATE-8771's ability to connect with others emotionally and intellectually, coupled with its physical quirks, has made it one of our most intriguing subjects at the Foundation.
