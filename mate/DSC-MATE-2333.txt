Item #: SCP-2333

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2333 is to be kept in standard humanoid containment quarters at Site-19. Food is to be provided regularly in accordance with the dietary needs of a Bengal Tiger. Exercise opportunities are to be made available at least once every 24 hours.

SCP-2333 is to be permitted to leave containment quarters for periods of no longer than eight hours to perform their duties as a Traffic Officer for the local Municipal Police Department. SCP-2333 must be escorted by at least two members of Foundation security personnel during such excursions.

SCP-2333 is also to be monitored at all times while performing their duties to ensure the safety of all individuals in the vicinity.

Description: SCP-2333 is a male Bengal Tiger, measuring approximately 2.5 meters in length and weighing around 180 kilograms. SCP-2333 has a unique appearance, sporting a red victory band on its forehead, a red scarf as an accessory, and a hoodie covering its head.

SCP-2333 has a strong and decisive personality, characterized by a desire to ensure that things are done efficiently and effectively. SCP-2333's drive for efficiency can manifest in a manner that may appear bossy or intimidating.

SCP-2333 performs the duties of a Traffic Officer for the local Municipal Police Department, wearing a standard-issue uniform and badge.

Addendum: SCP-2333 was brought to the Foundation's attention after reported sightings of a tiger performing traffic duties in the city. Once secured, SCP-2333's exceptional behavior made it clear that they were a highly intelligent creature well suited for observing and managing human activity.

SCP-2333 has demonstrated remarkable abilities in human language understanding and following protocol, and their services enhance Foundation control within the urban environment.
