Item #: SCP-2403

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2403 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times, with no access to any communication devices or outside media. All communication with SCP-2403 must be done through designated personnel, as SCP-2403 has shown a dangerous ability to manipulate emotions and thoughts of others.

Description: SCP-2403 is a male human identified as a real estate agent, standing at 6 feet tall with blue clown eyes and a two-color band on their forehead. They wear a single jade earring and a silver chain, as well as an LED gas mask.

SCP-2403 possesses a strong sense of empathy and is highly attuned to the emotions of those around them. They have been observed manipulating the emotions of others, often causing individuals to feel better or see the humor in a difficult situation. SCP-2403 has also displayed the ability to detect and manipulate the thoughts of others, making them a potential danger to Foundation personnel.

Addendum 2403-A: Incident Report

On ██/██/20██, SCP-2403's containment chamber was breached by an unknown individual. SCP-2403 was able to manipulate the emotions and thoughts of the intruder, causing them to release SCP-2403 and flee the site. No further information on the identity of the intruder has been discovered.

Further research into SCP-2403's abilities and potential uses is ongoing.
