Item #: SCP-7827

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7827 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. SCP-7827 is allowed access to veterinary equipment and tools, as per his job duties. SCP-7827 is not to be allowed to interact with any anomalous or hostile entities unless authorized by at least two Level 3 personnel or higher.

Description: SCP-7827 is a male human of African descent, standing at approximately 5 feet 10 inches tall. SCP-7827's most notable feature is the presence of multiple spots on his skin, giving him a distinctive appearance. SCP-7827 wears a gold earring in his left ear and a silver chain around his neck. SCP-7827 displays a frightened expression on his face, with a mouth that is often agape or trembling.

SCP-7827 works as a veterinarian and is known to have a playful and goofy side. Despite this, he values harmony and social connections and is known to engage in humorous situations, such as telling jokes or engaging in playful banter with his colleagues. SCP-7827 has displayed no anomalous abilities or tendencies.

Addendum: SCP-7827's veterinary skills have proven useful in certain situations and are to be utilized by the Foundation as necessary. SCP-7827 is to be monitored for any potential changes in behavior or abilities.
