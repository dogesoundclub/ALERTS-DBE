Item #: 9946

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedure: SCP-9946 is to be kept in a standard containment chamber at Site-██. It is to be provided with regular food and medical care. SCP-9946 is permitted to continue its work as a beautician as it complies with Foundation protocols. Under no circumstances is SCP-9946 to be allowed to leave Site-██ without prior approval from at least two level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-9946 is a male rabbit of average height, weighing 2.5 kg. It resembles a common European rabbit (Oryctolagus cuniculus). SCP-9946 has a frightened expression on its face, and its mouth is always slightly agape. It wears a pair of normal glasses over its eyes and a red scarf around its neck. Its forehead has a two-color band.

SCP-9946's anomalous properties become apparent when it is provided with a gas mask with an LED display. When SCP-9946 wears the mask, it gains the ability to communicate verbally. SCP-9946 expresses a deep curiosity about the world and an interest in obscure or unusual topics. It has been observed randomly spouting off obscure facts or trivia to others.

Addendum: SCP-9946 is an excellent beautician and is highly proficient in hairstyling and makeup. Its talents have been utilized by Foundation personnel for undercover operations. SCP-9946's love for learning is to be encouraged to help maintain its mental health. SCP-9946 is not to be used for experimentation, as it presents no current threat to Foundation security.
