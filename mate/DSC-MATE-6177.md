Item #: DSC-MATE-6177

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-6177 is currently kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Personnel are strictly prohibited from providing any unauthorized items or food items to DSC-MATE-6177. Any interaction with DSC-MATE-6177 is only allowed for research purposes and must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel or higher.

Description: DSC-MATE-6177 is a talking monkey which identifies itself as a "Doctor". It is easily recognizable due to its red beanie wearing long silver chain and a candy stick. DSC-MATE-6177 communicates in English and other known human languages fluently.

DSC-MATE-6177 is very caring and nurturing, often seen taking care of other DSCs, sometimes even playing 'doctor' for entertainment. It shows a particularly strong attachment to DSC-███ and has often been seen taking care of it. Its nurturing personality is particularly affectionate towards children, even going to great lengths to care for them as if they were its own offspring.

The exact origins of DSC-MATE-6177 are currently unknown. However, through various conversations and studies of its behavior, it is believed that DSC-MATE-6177 may have been a product of an anomalous event.

Addendum 6177-1: Encounter with Dr. Wondertainment

On ██/██/20██, DSC-MATE-6177 was found wandering outside Site-██, holding a Dr. Wondertainment brand box. The box contained a Dr. Wondertainment branded toy, which had been severely damaged from use. When questioned about its intentions with the toy, DSC-MATE-6177 became agitated and refused to answer further questioning. It was later discovered that DSC-MATE-6177 had taken care of several children who were also in the possession of Dr. Wondertainment toys. DSC-MATE-6177 was reprimanded and reminded that it cannot provide items from outside sources to other DSCs without permission.
