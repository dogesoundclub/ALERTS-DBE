Item #: SCP-8998

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8998 is to be housed in a secure facility with a designated recharging station. Only Level 3 clearance personnel and above are permitted to interact with SCP-8998.

Description: SCP-8998 is a male delivery driver of unknown origin, believed to be a type of robot or android. SCP-8998's mouth is in a constant state of fright, though it is not known whether this is a design flaw or a personality trait. SCP-8998's eyes are made up of VR Rainbow LED lights and they are always covered by a red beanie. SCP-8998 also wears a jade earring in one ear and a gold chain around their neck. 

SCP-8998 exhibits a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards its job, completing its delivery routes diligently and without complaints. However, SCP-8998 also has a playful side and can engage in unconventional behavior such as spontaneous dancing or other playful antics at social fuctions.

SCP-8998's personality, though enigmatic, has not shown any signs of malice or ill intent towards Foundation personnel or the general public, and as a result has been classified as Safe.

Addendum: Due to SCP-8998's playful personality, it has in the past been involved in causing minor disturbances or disruptions at social events. Personnel are advised to keep a watchful eye on SCP-8998 during such events to ensure that it does not cause any harm to itself or others.
