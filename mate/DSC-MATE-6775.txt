Item #: 6775

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6775 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Communication with SCP-6775 is to be limited to approved personnel only.

Description: SCP-6775 is a human male, identified as a farmer prior to containment. SCP-6775 displays an abnormal physical appearance, including orange stripes decorating his face, a clown mouth, red clown eyes, and green layers on the top of his head. SCP-6775 also wears a medical mask and goggles on his forehead.

SCP-6775 displays a deep empathy and understanding for others, often becoming emotional when exposed to pain or suffering. Additionally, SCP-6775 has a particular fondness for animals and nature. SCP-6775 has not shown any signs of aggression or hostility towards Foundation personnel.

Addendum: SCP-6775 was recovered by Foundation agents after multiple reports of a "clown-faced farmer" allegedly healing animals through an unknown force. SCP-6775 has since been placed under Foundation custody, and his anomalous properties are currently under investigation.
