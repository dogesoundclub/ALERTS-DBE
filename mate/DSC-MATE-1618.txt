Item #: SCP-1618

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1618 is to be allowed to carry out their routine work as a train driver without any interference. They are to be monitored only for the purposes of record-keeping, with any deviations from their normal schedule to be reported to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-1618 appears to be a female human of indeterminate age, although they claim to be in their mid-thirties. They have spots on their face, and their lips are purple in color. Their hair is not arranged green, and they wear a green cross earring in each ear. They also wear a silver chain around their neck and smoke cigarettes regularly.

SCP-1618 is highly organized and likes to plan ahead, but they also have a spontaneous side that can manifest in surprising ways. They often surprise their friends with impromptu adventures or last-minute party planning that turns out to be a hit.

Despite their quirky personality, SCP-1618 is a highly skilled train driver who takes their job very seriously. They are punctual and efficient, and take pride in ensuring the safety of their passengers.

SCP-1618 has not exhibited any anomalous behavior beyond their unusual appearance and personality, and as such, has been classified as a Safe object.
