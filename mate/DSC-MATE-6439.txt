Item #: SCP-6439

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6439 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Any communication with SCP-6439 must be monitored and recorded for analysis. Personnel are not permitted to divulge personal information or make any promises to SCP-6439.

Description: SCP-6439 is a human male identified as a Politician. SCP-6439's eyes resemble those of a clown, with red sclera and pupils. SCP-6439 wears a purple cap with a headset, with a red victory band on his forehead. SCP-6439 also wears a gold earring and carries a candy stick.

SCP-6439 has demonstrated a unique ability to sway emotions of those around him through his speeches and debates. Those exposed to SCP-6439 often experience intense emotions, ranging from euphoria to depression.

SCP-6439 is highly sensitive to emotional stimuli and is often moved to tears by sentimental moments in movies or TV shows. SCP-6439 has a fondness for music or art that evokes strong feelings.

Interview Log:

Interviewer: Dr. ██████
Interviewee: SCP-6439

[BEGIN LOG]

Dr. ██████: Good morning, SCP-6439.

SCP-6439: Good morning, Doctor.

Dr. ██████: Can you tell me about your abilities and how they work?

SCP-6439: My abilities allow me to connect with people on an emotional level. I use my words and charisma to influence their emotions and thoughts.

Dr. ██████: Do you have any control over when and how you use your abilities?

SCP-6439: Not entirely. Sometimes my emotions get in the way, and I can't control how it affects others.

Dr. ██████: How do you feel about being contained by the Foundation?

SCP-6439: I understand the need for containment; I have a responsibility to use my abilities for the greater good.

Dr. ██████: Thank you for your time, SCP-6439. We will continue to monitor your abilities and behavior.

[END LOG]

Addendum: Due to the potential threat to containment, further research and testing of SCP-6439's abilities have been suspended indefinitely.
