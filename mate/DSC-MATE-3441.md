Item #: DSC-MATE-3441

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3441 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell. The cell should contain no less than three airlocks and be constructed of steel-reinforced concrete. DSC-MATE-3441 is to be provided with a supply of raw meat and water at regular intervals. No personnel are to interact with DSC-MATE-3441 outside of pre-approved testing procedures.

Description: DSC-MATE-3441 is a male tiger specimen with a height of 6'2" and a weight of 430lb. DSC-MATE-3441 displays anxiety and fear through its expression and body language. The creature is capable of speaking human language and has a great sense of humor, often making playful jests and doing impersonations to lighten the mood. DSC-MATE-3441 expresses a desire to help Foundation personnel, particularly with mechanics.

DSC-MATE-3441 is unique due to its jade LED eyes and red beanie, as well as the jade earring on its left ear. Additionally, it wears a silver chain and keeps a whistle with it at all times. DSC-MATE-3441 displays an extreme level of proficiency with machinery and has repaired numerous machines within Foundation facilities.

Personality: DSC-MATE-3441 is cooperative, loyal and eager to please personnel. It shows an inclination towards humor and has been known to share amusing anecdotes and jokes with researchers. However, despite its friendly demeanor, DSC-MATE-3441 can become aggressive when provoked or threatened. DSC-MATE-3441 has also demonstrated a reluctance to discuss its past and origins.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-3441 came to the attention of the Foundation following reports of a tiger wearing a red beanie performing feats of mechanical repair in a repair shop in ███████, United States. Authorities were alerted to the presence of DSC-MATE-3441 following tampering with the security cameras on-site. The creature was subdued by the authorities and transfered to Foundation custody. Further investigaion is ongoing.
