Item#: 6935

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6935 must be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. All items worn by SCP-6935 must be kept separate from other containment items.

Description: SCP-6935 is a human female with distinctive facial features including a purple lip and blue clown eyes. SCP-6935 also wears a retro cap with a 2 color band and goggles on their forehead. A medical mask is worn over their mouth.

SCP-6935 works as a beautician and has a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards their job. However, SCP-6935's personality also includes a playful and humorous side which they express through dance moves or silly antics on the dance floor at social events.

SCP-6935's abilities are not fully understood but it is theorized that their unique appearance may have resulted from exposure to an unknown anomaly. Further research is needed in order to fully understand the extent of SCP-6935's abilities.

SCP-6935 has displayed a willingness to cooperate with Foundation personnel and has agreed to participate in research in order to better understand their abilities.

Addendum: SCP-6935 has expressed a desire to have a dance party with Foundation staff. While this request was denied due to concerns of potential containment breaches, it is noted that SCP-6935's playful nature can be used as a means of positive reinforcement.
