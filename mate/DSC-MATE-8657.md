Item #: DSC-MATE-8657

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8657 is to be kept in standard humanoid containment cell within Site-██. The entity is permitted to maintain its occupation as a news anchor, but any public appearances must be pre-approved by Foundation personnel. When speaking in public, DSC-MATE-8657 is to wear a surgical mask and sunglasses to obscure its facial features.

Description: DSC-MATE-8657 is a female humanoid with distinctive facial features. Its skin is covered in spots, and its lips are a shade of purple not found in humans. Its eyes resemble those of a clown, blue and far larger than normal human eyes. DSC-MATE-8657 has green hair, which it wears in a purple cap. Betwixt its eyebrows is a single, round goggle.

DSC-MATE-8657's most notable accessory is the jade earring it wears on its left ear. It also has a silver chain around its neck and habitually smokes cigarettes.

Personality-wise, DSC-MATE-8657 is very outgoing and enjoys being the center of attention. Its humor can be infectious and has been known to cause entire newsrooms to erupt into laughter. Despite this, DSC-MATE-8657 displays introspective traits when it believes it is not being watched.

DSC-MATE-8657 was discovered by the Foundation when its broadcast network reported it missing for several hours during the evening news. It reappeared on screen, but with its facial features drastically altered. Due to its status as a prominent public figure, the Foundation took immediate action to contain the entity.

Debriefing of DSC-MATE-8657 revealed that it, and individuals with similar alterations, were a result of exposure to [REDACTED]. Individuals exposed to the anomalous event displayed largely cosmetic traits, save for psychological effects.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-8657 is to be supplied with a carton of cigarettes every week as a means of psychological maintenance. Cigarettes must be rationed as to minimize the risk of addiction.
