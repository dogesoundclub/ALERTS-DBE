SCP Foundation Description:

Item #: SCP-9365

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9365 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber with access to a minimum of one hour exterior exercise per day supervised by at least one physical containment specialist. SCP-9365 is to be provided with a diet of fresh vegetables and fruits. Any form of physical harm or experimentation to SCP-9365 must be approved by at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-9365 is a male rabbit of the species Lepus europaeus. SCP-9365 displays a frightened expression in its mouth and has jade LED eyes that display a virtual reality window. SCP-9365's head features green fur that is not arranged. When not in use, SCP-9365 is often seen wearing a silver chain.

SCP-9365 possesses a unique personality, characterized by its tendency to value tradition and approach situations with a no-nonsense attitude. However, SCP-9365 also exhibits a playful side and often engages in humorous situations by telling stories or jokes passed down through its family or community. SCP-9365's comedic persona has received attention from Foundation personnel, often providing positive morale during stressful situations.

SCP-9365 previously worked as a reporter for a local news agency, but was later detained by the Foundation under suspicious behavior. SCP-9365's reporting on anomalous activities aligns with Foundation's objectives, and has been utilized as an asset for performance in field investigations.

Addendum: SCP-9365's anomalous abilities have yet to be fully documented, but it has demonstrated limited reality-bending properties suggesting a higher-than-normal sapience level. Research is being conducted to fully understand the extent of SCP-9365's abilities.
