Item #: 1712

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1712 is to be stationed as an active member of site-██'s security detail. The subject should be allowed to wear their preferred clothing and accessories while on duty, but they must be monitored for any unexplained and anomalous behavior.

Description: SCP-1712 is a male rabbit, standing at approximately 50 cm in height. The subject has been observed to wear a red beanie as a part of his uniform, with a goggle attached to the forehead. SCP-1712's eyes are also equipped with VR Jade LED, which is hypothesized to enhance his visual capabilities. The subject also wears a gold earring on his left ear, along with a red scarf and a whistle.

SCP-1712 exhibits a strong sense of duty and responsibility, and is a reliable member of the security team. However, the subject has also been noted for their ability to let loose and enjoy life, leading to humorous situations. SCP-1712 is especially known for surprising others with their dance moves and silly antics on the dance floor.

Despite SCP-1712's humorous demeanor, they should not be underestimated. The subject is equipped with enhanced visual and auditory capabilities, and has demonstrated remarkable speed and reflexes when needed. While on duty, SCP-1712 is entirely focused on their job, and is capable of dealing with anomalous threats in a professional manner.

Addendum: SCP-1712's playful nature and positive attitude have proven to be beneficial in maintaining morale of site personnel during high-stress situations. The subject is considered by many as a valuable asset to the site's security team.
