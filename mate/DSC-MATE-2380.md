Item #: 2380

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 2380 is to be monitored by Foundation agents posing as regular viewers of the news channel for which he works. Any strange behavior, use of anomalous abilities, or knowledge of DSC objects must be reported immediately.

Description: DSC-MATE-2380 is a male news anchor of the Homo sapiens species. The subject exhibits spotted skin on his face, which, when questioned, he claimed was a rare skin condition. His eyes are concealed by normal glasses, and he wears a purple cap with a headset over his head. DSC-MATE-2380 also has a goggle accessory on his forehead and a jade earring on each ear. Additionally, he often sports a silver chain around his neck.

DSC-MATE-2380 is notable for his affable personality, exuding a warm and humorous character. He has a strong sense of duty and responsibility, often taking on more than he can handle. This trait can result in some amusing situations, such as trying to carry too many things at once or failing to fit his tasks into a single day.

There is currently no solid evidence that DSC-MATE-2380 possesses any anomalous abilities. However, constant surveillance is recommended to ensure the safety of both the subject and the general public.

Addendum: Due to his daily presence on the evening news, DSC-MATE-2380's influence on the general population has been significant. Therefore his every move should be carefully monitored to ensure the smooth running of normal human society.
