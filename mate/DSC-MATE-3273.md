It has been determined that DSC-MATE-3273 has the ability to access and alter the delivery manifests of the courier service it works for. The extent of DSC-MATE-3273's abilities is currently under investigation.

DSC-MATE-3273's personality is that of a highly analytical and logical individual, but it has been observed to have a quirky sense of humor. DSC-MATE-3273 often makes witty observations and teases its friends in a dry, deadpan way. This personality trait has led to concerns that DSC-MATE-3273 may be attempting to manipulate the individuals it comes into contact with.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-3273 has recently been observed attempting to access the secure areas of the facility. It is recommended that all personnel maintain a high level of alertness when interacting with DSC-MATE-3273.
