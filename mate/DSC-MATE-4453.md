Item #: DSC-MATE-4453

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4453 is to be housed in a standard containment chamber, outfitted with an obstacle course suitable for a rabbit of its size. Task forces assigned to DSC-MATE-4453 are to ensure that the object is well-rested, well-fed, and provided with any necessary medical attention.

DSC-MATE-4453 is to be given a traffic officer uniform and tasked with traffic control in Level-4 area. Under no circumstances should DSC-MATE-4453 be allowed to participate in any undercover operations.

Description: DSC-MATE-4453 is a male rabbit with VR Jade LED eyes. Its head is adorned with a blue bandana and a worn goggle on its forehead. Its right ear is pierced with a jade earring and it wears a silver chain around its neck. DSC-MATE-4453 stands at approximately 23 cm tall and weighs 2.3 kg.

DSC-MATE-4453 demonstrates an efficient and effective ability to control traffic, efficiently coordinating the flow of vehicles, humans and other entities with ease. However, it suffers from forgetfulness and can be prone to absent-mindedness, causing it to miss appointments or become lost in thought while on duty.

DSC-MATE-4453 has demonstrated strong loyalty to the Foundation and has shown no signs of attempting to escape Foundation custody. DSC-MATE-4453's cooperation and loyalty have earned it a position as a respected member of Task Force Gamma-5 ("Road Hoppers").

Addendum: Despite its physical limitations, DSC-MATE-4453 has proven to be an invaluable asset to the Foundation in numerous operations around the world. Its impressive command of traffic flow is second only to its loyalty and bravery, qualities that have led to its recognition as a highly decorated member of the Foundation.
