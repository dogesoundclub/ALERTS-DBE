Item #: 7409

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Item 7409 must be contained in a reinforced steel room, impossible to break from the outside. It must remain powered off at all times as a precaution. Personnel are forbidden from interacting with it.

Description: Item 7409 appears to be a robot with a frightened mouth as its primary facial feature. Its eyes take the form of ski goggles while its red cross earrings make up its ears. Its mouth is always covered with a medical mask.

Personality: Despite being designated as a villain, Item 7409 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. This can lead to humorous situations, such as telling funny stories or doing silly impersonations to lighten the mood. However, these antics can be deceiving as Item 7409 is highly intelligent and calculating. It is important to remember that Item 7409 is a dangerous and unpredictable entity.
