Item #: 4950

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4950 is to be monitored at all times through their real estate agency. They are to be allowed full freedom to operate in the human world within the limits of local and national laws. If SCP-4950 exhibits any anomalous behavior, MTF Omega-12 ("Dreamcatchers") must be dispatched to investigate and contain the situation.

Description: SCP-4950 is a female human real estate agent with red lips and red clown eyes. They often wear a bucket hat and gold earrings along with a medical mask covering their mouth. SCP-4950 has a goggle attached to their forehead and a silver chain draped around their neck.

SCP-4950 displays an idealistic personality and has a strong desire to make the world a better place. They often engage in protesting and boycotting companies or causes that go against their values. It is unknown if SCP-4950's belief system stems from an anomalous origin.

SCP-4950 has not displayed any anomalous abilities or effects, but Foundation researchers are monitoring their activities to ensure that they do not pose a threat to normalcy.

Addendum: SCP-4950 has recently shown interest in purchasing a property rumored to be haunted. Researchers are currently investigating the location and will provide updates as necessary.
