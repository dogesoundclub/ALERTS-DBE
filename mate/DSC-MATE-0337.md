Item #: 337

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Item 337 is to be assigned to undercover duty as a Traffic Officer in densely populated urban areas. Due to the potentially hazardous nature of its abilities, it is only to be used in extreme circumstances.

Description: Item 337 appears to be a male tiger with a frightened mouth expression. This particular specimen is often seen wearing horned rim glasses, a doge hat, and a red victory band on its forehead, with a red cross earring on each ear. Item 337 is often seen wearing a medical mask when in the field.

Personality: Item 337 tends to be extremely detail-oriented, bordering on obsessive, often alphabetizing its spice rack or color-coding its closet. These meticulous tendencies manifest in unexpected and often unusual ways. Despite its quirks, Item 337 is a reliable and effective agent in the field, using its attention to detail to great effect in its duties as a Traffic Officer.

Caution: Item 337's abilities are potentially hazardous to civilians and Foundation personnel. All personnel are to exercise extreme caution when in the presence of Item 337, and report any unusual behavior to their superiors immediately.
