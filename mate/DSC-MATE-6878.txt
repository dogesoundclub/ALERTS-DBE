Item #: 6878

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6878 is to be monitored at all times through remote audio and visual surveillance. Any unusual behavior or activity must be reported immediately to the Site Director. Interaction with SCP-6878 must be done with extreme caution due to its political influence.

Description: SCP-6878 is a male rabbit politician, measuring approximately 1 meter in height. It wears a bucket hat on its head and a gold earring on the left ear. The rabbit's mouth often assumes a frightened expression, and it has been noted that SCP-6878 wears normal glasses. The subject is often seen carrying a whistle as an accessory.

SCP-6878 exhibits traits typical of rabbits, such as agility and quick reflexes. However, it displays unusual intelligence and sophistication in its mannerisms, communication, and decision-making.

Personality-wise, SCP-6878 is reliable, focused, and highly organized. Nevertheless, it tends to cling to outdated routines and doesn't adapt well to change. It has been observed that the subject struggles to embrace new concepts or ideas, leading to comical situations when facing unforeseeable events.

SCP-6878 has gained significant political influence and is highly influential among political circles. Caution is advised when dealing with SCP-6878, as its political power is believed to be highly influential.

Addendum: SCP-6878 is believed to have ties with other anomalous entities operating in the political sphere. Investigations are ongoing.
