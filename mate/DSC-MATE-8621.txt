Item #: SCP-8621

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8621 is to be kept in a standard human containment chamber, modified to fit the needs of a rabbit. The chamber must be stocked with appropriate food and toys. SCP-8621 must be given access to writing materials as they exhibit no anomalous qualities in their work as a reporter for the Foundation.

Description: SCP-8621 is a male rabbit standing at approximately 30 cm tall when not wearing its bucket hat. SCP-8621 has been observed to display two facial features; one is best described as a frightened mouth, and the other is a wide, toothy grin. SCP-8621 is often seen sporting a gold chain necklace and carrying a candy stick. Its eyes are red with a black pupil, similar in appearance to those seen in clown makeup.

SCP-8621 is sapient, and its communication is clear and coherent. It identifies as a reporter, and it has been observed to complete tasks requested by the Foundation in a professional manner. SCP-8621 is capable of writing articles and making news broadcasts, and it likes to plan ahead to ensure everything is in order.

SCP-8621 has a spontaneous side and enjoys impromptu adventures and last-minute party planning. It has been known to create humorous situations with its actions, which often turn out to be a hit with its colleagues.

Addendum: SCP-8621 has recently expressed concerns about the containment protocols in place for its species. The matter is under review by the Ethics Committee.
