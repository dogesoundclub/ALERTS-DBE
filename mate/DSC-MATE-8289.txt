Item #: SCP-8289

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8289 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell must be secured with a magnetic lock, and a minimum of two (2) armed guards must be stationed outside the cell at all times.

Description: SCP-8289 is a humanoid entity that appears to be a human female of indeterminate age. SCP-8289 possesses a number of unusual physical features, including metal blue hair, an unusual goggle attached to the forehead, golden earrings, and lips that are typically stained a deep purple color. SCP-8289 is a lawyer by profession, and it is believed that they utilize their unique personality traits to manipulate individuals into providing sensitive information.

SCP-8289 demonstrates a high degree of charisma and is able to connect with individuals on a deep level. Through their charming personality and wit, SCP-8289 is able to elicit personal stories from subjects and become relatable, often resulting in the subject's decision to provide SCP-8289 with confidential information or to assist them in their schemes.

Due to SCP-8289's ability to manipulate individuals with ease, it is possible that they possess a metaphysical or anomalous trait. Research is currently ongoing to determine the nature of SCP-8289's abilities.

Addendum: SCP-8289 has expressed a fascination with the occult and paranormal, and information obtained during interviews suggests that they may have knowledge beyond that of an average human. Researchers are advised to exercise caution when interacting with SCP-8289.
