SCP Foundation Containment Report

Identification Number: SCP-1255

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-1255 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell within Site-██. SCP-1255 is permitted limited interaction with other humanoid SCPs, but should be closely monitored at all times. All communication between SCP-1255 and other SCPs is to be recorded and promptly reviewed by designated personnel. SCP-1255 is permitted to wear their bucket hat and gold earring, but all other accessories are to be confiscated and stored separately in a secured locker.

Description:

SCP-1255 is a male monkey. SCP-1255 stands at approximately 4 feet tall and displays human-like characteristics. SCP-1255 has a great sense of humor and enjoys making others laugh. SCP-1255 often tells funny stories and does silly impersonations to lighten the mood. SCP-1255 has been known to use this as a distraction during attempts to escape from containment.

SCP-1255 frequently wears normal glasses, a bucket hat, and a goggle on their forehead. SCP-1255 also sports a silver chain and an LED gas mask. When questioned about these accessories, SCP-1255 has been uncooperative and defensive.

SCP-1255's abilities are currently unknown, but their behavior indicates above-average intelligence and an aptitude for stealth.

Personality:

SCP-1255 displays a generally pleasant demeanor and enjoys playful banter with researchers and other SCPs. However, SCP-1255 has been known to display aggressive and confrontational behavior when its possessions or position are threatened. SCP-1255 is to be handled with caution and monitored at all times to ensure the safety of all personnel involved.

Addendum:

During routine surveillance, SCP-1255 was observed communicating with an unknown entity via an electronic device hidden in its gas mask. Investigation of the device revealed encrypted messages and sensitive information pertaining to the Foundation's containment procedures. Further investigation into the nature of the entity and its relationship with SCP-1255 is ongoing.
