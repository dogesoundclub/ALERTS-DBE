SCP Foundation Entry:
Item #: SCP-9625

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9625 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment quarters. Physical interaction with SCP-9625 is permitted only under the supervision of Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-9625 is a human female of European descent, with blonde hair and a recognizable smile. SCP-9625's most notable feature is her VR Rainbow LED eyes. SCP-9625 is often seen wearing a gold chain, and a ushanka hat.

SCP-9625 is a reporter by profession, carrying out her duties with passion and a strong sense of responsibility. However, she also enjoys letting loose and having fun when the opportunity arises. SCP-9625 is known to surprise others with her dance moves and silly antics, often leading to humorous situations at social events.

SCP-9625's abilities are yet to be fully evaluated but appear to be non-anomalous. Further research will help understand the nature of SCP-9625's eyes and if they affect its personality.

Addendum: After the recent incident with SCP-████, it has been suggested that SCP-9625 be monitored more closely, and no information regarding the SCP Foundation or anomalous objects should be provided to SCP-9625 to avoid any possible leaks.
