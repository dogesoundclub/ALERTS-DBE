Item #: SCP-2565

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2565 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber located in Site-19. Due to SCP-2565's occupation as a security guard, it is permitted to carry its LED gas mask and silver chain as part of its uniform while on duty. SCP-2565 is to undergo weekly psychological evaluations to monitor its mental state and ability to perform its job duties effectively.

Description: SCP-2565 is a human male of average height and build, with distinctive orange stripes on its cheeks and forehead. Its head is usually covered by a red beanie, and it wears a pair of ski goggles over its eyes. SCP-2565's ears are adorned with a jade earring, and it often wears a silver chain around its neck.

SCP-2565 displays some scattered thinking and forgetfulness. While often humorous, this can lead to issues in its job as a security guard. SCP-2565 has expressed a desire to improve its memory and focus, and has been undergoing training to address these issues.

Addendum: During routine testing, SCP-2565 displayed an unexpected ability to perceive and interact with certain anomalous entities. Further testing is required to determine the extent of this ability and any potential danger it poses to SCP-2565 or others.
