Item #: 4519

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4519 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell and monitored at all times. Any interaction with SCP-4519 must be cleared by at least Level 3 personnel. In the event of a containment breach, all personnel must avoid direct contact with SCP-4519 and contact security immediately.

Description: SCP-4519 is a female humanoid with orange stripes on their face and a permanent smile. Their eyes are red clown eyes and their head appears to be not arranged green. SCP-4519 has two color bands on their forehead, and gold earrings on their ears. They also wear a choker as an accessory.

SCP-4519 appears to be a human, but their leadership abilities suggest they have some unknown anomalous abilities. They work as a firefighter and have shown to have a natural talent for leadership. SCP-4519 often takes charge of situations, whether it's at work, in a social group, or even in their personal life.

SCP-4519 has not shown any signs of aggression or harmful behavior. However, due to their anomalous abilities and potential for causing harm, they are classified as Euclid.

Addendum: SCP-4519's origin is currently unknown, and further investigation is ongoing.
