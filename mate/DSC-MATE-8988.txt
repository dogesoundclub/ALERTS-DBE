Item #: SCP-8988

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8988 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. All interactions with SCP-8988 must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. Any unauthorized contact with SCP-8988 will result in disciplinary action.

Description: SCP-8988 is a humanoid entity standing at 1.82 meters tall and weighing approximately 90 kilograms. SCP-8988 resembles a middle-aged male with orange stripes on his face, emphasized by his giant smile. SCP-8988 has a normal set of eyesight, but is often seen wearing glasses.

SCP-8988 possesses a silver chain and green cross earring on his left ear. In addition, SCP-8988 can usually be found sucking on a candy stick. SCP-8988's most distinct feature is the 2 color band on its forehead.

SCP-8988 claims to be a mafia boss and enjoys brainstorming new ideas that would aid his business. SCP-8988 comes up with outlandish solutions to problems, often just to see how others react. SCP-8988 possesses a charismatic demeanor and frequently displays malicious intent.

Discovery: SCP-8988 was discovered during a Foundation raid on a mafia operation in ██████████, ██. SCP-8988 was apprehended alongside several associates in the operation. SCP-8988 stood out with his distinct features and demeanor, which prompted further investigation.

Addendum-8988-01: During an interview conducted by Agent ████, SCP-8988 revealed that he was aware of the Foundation and expressed interest in making a deal. SCP-8988 revealed that he has knowledge of several anomalies that may be of interest to the Foundation.

Addendum-8988-02: SCP-8988's knowledge has proven to be valuable to the Foundation, and as such, a deal has been made. SCP-8988 will remain in Foundation custody and provide information in exchange for a reduced sentence. SCP-8988 has shown to be cooperative with Foundation personnel and has provided key information in several ongoing investigations.
