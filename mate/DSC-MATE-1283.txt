Item #: SCP-1283

Object Class: Euclid Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1283 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with limited access to the outside world. Access to SCP-1283 is only permitted with the expressed permission of two Level 3 personnel or higher. Any instances of anomalous activity are to be reported immediately to on-site personnel, who are responsible for handling the situation.

Description: SCP-1283 is a human male, approximately 35 years of age, with spots covering the face and a frightened mouth. SCP-1283 has brown eyes resembling ski goggles, and frequently wears a doge hat on its head. SCP-1283 possesses green cross earrings on its ears and wears a silver chain around its neck. SCP-1283 frequently smokes cigarettes regardless of any non-smoking policy in place.

SCP-1283 is an urban legend among the locals and commonly known as a Traffic Police Officer. Not much is known about the origins of SCP-1283 or how it obtained its anomalous abilities, but it is believed that SCP-1283 has a strong sense of intuition and often appears to possess psychic powers. It is capable of predicting future events or sensing emotions and behavior of people like stress, anxiety or depression.

Addendum: SCP-1283 was first detected by Foundation personnel after multiple reports of its anomalous activity in local news. Upon containment, it has been observed that SCP-1283 has heightened awareness of psychic abilities which have extensively manifested in times of stress. Caution is advised when dealing with SCP-1283.
