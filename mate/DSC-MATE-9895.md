Item #: DSC-MATE-9895

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9895 is to be monitored at all times through surveillance cameras installed in its workplace. Any malfunction or unusual behavior is to be reported immediately to the Foundation. DSC-MATE-9895 is to be allowed limited access to the outside world only during working hours.

Description: DSC-MATE-9895 appears to be a delivery driver, working for a local courier company. However, it is actually a robot with an unknown origin. DSC-MATE-9895 has a silver chain around its neck and a single jade earring on its left ear. It also has ski goggles over its eyes and an additional pair of goggles on its forehead.

DSC-MATE-9895's behavior is generally introverted and tends to get lost in thought frequently. This is thought to be a quirk of its programming or possibly a malfunction. DSC-MATE-9895's introspective nature can lead to humorous accidents, such as walking into walls or tripping over objects.

Addendum 9895-1: Incident Log

On [REDACTED], DSC-MATE-9895 malfunctioned during a delivery and began rambling incoherently. It was reciting poetry and singing to itself, causing a traffic accident. DSC personnel were dispatched to retrieve DSC-MATE-9895 and return it to containment. Further testing is required to identify the cause of the malfunction.

Dr. ██████: Thank you for your time, DSC-MATE-9895.
