Item #: DSC-MATE-2817

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2817 is to be monitored at all times while on duty and all interactions with DSC-MATE-2817 are to be recorded. DSC-MATE-2817 is allowed to continue carrying out their job as a firefighter, however, they must wear their medical mask at all times while on duty to prevent revealing their anomalous properties to civilians.

Description: DSC-MATE-2817, also known as "The Detail-Obsessed Firefighter", is a human male with distinctive markings on his face. His face is covered in spots and a permanent, almost eerie, smile. DSC-MATE-2817 also has unique features such as green layers on his head, jade earring in his ears, and a silver chain around his neck.

DSC-MATE-2817 displays an unusual personality trait, characterized by his obsession with minor details. This trait makes him an excellent firefighter, as he is known for completing his duties with remarkable efficiency. However, this trait can also manifest itself in unexpected ways, from alphabetizing his spice rack to color-coding his wardrobe.

DSC-MATE-2817's anomalous property lies in his medical mask. When DSC-MATE-2817 is wearing his mask, all those who interact with him become similarly detail-oriented, focusing on minor details and sometimes overlooking important big-picture ideas. DSC-MATE-2817 is the only one unaffected by this property, which is why he must wear the mask at all times when in public.

Addendum: Due to DSC-MATE-2817's efficient and thorough performance when responding to fires, he has been commended multiple times for his dedication to his job. The Foundation has initiated measures to recruit DSC-MATE-2817 to join its ranks officially.
