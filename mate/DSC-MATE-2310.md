DogeSoundClub Confidential Information:

Item #: DSC-MATE-2310

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2310 is to be contained within its train engine compartment at all times when not operating a train. The compartment must be secured with reinforced steel walls and locked from the outside. In addition, DSC-MATE-2310 is not permitted to operate a train that is carrying any Foundation personnel or DSCs. Any attempt by DSC-MATE-2310 to access these personnel or DSCs is to be met with immediate neutralization.

Description: DSC-MATE-2310 appears to be a monkey approximately 1 meter tall with unknown eye color. Its head is adorned with a purple cap which has a headset attached, and its ears bear a gold earring. DSC-MATE-2310 is often seen wearing a gold chain around its neck and smoking a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-2310 is a train driver who operates transportation through various urban areas. DSC-MATE-2310 appears to possess a high level of intelligence and is capable of communicating through speech with human beings. Further, DSC-MATE-2310 exhibits a strong desire for harmony and social connections, often engaging in playful banter and telling jokes.

However, DSC-MATE-2310 also possesses a goofy and mischievous side which can result in humorous but potentially dangerous situations, particularly when interacting with other DSCs or Foundation personnel.

DSC-MATE-2310 was initially discovered operating a train in urban areas, where it had developed a significant following in the local population. Following reports of strange occurrences associated with the train's operation, the DogeSoundClub was alerted and DSC-MATE-2310 was contained.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-2310 has been observed attempting to communicate with other DSCs in containment. As such, all interactions between DSC-MATE-2310 and other DSCs must be closely monitored and limited to prevent any potential harm to Foundation personnel or DSCs.
