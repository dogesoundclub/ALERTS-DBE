Item #: SCP-1173

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1173 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. They are to be provided with a steady supply of carrots and a pack of cigarettes per day. Interaction with SCP-1173 should be kept to a minimum due to their unpredictable nature.

Description: SCP-1173 appears to be a humanoid rabbit approximately 1.8 meters in height. They are a male and known to work at a local fast food restaurant. SCP-1173's face feature includes a frightened mouth and their eyes resemble that of a clown, colored blue. SCP-1173 wears a retro cap on their head and has a jade earring in each ear. They are often seen wearing a red scarf and smoking a cigarette.

SCP-1173's personality is highly independent and rebellious towards authority and unnecessary regulations. They have been known to display erratic behavior, such as skipping work or leaving early. SCP-1173 has also displayed a lack of fear towards hazardous situations, often placing themselves in danger.

SCP-1173's true nature is currently unknown, but it is believed that they possess anomalous properties that have not yet been fully understood. Further research and interaction with SCP-1173 will be necessary to determine the extent of their abilities.
