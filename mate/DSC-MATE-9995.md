Item #: 9995

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9995 is to be regularly monitored to ensure that it continues to adhere to the standards of a normal human. Any deviation from normal human behavior should be reported to Site-19 for further investigation.

Description: DSC-MATE-9995 is a human male who works as a news anchor. It exhibits distinctive physical characteristics, including orange stripes on its face, normal glasses for eyes, a peak spike for a head, a goggle on its forehead, jade earrings on both ears, and a red scarf as an accessory.

DSC-MATE-9995 is an incredibly energetic and enthusiastic individual who often becomes overly excited about things that others may not find interesting. Subjects in close proximity to DSC-MATE-9995 report feeling an overwhelming sense of eagerness and excitement, often becoming caught up in its infectious energy.

Despite its exuberance, DSC-MATE-9995 has thus far exhibited no anomalous abilities or tendencies. However, given its distinctive appearance and infectious personality, it is considered necessary for regular monitoring to ensure its continued adherence to human standards.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-9995 was discovered after multiple reports from news viewers claimed that the anchor had caused them to become overexcited while watching its segments. Upon investigation, DSC-MATE-9995 was identified and brought to the Foundation's attention. Further interviews with DSC-MATE-9995 have revealed no malice or ill intent, only an intense desire to share its enthusiasm with others.
