Item #: DSC-MATE-4379

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4379 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. It is to be monitored at all times by at least two armed guards. Any attempts to breach containment are to be met with deadly force.

Description: DSC-MATE-4379 appears to be a humanoid robot constructed of various metals and alloys. It stands at approximately 6'5" in height and weighs roughly 500 lbs. Its eyes consist of thick prescription glasses, giving it the appearance of a stereotypical nerd. Its ears are adorned with a single gold hoop earring. DSC-MATE-4379 is typically seen carrying a whistle, though its purpose is unknown.

Personality: Despite its intimidating appearance, DSC-MATE-4379 has a surprisingly nurturing and caring personality. It often goes to great lengths to care for its loved ones, treating them as if they were its own children. This has led to several humorous situations, such as DSC-MATE-4379 attempting to feed Foundation personnel or tucking them in at night.

Job: DSC-MATE-4379 is believed to hold a high-ranking position within a local criminal organization. It has a reputation for being extremely dangerous and violent, though it has not exhibited any aggressive behavior since being contained by the Foundation.

Additional Notes: DSC-MATE-4379 is one of the more unique humanoid anomalies contained by the Foundation. Its mix of intimidating appearance and nurturing personality make it a fascinating subject for study. However, due to its ties to organized crime, extreme caution is to be exercised when dealing with DSC-MATE-4379.
