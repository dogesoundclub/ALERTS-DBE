ITEM #: SCP-7947

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7947 is currently being contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. She is to be monitored 24/7 by at least two security personnel. Access to SCP-7947 is restricted to Level 3 personnel or higher and all interactions with SCP-7947 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel.

Description: SCP-7947 is a human female with spots on her face and red lips. Her eyes are replaced with VR rainbow LED and she wears a bucket hat on her head. She also wears a red cross earring on each ear, a silver chain, and is frequently seen with a cigarette. SCP-7947's personality appears to value harmony and social connections, often engaging in humorous banter or telling jokes with others.

SCP-7947 has demonstrated a wide range of abilities, including heightened agility, increased strength, and the ability to create and manipulate fire. SCP-7947 has also been observed using telekinetic abilities to manipulate objects or individuals.

SCP-7947 is a known villain and has been linked to various criminal activities, including theft and murder. SCP-7947 has been observed working alone, as well as with other known anomalies.

Addendum: On ██/██/20██, SCP-7947 breached containment and escaped Site-██. She is currently at large and is considered extremely dangerous. Any sightings of SCP-7947 should be reported immediately to any local SCP Foundation facilities.
