Item #: SCP-9040

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9040 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber with a light source and comfortable amenities appropriate for its species. Access to SCP-9040 is granted to facility staff with Level 2 clearance or higher, with approval from at least two senior researchers.

Description: SCP-9040 is a male monkey, approximately 50 cm tall and weighing 9 kg. It has green fur that is not arranged in any significant pattern, horned rim glasses, and a 2 color band on its forehead. SCP-9040 also has a pair of goggles on its forehead, a silver chain around its neck, a jade earring on its right ear, and a whistle around its neck.

SCP-9040 works as an electrician and shows advanced knowledge of electrical systems and repair. It demonstrates a high level of intelligence, problem-solving abilities, and cooperation.

SCP-9040 has a very friendly and sociable personality, valuing harmony and social connections. It also has a goofy side, which can lead to humorous situations, such as telling jokes or engaging in playful banter with others.

SCP-9040's anomalous properties are currently unknown, and further testing is required to determine the extent of its abilities.

Addendum: SCP-9040 has expressed interest in playing practical jokes on its handlers. It is important to monitor its interactions and ensure that no harm is caused to personnel or the facility.
