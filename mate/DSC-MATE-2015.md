Addendum: Due to DSC-MATE-2015's love of argumentation, it has become necessary to limit its access to electronic devices that provide a readily available platform for debate, as DSC-MATE-2015 has been known to engage in lengthy and sometimes heated debates online. All personnel interacting with DSC-MATE-2015 must be prepared for its enthusiastic nature and its willingness to debate any subject, regardless of its relevance to its containment.
