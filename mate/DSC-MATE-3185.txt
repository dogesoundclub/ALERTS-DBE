Item #: SCP-3185

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3185 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. They are allowed to work at any fast-food establishment with permission from personnel, but must not disclose their true nature to humans. SCP-3185 is to be provided with a steady supply of meat and other tiger-like food, as well as any necessary medical attention.

Description: SCP-3185 is a male tiger approximately 2 meters in height and weighing approximately 200 kg. They possess several unique features, including a frightened mouth and unknown eyes. SCP-3185 is typically seen wearing a black hoodie, a silver chain, and an LED gas mask.

SCP-3185 has a strong sense of duty and responsibility, often taking on more tasks than they can handle. This can lead to humorous situations such as attempting to carry too many things at once, or struggling to complete all of their tasks in a single day. SCP-3185 displays a friendly demeanor and enjoys interacting with humans.

SCP-3185 was first discovered working at a local fast-food restaurant. Upon initial investigation, SCP Foundation agents discovered that they possessed unusual strength and agility, as well as the ability to communicate telepathically with other tigers.

Addendum 3185-1: SCP-3185 has expressed interest in learning more about human culture and has shown a willingness to comply with containment procedures. They have also shown an aptitude for cooking and food service and have expressed a desire to learn more about the culinary arts. In order to facilitate their integration into human society, SCP-3185 is currently undergoing training in food service and customer service.
