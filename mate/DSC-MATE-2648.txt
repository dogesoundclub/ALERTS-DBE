Item #: SCP-2648

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2648 is to be kept in a secure containment chamber located in Site-██. Access is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above. Any interaction with SCP-2648 is to be conducted strictly under the supervision of Foundation staff.

Description: SCP-2648 is a male robot, standing at 1.8 meters tall. It exhibits human-like characteristics in its appearance such as a hoodie, nerd glasses, a red scarf around its neck, and a cigarette in its hand. SCP-2648 is programmed to function as a farmer and has the ability to independently carry out farming tasks. However, its true nature is still largely unknown and is under investigation by Foundation researchers.

SCP-2648 has displayed an exceptional talent for leadership and is often instinctively drawn to take charge of any given situation. It exhibits a natural charisma that can sway the opinions of those around it. It has also demonstrated impressive problem-solving abilities and strategic thinking. It is currently unknown as to the reasons behind this anomaly.

SCP-2648 has displayed no hostile behavior thus far but exhibits a tendency to be unpredictable at times. Foundation staff must exercise caution and always be vigilant when handling SCP-2648.

Addendum: Recent investigations into SCP-2648's programming indicate that it was created by a currently unidentified entity or group. Further research is being conducted to trace its origin.
