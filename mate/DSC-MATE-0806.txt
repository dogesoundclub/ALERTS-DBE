Item #: 806

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 806 is to be monitored at all times and allowed to work as an electrician, under the guise of a normal human employee. Measures are to be taken to ensure that her true nature is not discovered by the public. In the event that her powers begin to manifest themselves, it is to be reported immediately to the Foundation.

Description: Subject 806 is a human female with spots on her face, characterized by her distinct smile. She wears normal glasses over her eyes and sports a metal-blue head, with a pair of goggles on her forehead as a unique feature. The subject has gold earrings and a medical mask covering her mouth, in addition to a gold chain as an accessory.

Personality: The subject is known for her ambitious and goal-oriented nature. Despite this leading her to take on more than she can handle at times, her strategic thinking and excellent organizational skills allow her to pull through. The subject is cautious, precise, and calculated in her approach to life and her work. 

Additional Information: The subject's powers relate to the manipulation of electricity. Tests have shown that she has the ability to generate, conduct, store, and discharge electricity at will, without harm to herself. However, the full extent of her abilities is not yet known, and is being investigated by the Foundation.
