Item #: 8593

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8593 is to be treated as a normal human and allowed to continue their duties as a police officer within the designated jurisdiction. SCP-8593 is to be monitored for any abnormal or potentially dangerous behavior.

Description: SCP-8593 is a male human with distinct physical features. He has spots on his face and a perpetual smile, regardless of the situation. SCP-8593's eyes have been replaced with what appears to be ski goggles, though they function as normal eyes. SCP-8593 often wears a retro cap on his head and a jade earring in one of his ears.

SCP-8593 also wears a gold chain and often smokes cigarettes. SCP-8593 has a love for adventure and may enjoy taking risks or trying new things. Members of SCP's security team report humorous stories involving SCP-8593, such as surviving a near-death experience while bungee jumping or accidentally getting lost in the wilderness. Despite SCP-8593's adventurous nature, he remains a functioning member of society and his work as a police officer is commendable.

Addendum: SCP-8593's adventurous nature is being monitored for any signs of dangerous behavior. At this time, SCP-8593 poses no significant threat to the public or SCP security.
