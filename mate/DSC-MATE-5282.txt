Item #: SCP-5282

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5282 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell. Access to electronic devices, including cell phones or computers, is strictly forbidden. SCP-5282 is allowed weekly visits with a designated psychologist for mental stability.

Description: SCP-5282 is a humanoid entity identified as a female car salesman. SCP-5282 displays distinctive facial features, including orange stripes and a red lip, complemented by an orange side head and green cross earring.

SCP-5282 is often seen wearing a gold chain and an LED gas mask. SCP-5282's personality is highly outgoing and social, often playing the role of the center of attention. SCP-5282 displays a sense of humor that can cause uncontrollable laughter in small or large groups.

SCP-5282's ability to influence people through its humor is still under investigation. SCP-5282's ability to control people in conversation and salesmanship will require extensive research as it raises some serious security concerns.

Addendum: SCP-5282 has been of interest to various groups in the underground market who have shown interest in SCP-5282's abilities. SCP-5282's containment protocols have been updated accordingly. Any mention of SCP-5282 in underground communities will be reported and investigated thoroughly.
