Item #: 2581

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 2581 is to be kept in a standard containment chamber lined with a minimum of 6 inches of rubber padding to prevent potential injury. The chamber must be cleaned twice a week, with fresh vegetation provided for the subject daily. The subject must be fed a diet of fresh fruits and vegetables, along with the occasional insect. No humans are to be allowed within 10 feet of the subject's containment chamber without express permission from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: Subject 2581 appears to be a male monkey that has been genetically modified to possess the ability of human-like speech and programming skills. The subject stands at a height of 2.5 feet and has a distinctive mohawk on its head. The subject is often seen with a pair of goggles on its forehead and a jade earring in one of its ears.

Subject 2581 has a frightened expression on its face, with its mouth consistently agape. It is also adorned with a gold chain and can often be seen holding onto a candy stick. The subject has a strong sense of duty and responsibility, often taking on more tasks than it can handle which can result in humorous situations.

Addendum: Subject 2581 has proven to be very useful in programming tasks and has assisted in the Foundation's IT department with impressive results. The subject has also shown remarkable aptitude in problem-solving tasks, earning it a place on several research teams. Care must be taken to ensure the subject does not become overloaded with work and begins to exhibit signs of distress.
