SCP Foundation Classification: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6157 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell, with a specialized enclosure to accommodate its physical features. Personnel must wear protective gear when handling SCP-6157 to protect against potential disease transmission. SCP-6157 is to be fed a diet consisting of fresh fruit and vegetables.

Description: SCP-6157 is a male monkey, standing at 5 feet tall when in a standing position. Its body is covered in short brown fur, except for its face and hands which are hairless. SCP-6157 has two red clown eyes, which have been surgically implanted. The subject's head is adorned with green hair and a purple cap. It also has a goggle on its forehead and jade earring on each ear. Additionally, SCP-6157 can be seen wearing a gold chain as an accessory.

SCP-6157 possesses a strong and decisive personality, which can sometimes come across as bossy or intimidating. It shows a high level of intelligence and problem-solving ability. SCP-6157's main focus is efficiency and effectiveness, and it often takes on a leadership role to ensure that this goal is achieved.

SCP-6157's job prior to containment was as a banker in a local bank. Its position allowed it to successfully amass a considerable amount of wealth. Investigation into the source of this wealth is ongoing.

Addendum: SCP-6157 is highly susceptible to the transmission of human diseases due to its close proximity to human society. As such, interaction with SCP-6157 should be limited to trained personnel only. Any unauthorized interaction may result in disciplinary action.
