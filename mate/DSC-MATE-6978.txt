SCP-6978 is a human of the male gender, employed as a delivery driver for the Foundation. His physical appearance is distinguished by his nerdy glasses and his short peak cap. SCP-6978 bears a gold earring on his left ear, adding to his rough appearance.

Despite his appearance, SCP-6978 is known for his intellectual curiosity about the world around him. He has a particular fondness for obscure and unusual topics, making him an occasional source of surprise or amusement for those around him.

However, SCP-6978's random outbursts of trivia can be unsettling at times, leading to his nickname as the "walking encyclopedia." Despite his quirks, SCP-6978 is a valuable member of the Foundation team, bringing his unique brand of inquisitiveness to each delivery he makes.
