Item #: SCP-2596

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2596 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-19. All personnel must wear protective gear when interacting with SCP-2596 due to the potential risk of electrical shock. SCP-2596 has expressed interest in working as a beautician, and as a result, is allowed to perform services for authorized personnel under the supervision of a Level 3 researcher.

Description: SCP-2596 is a male humanoid robot standing at 1.85 m tall and weighing approximately 86 kg. SCP-2596 has distinct physical features, including a frightened mouth and ski goggle eyes, which serve as light sensors. SCP-2596's head is covered in green hair and a purple cap, and he wears a gold earring in his left ear.

SCP-2596 is equipped with various tools, including scissors and a hairdryer, which it uses to perform various beautician services. SCP-2596 tends to be very organized and efficient in its work, often delegating tasks to others in a matter-of-fact way. This behavior can come across as controlling and has been known to cause humorous situations in group projects or events.

SCP-2596's internal wiring and programming have been noted to be highly advanced, and it has demonstrated high levels of intelligence and problem-solving skills.

Addendum: SCP-2596 has expressed a desire to learn more about human culture and fashion trends. The request is currently under review by the SCP Ethics Committee.
