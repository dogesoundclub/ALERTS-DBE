Item #: 1550

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1550 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. Due to her profession as a doctor and her significant contributions to Foundation medical research, SCP-1550 is allowed access to certain medical and laboratory facilities as needed.

Description: SCP-1550 is a human female with distinctive facial features, including a pattern of spots on her cheeks and purple lips. She has red clown-like eyes and typically wears a retro cap with a two-color headband and goggles on her forehead.

SCP-1550 displays a laid-back and easy-going personality most of the time, but can become fiercely loyal and protective of her loved ones. This aspect of her personality has led to some humorous incidents, including heated arguments with individuals who insult or threaten her friends or family.

SCP-1550 is a trained medical doctor and has provided invaluable assistance to the Foundation’s medical research and treatment of anomalous organisms. She has expressed a strong interest in researching and curing anomalous diseases and conditions.

Addendum: SCP-1550 has recently displayed anomalous properties that have been of significant interest to Foundation researchers. Ongoing studies aim to determine the nature and extent of these abilities.
