Item #: SCP-5607

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5607 is to be housed in a standard containment chamber at Site-██. Access to SCP-5607 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above, and all interactions with SCP-5607 must be recorded and monitored.

SCP-5607 is to be provided with a variety of fresh vegetables and fruits for consumption. A whistle has also been provided to SCP-5607, which they may use if they require assistance. Any injuries or illnesses sustained by SCP-5607 must be immediately reported to medical staff for treatment.

Description: SCP-5607 is a male rabbit standing at approximately 30 cm in height. SCP-5607's eyes are in the shape of ski goggles, which are believed to grant SCP-5607 night vision and thermal vision. SCP-5607 also wears a bucket hat and gold earring in their ears. They have a bright red scarf tied around their neck and a whistle hangs from their neck.

SCP-5607 has demonstrated a highly analytical and logical personality, and tends to approach problems and situations from a rational standpoint. Despite this, SCP-5607 has also been observed to have a quirky sense of humor, often making dry, deadpan remarks or teasing those around them. SCP-5607 has displayed an affinity for veterinary medicine, specifically the care of small animals.

SCP-5607 is capable of speaking English fluently, but prefers to communicate in chirps, barks, and other animal noises. SCP-5607 has also displayed the ability to manipulate plant growth and communicate with animals of various species.

Addendum: Researchers are currently investigating the origins of SCP-5607 and the extent of their abilities. Further testing and observation is needed to fully understand the capabilities of SCP-5607.
