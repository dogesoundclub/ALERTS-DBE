Item #: SCP-9072

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9072 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell. SCP-9072 is permitted to continue its profession as a beautician under the scrutiny of Foundation personnel. Any instances of SCP-9072 exhibiting abnormal behavior are to be reported immediately to Site Director.

Description: SCP-9072 appears to be a male human of average build and height. SCP-9072 is characterized by a series of spots on its face and a gold earring on its left ear. Its hair is often visible beneath its short peak cap. SCP-9072's eyes are corrected with normal glasses and it is commonly seen carrying a candy stick.

Despite being amicably laid back, SCP-9072 is capable of extreme loyalty and protectiveness to loved ones. SCP-9072 has exhibited instances of aggressive behavior towards individuals who insult or harm its friends and family members in a humorous manner. SCP-9072 is particularly adept at humor and can often diffuse tense situations with a well-timed joke.

Addendum: SCP-9072 has requested access to Foundation personnel for cosmetic and grooming services. Request has been approved pending security clearance.
