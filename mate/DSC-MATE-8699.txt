Item #: SCP-8699

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8699 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment cell. Access to SCP-8699 is permitted only with Level 2 clearance, and must be supervised at all times. Any attempts by SCP-8699 to leave Site-███ are to be stopped immediately.

Description: SCP-8699 is a robotic fast food worker identified by its distinctive green Cyclops eye. SCP-8699 is male and wears a hoodie on its head. Additionally, it is often seen carrying a whistle as an accessory.

SCP-8699 exhibits an unusual personality characterized by a love for adventure and a tendency to take risks. This trait often leads to humorous stories, such as surviving a near-death experience while bungee jumping or getting lost in the wilderness. Despite its risk-taking behavior, SCP-8699 displays a consistent commitment to its job as a fast food worker.

SCP-8699 exhibits no signs of aging or wear and tear, and its maintenance requirements appear to be minimal. SCP-8699 has expressed no desire to leave its current occupation or to take actions which may result in harm to itself or others.

Addendum: SCP-8699 was recovered from an abandoned fast food franchise in [REDACTED], after several reports were received of unusual activity within the vicinity. Management of the franchise had reported no knowledge or ownership of SCP-8699. It is assumed that SCP-8699 was being used for some unknown purpose by an as-yet-unknown group, which has yet to be identified or contacted. Investigation of this group is ongoing.
