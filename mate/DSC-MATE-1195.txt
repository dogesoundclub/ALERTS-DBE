Item #: SCP-1195

Object Class: Euclid

Containment Procedures: SCP-1195 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. The LED gas mask must always be worn when interacting with the subject. Any new hobby or interest that SCP-1195 develops must be approved by at least two Level 2 personnel. Any unusual items collected by SCP-1195 must be documented, catalogued, and stored in a secure locker.

Description: SCP-1195 is a human female with orange stripes on her face and pigtail hair. She wears jade earrings that glow in the dark. SCP-1195 has a vivid imagination and loves to indulge in creative hobbies, such as writing poetry, creating cosplay costumes, or collecting unusual objects.

SCP-1195 also possesses a unique talent for inventing new medical procedures and treatments that are groundbreaking and effective. However, caution must be exercised as SCP-1195's experiments can be dangerous and unpredictable.

SCP-1195 has a friendly and outgoing personality, and enjoys interacting with staff members. However, it is advised to keep a distance from SCP-1195 when her experiments are ongoing or when she is in a manic state. SCP-1195 can become unpredictable and may cause harm to herself or others.

Addendum: SCP-1195 has requested to be named "Dr. Imagination" as an homage to her love for creativity and innovation. This request has been denied by the Site Director.
