Item #: SCP-1829

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1829 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. All communication with SCP-1829 must be approved by at least one Level 2 personnel.

Description: SCP-1829 is a human female, identified as a Traffic Officer named [REDACTED]. SCP-1829 has metal blue hair and wears a medical mask over her mouth. SCP-1829 possesses a pair of goggles on her forehead and a gold chain accessory. SCP-1829's most notable feature is her constant smile.

SCP-1829 demonstrates idealistic tendencies and strives to improve the world around her. SCP-1829 has a reputation for protesting and boycotting companies or causes that she feels go against her values. Despite her seemingly harmless nature, SCP-1829 has been known to cause disruptions in public areas.

SCP-1829 was discovered after a series of protests in downtown ████, where she was discovered directing traffic while wearing her protest-related accessories. SCP personnel quickly detained SCP-1829 before any further disturbances could occur.

Addendum: SCP-1829 has expressed interest in joining the Foundation in order to contribute to the betterment of the world. This request is currently being reviewed by Foundation personnel.
