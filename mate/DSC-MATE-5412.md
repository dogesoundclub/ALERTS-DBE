Item #: DSC-MATE-5412

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-5412 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber with reinforced steel walls and equipped with a standard gas filtration system. Due to its animalistic nature, DSC-MATE-5412 must be sedated before entering the containment chamber. Any interaction with DSC-MATE-5412 must be done with the assistance of at least two armed guards as a safety precaution.

Description: DSC-MATE-5412 is a sentient, male tiger of unknown origin. It possesses a humanoid form and stands approximately 2 meters in height. DSC-MATE-5412 is most often seen wearing a police cap, a gold earring in each ear, a silver chain around its neck, and a normal gas mask that covers its face.

DSC-MATE-5412 maintains a job as a banker within the financial district of ████████. It has shown expert-level knowledge in the field of finance and has a reputation for being a ruthless negotiator. DSC-MATE-5412's ability to manipulate monetary systems has led to several large-scale economic fluctuations, leading the Foundation to prioritize its containment.

DSC-MATE-5412's personality is characterized by a mix of organization and spontaneity. It enjoys planning and strategizing but often displays an impulsive and adventurous side. DSC-MATE-5412 is known for hosting last-minute parties and surprising its friends with impromptu adventures.

Despite being a carnivorous predator, DSC-MATE-5412 has never shown aggression towards humans, instead preferring to engage in polite conversation. However, DSC-MATE-5412 has been known to exhibit territorial behavior when interacting with other DSCs.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-5412 was first discovered during an economic crisis in the early 20th century. Foundation agents, posing as bankers, were able to apprehend DSC-MATE-5412 without incident. DSC-MATE-5412 has been in containment ever since, with several attempts made by the financial industry to retrieve it. All attempts have been successfully thwarted by the Foundation.
