Item #: 5238

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Item 5238 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with no special requirements. Interaction with Item 5238 is permitted only under the supervision of authorized personnel.

Description: Item 5238, also known as "The Reporter," is a male human with distinctive features. He has spots across his face and a seemingly permanent smile. His eyes appear to be ski goggles that can be removed at will. His head is covered in a not-arranged green substance, and he wears a jade earring on one ear. Item 5238 is known to wear a medical mask and a gold chain as well.

Personality-wise, Item 5238 is known for being very impulsive, often acting on a whim and taking unnecessary risks. This has led to humorous situations, like impulsively jumping off a cliff into a body of water. Despite his propensity for taking risks, Item 5238 is relatively harmless and has shown no signs of hostility towards anyone.

Addendum: Item 5238 is known to work as a reporter and has been seen frequenting crime scenes, often a little too close to the action. It is recommended that he be kept away from dangerous situations and monitored during any on-site reporting.
