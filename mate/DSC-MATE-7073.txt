Item #: SCP-7073

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7073 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. All interactions with SCP-7073 are to be conducted remotely, with any physical contact requiring Level 3 clearance and proper protective gear due to their unpredictable behavior.

Description: SCP-7073 is a human female with a distinct appearance that includes purple lips, VR Rainbow LED eyes, and blonde hair adorned with an Ushanka hat. They are often seen wearing a medical mask and a jade earring, along with a choker.

SCP-7073's personality is characterized by a reckless streak and a tendency to act before thinking things through. This can lead to humorous situations, as SCP-7073 often tries to impress others with their skills as a mechanic. However, their impulsive behavior can also be dangerous, as evidenced by multiple minor accidents and mishaps.

SCP-7073's abilities are still being studied, but they have demonstrated a keen understanding of machinery and technology. They are also known to possess a high pain tolerance and a resistance to certain forms of psychic manipulation.

Addendum: As of ██/██/20██, SCP-7073 has requested a transfer to the Foundation's Research and Development Department for the purpose of improving the performance of certain SCPs. This request is currently under review.
