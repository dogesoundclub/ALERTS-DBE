Item #: SCP-1925

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1925 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber located in Site-██. Access to SCP-1925 is to be granted only to Level 2 personnel with prior clearance and approval from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-1925 is a human male identified as a firefighter with distinctive orange stripes on his face. His eyes are replaced with ski goggles, and he wears a silver chain and jade earring as accessories. SCP-1925 also wears a normal gas mask as his personal protective equipment.

SCP-1925 is known for his ambitious and goal-oriented nature, often taking on tasks beyond his capacity. Despite this, he manages to execute tasks efficiently thanks to his exceptional organizational skills and strategic thinking.

SCP-1925 displays no anomalous properties or abilities. However, it is noted that his equipment displays significant resistance to fire and extreme heat levels beyond typical firefighter gear.

Addendum: Research is ongoing to determine the source of SCP-1925's enhanced equipment resilience to fire. Any new developments regarding SCP-1925 will be updated accordingly.
