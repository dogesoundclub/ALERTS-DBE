Item #: DSC-MATE-8122

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8122 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell, furnished with appropriate facilities for its species. Access to any communication devices is strictly prohibited. Surveillance cameras are to be installed to monitor its behavior.

DSC-MATE-8122 is designated as a Level 2 security risk, and as such, must be accompanied by at least two armed personnel whenever it is transported outside of its containment cell.

Description: DSC-MATE-8122 is a male monkey, approximately 1.3 meters in height. The monkey wears a retro cap on its head, a silver chain around its neck, and is often seen smoking a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-8122 possesses a compelling and charismatic personality and displays a natural talent for leadership. It has been noted that DSC-MATE-8122 often assumes control of situations, both professional and personal, even without being asked or delegated authority.

DSC-MATE-8122 reveals its intelligence through its language abilities, exhibiting the capacity to comprehend and communicate in English. This remarkable trait is yet to be adequately explained, and DSC-MATE-8122 has refused to divulge any information relating to its gift.

To date, testing conducted on DSC-MATE-8122 has not uncovered any paranormal properties or abilities that would warrant its inclusion in any other DSC classification.
