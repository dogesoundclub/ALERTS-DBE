Item #: SCP-4035

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4035 is to be contained within a standard human-habitation chamber at Site-██. All requests for medical attention are to be made through SCP-4035's designated communication device.

Description: SCP-4035 is a male human, approximately 32 years of age. SCP-4035 possesses distinct facial features, including multiple spots across the face, nerd glasses over the eyes, and a red victory band on the forehead. SCP-4035 also wears a gold earring and carries a candy stick as an accessory.

SCP-4035 functions as a medical doctor, capable of providing medical assistance to Foundation personnel. SCP-4035's abilities as a doctor have been noted to be highly effective.

Personality-wise, SCP-4035 has been known to be scatterbrained and forgetful, which can lead to complications with scheduling or navigation during transport. Care should be taken to ensure that SCP-4035 maintains a clear schedule and properly delegates tasks.

SCP-4035's medical expertise proves invaluable to the Foundation's mission. Due to SCP-4035's friendly disposition, handling and containment is deemed to be simple and uncomplicated.
