Item #: 8501

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8501 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-███. Any attempts to modify or alter the internal programming of SCP-8501 are strictly prohibited without prior approval from at least two Level 3 personnel.

SCP-8501 is currently employed as a delivery driver for [REDACTED], and is to be accompanied by two armed security personnel at all times while on the job. SCP-8501 is also equipped with a GPS tracker, which is to be monitored by Foundation personnel to ensure its location at all times.

Description: SCP-8501 appears to be a humanoid robot of unknown origin, with green hair, a purple cap, and horned rim glasses. Its head also features a goggle attached to its forehead, and it wears a gold earring, gold chain, and carries a whistle as accessories.

SCP-8501 is able to communicate fluently in multiple languages, and displays a quick wit and love of debate. It has been observed engaging in playful arguments with friends and family members just for entertainment purposes.

SCP-8501's true capabilities are as of yet unknown. It has displayed remarkable physical strength, agility, and an ability to process information at a speed beyond what is currently known to be possible for humanoid robots.

Addendum: Investigation into the origin of SCP-8501 is ongoing. Any information regarding its creator or purpose is to be immediately reported to Foundation personnel for research and analysis.
