Item #: DSC-MATE-8217

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8217 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber, with limited access to cosmetic and beauty products. Any requests for such products must be first approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. DSC-MATE-8217 is to undergo regular psychological evaluations to monitor its mental state.

Description: DSC-MATE-8217 is a humanoid entity of the human species, identified as a beautician by profession. DSC-MATE-8217's most distinct feature is its spots across its face, with a constant smile. DSC-MATE-8217's eyes are blue clown eyes, giving it a whimsical and playful appearance.

DSC-MATE-8217's head features a metal blue color with a two-color band on its forehead, along with a goggle on top. It also wears a gold chain and an LED gas mask as accessories.

DSC-MATE-8217's personality exhibits high levels of empathy and compassion. It tends to take on other people's problems as its own, attempting to fix everyone and everything around it. While admirable, this behavior can be exhausting for DSC-MATE-8217.

Addendum 8217-A: DSC-MATE-8217 was brought to Foundation attention after reports of its cosmetic procedures resulting in anomalous effects on its clients. DSC-MATE-8217's abilities and the extent of these anomalies are currently under investigation.
