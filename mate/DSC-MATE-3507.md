Item #: DSC-MATE-3507

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3507 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber with reinforced walls and ceiling. The chamber is to be kept at 15 degrees Celsius at all times. DSC-MATE-3507 is to be fed with a diet consisting of raw meat, fruits, and vegetables. Any book or document introduced into the containment chamber must be thoroughly screened for anomalies and approved by two Level 3 personnel prior to distribution to DSC-MATE-3507.

Description: DSC-MATE-3507 is a male Tiger with red clown eyes and a frightened mouth. DSC-MATE-3507 wears a jade earring in its left ear, a red scarf around his neck, and holds a candy stick in his right paw. DSC-MATE-3507 exhibits a desire for knowledge and can be found constantly reading and researching obscure topics. DSC-MATE-3507 enjoys collecting books, documentaries, or other educational materials, and can be quite passionate about sharing its newfound knowledge with others.

DSC-MATE-3507 is capable of speech and exhibits a high level of intelligence. It has a vast amount of knowledge about various fields such as science, mathematics, and history. Its speech is characterized by a distinct accent and a tendency to use complex vocabulary.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-3507 was discovered after multiple reports of a "talking tiger" were received from a local bank. Upon containment, it was discovered that DSC-MATE-3507 had been working as a banker for over a year. It is unknown how DSC-MATE-3507 was able to secure the job or maintain it without being detected.

DSC-MATE-3507 is cooperative with Foundation personnel and has agreed to assist in research and containment efforts related to other DSCs.
