A daily logbook is to be maintained, detailing any changes in its behavior or physical appearance.

It is usually found wearing a silver chain and smoking a cigarette. DSC-MATE-5695 has demonstrated a humanoid intelligence and the ability to communicate fluently in English.

DSC-MATE-5695 displays a forgetful and absent-minded personality, often becoming lost in thought or absorbed in its work as an electrician. This has led to several humorous situations, including missed appointments and forgotten events. DSC-MATE-5695 also has a fondness for smoking, often requesting cigarettes from Foundation staff.

DSC-MATE-5695's profession as an electrician is noteworthy, as it has displayed an innate talent for working with electrical equipment. However, due to the potential danger of DSC-MATE-5695's abilities, it is to be restricted from any electrical equipment.

When attempting to repair a malfunctioning generator in its containment chamber, DSC-MATE-5695 accidentally caused a power surge which resulted in several Site-wide power outages.
