Item #: SCP-1513

Object Class: Humanoid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1513 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment unit. Access to media that may evoke strong emotional responses in SCP-1513 must be limited and monitored. Smoking is prohibited within the containment unit.

Description: SCP-1513 is a human female in her late thirties, standing at 162 centimeters tall and weighing approximately 72 kilograms. SCP-1513 has 90s brown hair and orange eyebrows, with a distinctive red lip and a green cross earring. SCP-1513 often carries a cigarette and exhibits a fondness for music and art that elicits strong emotional reactions.

SCP-1513 possesses a heightened sensitivity to emotional stimuli, often experiencing tears during sentimental moments in movies or television programs. SCP-1513 is emotionally empathetic to a degree that may suggest a possible form of anomalous ability.

SCP-1513's occupation as a mechanic is currently under investigation as some of the machinery and tools utilized by SCP-1513 have demonstrated anomalous capabilities. Further research is required to determine the extent of SCP-1513's anomalous properties.

Overall, SCP-1513's emotional sensitivity is a concern regarding potential breaches of containment. SCP personnel must take care to limit and monitor SCP-1513's exposure to potentially triggering media. Any personnel assigned to interact with SCP-1513 must be trained on de-escalation techniques to prevent emotional outbursts or heightened emotional states that could prove disruptive to Foundation operations.
