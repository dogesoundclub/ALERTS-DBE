Item #: SCP-520

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-520 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. As a precaution, all electrical installations in the chamber must be inspected and maintained on a regular basis by a certified electrician. Access to SCP-520 is limited to Level 2 personnel or higher and requires written approval from at least two Level 3 personnel. SCP-520 is to be provided with a regular supply of candy sticks, his preferred form of sustenance.

Description: SCP-520 appears to be a male tiger with a unique set of features. He wears a purple cap with a headset and has a goggle on his forehead. He also wears a gold chain around his neck and carries a candy stick as an accessory. SCP-520 often has a frightened expression on his mouth.

SCP-520 claims to be an electrician and has the ability to manipulate electricity. Observations indicate that electrical devices near SCP-520 will malfunction or short-circuit without warning. The tiger's exact origin is unknown, and he refuses to disclose any information about it.

SCP-520 has a laid-back and easy-going demeanor, which can sometimes result in humorous situations. He is fiercely loyal and protective of those he cares about, and insults against a friend or family member can trigger an aggressive response. SCP-520 has shown no hostility towards Foundation personnel since containment.

Addendum: Incident Report 520-A

On [Redacted], a D-Class personnel intentionally insulted SCP-520's "little brother." SCP-520 became agitated and attacked the personnel with electrical energy, causing significant bodily harm. SCP-520 was immediately sedated and taken for medical treatment. The affected personnel were treated for their injuries and reassigned to other duties. Lessons regarding the importance of avoiding familial insults are to be provided to Foundation personnel.
