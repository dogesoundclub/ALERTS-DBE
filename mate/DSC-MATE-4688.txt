Item #: 4688

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-4688 is to be kept in a secured, soundproofed room at all times when not on assignment. Only Level 2 personnel or above are allowed to interact with SCP-4688, and all interactions must be monitored by security cameras. Any attempts to tamper with or modify SCP-4688 in any way are strictly forbidden.

Description: SCP-4688 is a male robot designed to resemble a human news anchor. Its face features a frightened mouth that is permanently stuck in a clamshell shape, and it wears normal glasses over its eyes. SCP-4688's head is adorned with a Doge hat, and it also wears a silver chain and a normal gas mask as accessories.

SCP-4688's personality can be described as introspective, often lost in thought or daydreaming. This trait has caused several humorous situations, including but not limited to, SCP-4688 accidentally walking into a pole or tripping over an object while deep in thought.

SCP-4688's origin is currently unknown, and it is believed to have been created by an unknown group with nefarious intentions. Further research is ongoing to determine SCP-4688's true purpose and capabilities.

Addendum: Following incident 4688-02, SCP-4688 is no longer permitted to broadcast live news reports. All news reports must be pre-recorded and thoroughly vetted by Level 3 personnel before airing to prevent any potential security breaches.
