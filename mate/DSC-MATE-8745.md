DSC-MATE-8745 is a mechanic by profession, and appears to be well-versed in the repair and modification of machinery and vehicles. DSC-MATE-8745 demonstrates a natural talent for leadership, often taking charge of situations and commanding respect from his colleagues and subordinates. However, this personality trait does not appear to be anomalous in nature.

Addendum: Due to DSC-MATE-8745's exceptional mechanical skills and leadership qualities, he has been approved for use in Mobile Task Force Omega-5 ("Blacksmiths") for the purposes of equipment repair and maintenance. DSC-MATE-8745 is to be accompanied by at least two armed Foundation personnel at all times during field missions.
