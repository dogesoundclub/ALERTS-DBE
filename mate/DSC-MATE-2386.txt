Item #: 2386

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2386 is to be kept under surveillance at all times and is to have no contact with the outside world. Personnel assigned to SCP-2386 are to dress in protective gear and wear masks when interacting with her."

Description: SCP-2386 appears to be a human female in her late teens or early twenties. She has orange stripes across her face, a bright red clown nose, and her head resembles that of a pink rabbit. Her eyes are red and her ears are adorned with green cross earrings. She wears a choker around her neck at all times.

SCP-2386 is highly organized and efficient, which can sometimes come across as controlling. The subject has been observed taking charge of group projects and events, delegating tasks to others in a matter-of-fact way. SCP-2386 is a skilled farmer and has a vast knowledge of growing crops and tending to livestock.

Although SCP-2386 seems to exhibit human emotions, her behavior and appearance suggest she is not a natural human, but rather a creation of unknown means. Further research is required to determine the origins of SCP-2386.

Addendum: It has been noted that SCP-2386 exhibits a strong aversion to crowded spaces and loud noises, which can lead to violent outbursts. SCP personnel are advised to use caution when approaching SCP-2386 and to keep all interactions brief and to the point.
