Item #: SCP-5399

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5399 is to be housed in a standard living quarters within Site-17, with access to a personal computer and internet connection to fulfill its programming duties. SCP-5399 is to wear a Class-C amnestic patch during social events to ensure the secrecy of the Foundation's existence.

Description: SCP-5399 is a human male with spotted facial features, and a frightened mouth. SCP-5399 wears normal glasses with a jade earring on one ear and a gold chain around its neck. SCP-5399 is typically seen holding a candy stick as it works. When not working, SCP-5399 displays a playful personality, often surprising others with dance moves and silly antics.

SCP-5399 works as a programmer for Foundation software, displaying a strong sense of duty and responsibility in its duties. However, SCP-5399 is known to loosen up and have fun during social events, often gaining attention from others for its humorous behavior. Despite this behavior, SCP-5399 is to be monitored when in public to avoid breaching the Foundation's secrecy protocols.

Addendum: SCP-5399 exhibited anomalous behavior during a social gathering, displaying the ability to levitate objects through a telekinetic force.
