Item #: SCP-8234

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8234 is to be monitored at all times during deliveries and any abnormal behavior is to be reported immediately. SCP-8234 is to be allowed to continue their normal duties as a delivery driver under the guise of a non-anomalous human being.

Description: SCP-8234 is a female human delivery driver. They possess uncommon facial features with orange stripes, blue clown eyes, and a metal blue head. SCP-8234 also has jade earrings and is always seen wearing a silver chain and smoking a cigarette.

SCP-8234 exhibits a strong sense of intuition that can appear to be psychic in nature. They possess the ability to predict events that have yet to occur and sense when someone is experiencing heightened emotional states such as anxiety or distress. SCP-8234's abilities are a source of both amazement and unease to those who encounter them.

Addendum: SCP-8234's abilities have been monitored and analyzed, and while the origin of their ability remains unknown, it is recommended that caution be exercised when interacting with them. SCP-8234 has demonstrated a willingness to cooperate with the Foundation and has offered useful information regarding the supernatural community. Continued observation is advised.
