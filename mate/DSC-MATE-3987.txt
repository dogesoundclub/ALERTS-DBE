Item #: SCP-3987

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3987 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Any attempts to communicate with or interview SCP-3987 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel. SCP-3987 is to be provided with a variety of books and puzzles to keep their mind occupied.

Description: SCP-3987 is a humanoid individual of the female sex, with several distinctive features. They have orange stripes across their face, with purple lips and red clown eyes. SCP-3987's hair is dyed with a rainbow festival pattern, and they wear goggles on their forehead and a green cross earring on their left ear. They are often seen wearing a silver chain around their neck.

Despite their playful appearance, SCP-3987 is known for their ambitious, goal-oriented nature. They possess exceptional organizational skills and exhibit outstanding strategic thinking, allowing them to take on more than they can handle and still succeed. SCP-3987 is often considered to be a villain, as their goals often involve unethical or criminal activity.

SCP-3987 is highly intelligent and manipulative, and has been known to use this to their advantage when interacting with Foundation personnel. They possess an uncanny ability to read people and can quickly assess a situation, making them a formidable opponent. SCP-3987 is also known to be incredibly patient, biding their time and waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

Addendum: SCP-3987 has recently managed to breach containment and is currently at large. All personnel are to remain vigilant and report any sightings immediately. It is unknown what SCP-3987's endgame is or what their ultimate goal may be, but it is believed to be of great importance.
