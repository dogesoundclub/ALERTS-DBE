Item #: DSC-MATE-8641

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-8641 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell, with a specialized ventilation system that can withstand its unique requirements. DSC-MATE-8641's gas mask must be worn at all times, as its breath contains a toxin that can cause violent hallucinations in humans. In the event of a containment breach, lethal force is authorized.

Description: DSC-MATE-8641 is a male rabbit approximately 1.2 meters tall. It displays a number of anomalous properties, including the ability to speak, manipulate its environment with extreme precision and judgement, and emit a toxin through its breath. DSC-MATE-8641 has bright red clown eyes and a frightened mouth, giving it an unsettling appearance.

DSC-MATE-8641 was initially discovered working as a train driver on a subway system in [REDACTED], where it had been employed for an unknown amount of time. The Foundation was alerted to its presence when multiple workers suffered from violent hallucinations whenever near DSC-MATE-8641. The rabbit was apprehended with extreme force, causing significant damage to the subway system.

DSC-MATE-8641 tends to be highly analytical and logical, often arguing for the fun of it. Its debating skills have often led it into humorous situations, such as heated arguments over unimportant topics. Despite its jovial nature, DSC-MATE-8641 should be considered highly dangerous due to its toxic breath and unpredictable behavior.
