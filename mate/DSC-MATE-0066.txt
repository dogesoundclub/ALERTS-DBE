Item #: SCP-66

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-66 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell. Access to SCP-66 is strictly limited to Level 3 personnel and above. All interactions with SCP-66 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel. Any incidents involving SCP-66 must be reported immediately to the Site Director.

Description: SCP-66 is a human male with spotted skin and a permanent smile. SCP-66 wears large nerd glasses and a medical mask covering his mouth. SCP-66's head is covered in a metal purple material, and he wears a green cross earring in his left ear. SCP-66 also wears a silver chain as an accessory.

SCP-66's personality is highly empathetic and compassionate, but can also be exhausting due to his tendency to take on other people's problems as his own. SCP-66 believes that it is his responsibility to fix everything and everyone around him. This trait can be both admirable and dangerous when not monitored properly.

SCP-66 has been observed exhibiting abilities that suggest paranormal abilities, but this has not been confirmed. SCP-66 has been known to manipulate emotions and situations to his advantage. He possesses a keen intellect and is highly manipulative, making him a highly dangerous and unpredictable villain.

SCP-66 has been involved in numerous criminal activities, including extortion, theft, and murder. SCP-66's motives are unknown, but it is believed that he enjoys the feeling of power and control he has over others.

SCP-66's status as a highly dangerous villain warrants close monitoring and containment at all times.
