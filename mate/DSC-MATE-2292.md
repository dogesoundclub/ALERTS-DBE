DSC-MATE-2292 possesses a red scarf and jade earring, which it claims are important to its personal identity. The reason for these accessories and DSC-MATE-2292's background remain unknown.

However, caution must be exercised when using DSC-MATE-2292 in the field, as its outgoing personality can potentially reveal its anomalous nature and the existence of the Foundation to outside parties.
