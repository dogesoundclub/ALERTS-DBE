Item#: 7002

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7002 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell, with adequate medical equipment and provisions available on site. All personnel interacting with SCP-7002 are to wear standard protective gear to prevent possible contamination.

Description: SCP-7002 is a human male, identified as a doctor by profession. His face is characterized by a frightened mouth, and his eyes resemble ski goggles. SCP-7002 is always seen wearing a bucket hat and a jade earring on each ear. His mouth is covered with a medical mask, and he wears a red scarf as an accessory.

SCP-7002's personality is marked by absent-mindedness and forgetfulness, often losing track of time and appointments. While he is capable of working effectively, he may forget important details, leading to humorous situations.

SCP-7002 exhibits no anomalous properties or abilities, and is entirely mundane in nature. However, due to his profession, it is advised that SCP-7002 is treated with respect and caution when dealing with medical matters.

Addendum: SCP-7002 was recovered from a hospital in [REDACTED], where he was mistakenly considered a patient exhibiting anomalous behavior. SCP Foundation investigators were able to identify SCP-7002 and safely contain him, preventing any loss of medical information or contamination.
