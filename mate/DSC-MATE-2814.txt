Item #: 2814

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2814 is currently contained within an office building located in downtown [REDACTED], and is to be monitored regularly by Foundation personnel. Due to his harmless nature, no additional measures are required at this time.

Description: SCP-2814 is a human male, identified as a lawyer by profession. He appears to be in his late twenties to early thirties, and is known to possess a vivid imagination and creative tendencies, often delving into unusual hobbies and interests. Notably, SCP-2814 sports orange stripes on his face and displays a frightened mouth.

SCP-2814 retains a normal human head of blonde hair and wears a pair of normal glasses over its eyes. Additionally, he is known to wear a pair of goggles on his forehead and a jade earring on each ear. SCP-2814 may also be seen holding a candy stick, which he carries as a form of comfort.

Personality-wise, SCP-2814 is known to be harmless and cooperative, and displays no signs of aggression towards Foundation staff. It thrives on novelty and continues to push boundaries with its creative pursuits, which include writing poetry, creating elaborate cosplay costumes, and collecting unusual objects.

Addendum: SCP-2814 has expressed interest in collaborating with Foundation personnel for creative projects, in exchange for additional allowances and resources. This request is currently under review by O5 Command.
