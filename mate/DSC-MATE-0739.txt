Item #: 739

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-739 is to be allowed to continue their employment as a theater manager, with monthly psychological evaluations to monitor their mental health. SCP-739 is not to be allowed contact with any anomalous persons or objects.

Description: SCP-739 is a human female with bright orange stripes across their face and a permanent smile. SCP-739's eyes are red clown eyes, and their head is that of a pink rabbit. They have a two-color band on their forehead and goggles resting on their forehead. SCP-739 wears a gold earring on one ear.

SCP-739 exhibits a nurturing and caring personality, often treating their friends and family as if they were their own children. This behavior can lead to humorous situations, such as SCP-739 providing "parental" guidance to adult individuals. SCP-739 is cooperative with Foundation personnel and has not displayed any anomalous abilities or behaviors.

Addendum: SCP-739 has expressed an interest in helping with Foundation outreach programs in local communities. This request is pending approval from higher-level personnel.
