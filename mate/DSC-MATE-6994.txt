Item #: 6994

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6994 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell. All conversations between SCP-6994 and research personnel are to be recorded and monitored for safety. SCP-6994 is allowed access to designated recreational areas within the facility.

Description: SCP-6994 is a female human of average height and weight. However, SCP-6994 has unique facial features that include orange stripes on her cheeks and a red lip, as well as blue clown eyes. SCP-6994 often wears a tiara and a choker accessory. 

SCP-6994's personality traits tend to be very outgoing and social, as she enjoys being the center of attention. This often results in humorous situations where SCP-6994 tells jokes or stories that are incredibly funny, leading to uncontrollable laughter among those present.

SCP-6994 is a successful Real Estate agent, and when not within Foundation custody, is known to attend social events for her profession. SCP-6994 has no anomalous effects on individuals within close proximity.

Addendum: During Incident-6994-A, SCP-6994 was observed to have temporarily lost control of her anomalous effect leading to the deaths of four civilians. Further research into SCP-6994's anomalous effect is ongoing.
