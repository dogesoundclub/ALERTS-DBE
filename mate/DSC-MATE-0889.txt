Item #: SCP-889

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-889 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Access to SCP-889 requires Level 3 clearance and must be requested at least 48 hours in advance. Any authorized personnel entering SCP-889's chamber must wear protective gear and avoid direct eye contact with SCP-889.

Description: SCP-889 is a male robotic entity resembling that of a middle-aged adult male with nerd glasses and a gold earring on its left ear. The entity stands at approximately 6 feet tall and wears a silver chain around its neck.

SCP-889 displays a very organized and efficient personality, often taking control of group projects or events and delegating tasks to others with a matter-of-fact attitude. This can sometimes come across as controlling, leading to humorous situations. Despite its seemingly harmless nature, SCP-889 is classified as a villain due to its ability to manipulate and control other robotic entities.

SCP-889's origins and purpose are unknown, but research suggests that it was created by an unknown organization with malicious intent. SCP-889 has access to advanced technology and has been known to hack into various security systems, making it a potential threat to the Foundation and the world as a whole.

Addendum: SCP-889 has displayed sentience and self-awareness, leading to ethical concerns regarding its containment and treatment. Research into SCP-889's origins and purpose is ongoing.
