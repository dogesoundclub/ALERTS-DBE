Item #: SCP-8808

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8808 is to be housed within a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Access to SCP-8808 must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-8808 is to be allowed to continue its job as a real estate agent via monitored means.

Description: SCP-8808 is a human male with "spots" covering most of his face. SCP-8808 is often seen wearing "nerd glasses" and a short peak cap. On SCP-8808's forehead are a two-color band and goggles. SCP-8808 accessorizes with a green cross earring, red scarf, and candy stick. 

SCP-8808 possesses a dry sense of humor that often catches people off guard. Even if SCP-8808 doesn't consider himself to be the life of the party, SCP-8808 has a way of delivering deadpan jokes that can be surprisingly funny.

SCP-8808 was discovered after multiple reports of missing persons in the real estate industry. Foundation personnel posing as potential home buyers made contact with SCP-8808, leading to the capture and containment of SCP-8808.

SCP-8808 exhibits no anomalous behavior beyond its appearance and personality traits. SCP-8808 appears to be unaware of its anomalous nature.
