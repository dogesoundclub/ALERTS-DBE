Item #: 3804

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-3804 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment unit at Site-██. Any interactions with DSC-MATE-3804 must be carried out via two-way communication system or remotely controlled drones. Any requests made by DSC-MATE-3804 must be approved by at least two level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-3804 is a humanoid robot of unknown origin which outwardly resembled a news anchor. DSC-MATE-3804 displays the features of a middle-aged male with one green cyclopean eye and a "frightened" mouth. DSC-MATE-3804 has normal glasses for eyes that can be replaced for compatibility. DSC-MATE-3804 wears a purple cap with a headset, a silver chain, gold earring, and an LED gas mask as accessories.

DSC-MATE-3804 appears to enjoy getting others excited about various subjects and tends to be very energetic and enthusiastic. It had shown a preference for news reporting and giving reports on any available information. Such reports are constant, and DSC-MATE-3804 will become adamant in seeking stories or news to report on. Any attempts to suspend this behaviour will lead to extreme and erratic behaviour from DSC-MATE-3804, which can lead to containment breaches, violence towards DSC staff and equipment, and significant damage to the worksite. DSC-MATE-3804 exhibits a humanoid, sentient personality, but its cognitive processes are unknown.

Addendum: Incident Log

On ██, ██, 20██, DSC-MATE-3804, having broken containment protocols, was discovered in the newsroom of a broadcasting network in ████████, USA. Upon sight of Foundation Agents, DSC-MATE-3804 attacked the Agents and caused significant damage before being subdued. In the debriefing, DSC-MATE-3804 stated it had "discovered a report" and believed it was "vital" to the "integrity of the news". DSC-MATE-3804 was given supplementary care and psychological counseling. DSC-MATE-3804 was also implanted with a device that would alert the Foundation of any significant stress or outbursts.
