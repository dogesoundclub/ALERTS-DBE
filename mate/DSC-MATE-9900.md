Item #: 9900

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-9900 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. Any interaction with DSC-MATE-9900 must be done through a two-way communication system in the cell. No personnel are allowed to enter the cell without permission from Level 4 personnel. DSC-MATE-9900's cigarettes are to be replaced with non-toxic ones to prevent any fire hazards.

Description: DSC-MATE-9900 appears to be a male monkey with a frightened mouth as its main facial feature. Its eyes are modified ski goggles and its head is covered with a red beanie. DSC-MATE-9900 has a single jade earring on its left ear and a red scarf around its neck. DSC-MATE-9900 is often seen smoking a cigarette.

DSC-MATE-9900 displays a highly analytical and logical personality. It enjoys engaging in debates and arguments with others. This tendency leads it to engage in humorous situations, such as debating topics that others find unimportant or trivial. 

DSC-MATE-9900's abilities include telekinesis and the ability to teleport to any location it has previously visited. It is unknown how DSC-MATE-9900 acquired these abilities.

DSC-MATE-9900 is classified as a villain as it has been observed to use its abilities for personal gain. It has been reported to rob convenience stores and steal from individuals. It displays a cunning and mischievous side, often playing pranks on the staff assigned to its containment.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-9900 has shown signs of being cooperative with the Foundation in exchange for certain privileges. However, personnel must exercise caution when interacting with DSC-MATE-9900 as it is known to be unpredictable and manipulative.
