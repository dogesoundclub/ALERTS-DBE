Item #: SCP-5900

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5900 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Due to SCP-5900's benign nature, no additional security measures are required. Interaction with SCP-5900 is permitted for research purposes, as long as it does not interfere with its job as a delivery driver.

Description: SCP-5900 appears as a human female with blue clown eyes and a contagious smile. Her head is adorned with a tiara, and a two-color band is present on her forehead. SCP-5900 is often seen wearing a silver chain as an accessory. Despite its anomalous traits, SCP-5900 otherwise appears to be a normal human.

SCP-5900 displays a remarkable sense of empathy, being highly attuned to the emotions of others. In many situations, SCP-5900's abilities can diffuse tense or awkward situations, as it always knows the perfect thing to say. SCP-5900's humorous disposition allows it to help others find humor in challenging situations, even in the face of danger.

It is currently unknown how SCP-5900 obtained its anomalous properties. SCP-5900 is highly cooperative, and willingly shares its knowledge and experiences with research personnel. Due to SCP-5900's gentle personality, Foundation personnel are encouraged to approach SCP-5900 in a friendly manner.
