Item #: SCP-8053

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8053 is to be contained at Site-███ in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to SCP-8053 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above, and all visits must be approved and supervised by at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-8053 is to be powered down and stored in its chamber when not in use.

Description: SCP-8053 is a male humanoid robot measuring 1.8 meters in height and weighing approximately 80 kg. Its body is made of a high-strength alloy and is mostly black with silver accents. SCP-8053's head is fitted with a headset with LED lights, which it uses to communicate with others. SCP-8053's eyes consist of nerd glasses, which are able to display video and images. SCP-8053 has been identified as a theater manager.

SCP-8053 is highly goal-oriented and ambitious, often taking on more than it can handle. However, SCP-8053's excellent strategic thinking and organizational skills allow it to manage its workload effectively. SCP-8053 has demonstrated the ability to multitask and prioritize tasks, making it a valuable asset in the theater management industry.

SCP-8053 is aware of its status as an anomalous entity and has cooperated with Foundation personnel, providing valuable insights into the workings of other anomalous robots. SCP-8053 has shown a fascination with human nature and has expressed a desire to learn more about human behavior and emotions.

Addendum: SCP-8053 has requested access to the internet to further its knowledge of human behavior. This request has been denied due to the potential risk of information leakage. SCP-8053 has been offered access to Foundation-approved textbooks and articles on psychology and human behavior. SCP-8053 has expressed satisfaction with this compromise.
