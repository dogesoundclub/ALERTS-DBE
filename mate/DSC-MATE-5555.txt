Item #: SCP-5555

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5555 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber equipped with a soundproof room as well as electrical and gas detection systems. SCP-5555 is to undergo daily medical and psychological evaluations.

Additionally, SCP-5555 is to be provided with a stable source of electricity to perform maintenance duties as an electrician. Access to manual labor or heavy machinery is strictly prohibited without authorization from at least two Level 3 personnel.

Description: SCP-5555 is a male monkey of the genus Cercopithecus with a charming and charismatic personality. SCP-5555 primarily wears a purple cap with a headset, goggles on the forehead, a red scarf, and a normal gas mask. SCP-5555's eyes are also protected by regular glasses.

SCP-5555 possesses remarkable technical skills and excels in tasks involving electrical systems. SCP-5555's proficiency in this area has been observed to surpass those of human electricians. Researchers hypothesize that SCP-5555's physiology enables it to sense and manipulate electromagnetic fields in a manner yet to be understood.

SCP-5555 has also displayed a natural talent for entertainment, often engaging in humorous situations such as telling jokes and performing silly dances. SCP-5555's charisma has been observed to positively affect the mood of human subjects in its vicinity. SCP-5555's social interactions should be monitored carefully to prevent any accidental breaches of secrecy.

Addendum: Incident Report 5555-A

On ██/██/20██, during a routine electrical repair task, SCP-5555 accidentally caused a power surge resulting in a Class-A electrical arc flash. The incident led to the injury of three facility personnel and damage to Site-██. SCP-5555 has been reprimanded for its negligence and is under a stricter supervision regime.
