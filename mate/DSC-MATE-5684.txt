Item #: SCP-5684

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5684 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell furnished for a banker. Standard amenities are to be provided, including a bed, toilet, sink, and writing desk. The security clearance of all personnel entering the containment cell should be level 3 or higher. SCP-5684 is to be monitored 24/7 with a camera, which must have a direct feed to the nearest security control room. Any unusual activity is to be reported immediately to the on-duty supervisor.

Description: SCP-5684 is a human female identified as a banker. It has spots on its face, red lips, orange eyebrows, and goggles on its forehead. SCP-5684 also wears a silver chain, and it has a jade earring on each ear. Its eyes are also orange, and its hairstyle is a classic bobcut.

SCP-5684 may have a laid-back and easy-going demeanor, but it can also be fiercely loyal and protective of its loved ones. Research has shown that SCP-5684's personality can lead to some humorous situations, such as getting into a heated argument with someone who insults a friend or family member.

At present, SCP-5684 exhibits no anomalous behavior or ability; however, it remains under Foundation observation to ensure no anomalous traits manifest in SCP-5684. It is being monitored and observed for any behavior or changes that may indicate the need for further containment.
