Item #: SCP-6336

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6336 is to be kept in a secured location at all times. Personnel are only permitted to enter SCP-6336's containment area with Level 2+ clearance and must wear proper protective gear. SCP-6336 must be checked regularly for signs of unusual behavior.

Description: SCP-6336 appears to be a female human with orange stripes on their face and red lips. The eyes are blue clown eyes, which emanate a hypnotic effect, making those who look into them feel dizzy and disoriented.

SCP-6336's head is metallic blue with a two-color band on its forehead, and its ears are adorned with gold earrings. SCP-6336 is seen to wear a medical mask and a choker as accessories. SCP-6336 has a charismatic personality, which can lead to many humorous situations.

SCP-6336 works as a security guard but shows signs of being ill-suited for the job. SCP-6336 demonstrates a particular interest in entertaining people and making them happy. SCP-6336's behavior can be unpredictable, leading to a potential danger to those around it.

Addendum: SCP-6336's behavior has become increasingly erratic in the latest months despite no apparent changes in the containment procedures. Investigation into the cause of these changes is ongoing.
