Item #: SCP-6864

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-6864 has been granted Level 2 clearance by the Foundation and is allowed to continue practicing medicine under standard medical laws and regulations. SCP-6864 is to be provided with necessary medical supplies to conduct their duties efficiently. Any contact with SCP-6864 is to be recorded and monitored for potential anomalous activity.

Description: SCP-6864 is a male rabbit of the species Oryctolagus cuniculus. SCP-6864 wears a blue bandana around their head and carries a candy stick as their primary accessory. The color of their eyes is unknown.

SCP-6864 is a practicing Doctor with a strong sense of duty and responsibility. They are prone to taking on more than they can handle, leading to humorous situations. SCP-6864's duties include general medicine and treatment of anomalous afflictions.

SCP-6864's anomalous abilities have not been fully studied or identified. However, it has been observed that SCP-6864's presence can calm individuals experiencing heightened emotions or anxiety.

Addendum: SCP-6864 has been deemed safe for standard medical practice by the Foundation's medical experts. Further testing into SCP-6864's anomalies is ongoing.
