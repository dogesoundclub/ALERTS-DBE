Item #: SCP-5230

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5230 is to be kept in a secure, designated living quarters on-site. Access to SCP-5230 is only permitted with Level 2 clearance, and interactions with SCP-5230 must be kept to a minimum.

Description: SCP-5230 is a human female identified as a beautician by profession. SCP-5230's physical appearance features orange stripes and purple lips on their face, and orange eyebrows with green hair in a purple cap on their head. SCP-5230 also has jade earrings and wears a medical mask over their mouth.

SCP-5230's personality is characterized by a love for adventure and a willingness to take risks. SCP-5230 enjoys trying new things and has been known to engage in dangerous activities such as bungee jumping and wilderness exploration. SCP-5230 has survived several near-death experiences and has shared humorous stories about these incidents.

SCP-5230's anomalous properties are currently unknown, but extensive testing is ongoing. Initial observations have shown that SCP-5230 has no apparent physical abnormalities or heightened abilities compared to a standard human.

Addendum: Interview Log-5230-A

Interviewer: Dr. ██████
Interviewed: SCP-5230

<Begin Log>

Dr. ██████: Good afternoon, SCP-5230. How are you feeling today?

SCP-5230: Oh, hi there! I'm doing great, thanks for asking.

Dr. ██████: Can you tell me about your love for adventure?

SCP-5230: Of course! I just love trying new things and taking risks. It's so much fun to push myself to the limit and see what I'm capable of.

Dr. ██████: Have your adventures ever led to any dangerous situations?

SCP-5230: Oh, definitely. I've had a few close calls, but I always manage to come out on top. I've got some great stories to tell from those experiences, too.

Dr. ██████: Do you think your love for adventure is related to any anomalous properties you may possess?

SCP-5230: Anomalous properties? What are you talking about?

Dr. ██████: Never mind. That will be all for today.

<End Log>

Closing Statement: Further testing is required to determine if SCP-5230's love for adventure is related to any anomalous abilities. For now, SCP-5230's containment procedures remain the same.
