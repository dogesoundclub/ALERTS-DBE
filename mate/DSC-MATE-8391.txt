Item #: SCP-8391

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8391 is to be contained within a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to SCP-8391 must be authorized by at least one Level 3 personnel. Interaction with SCP-8391 is restricted to research purposes only.

Description: SCP-8391 is a male human, aged 34 years old, with a distinctive mouth spot on the left side of his face. SCP-8391 commonly wears a medical mask to cover his mouth, and a purple cap with a headset. SCP-8391 also has a silver chain around his neck that he never removes.

SCP-8391 is a car salesman by profession, and is known for his remarkable organizational skills and strategic thinking. SCP-8391 is highly ambitious, and his goal-oriented nature drives him to take on tasks that often exceed his capabilities. Nevertheless, he manages to complete them successfully, much to the amazement of his peers.

SCP-8391 also wears a goggle on his forehead, the function of which is currently unknown. SCP-8391 has refused to remove the goggles, citing personal reasons, and efforts towards further investigation have been fruitless thus far.

Addendum: SCP-8391 has exhibited no anomalous properties or behaviors, apart from his tendency to take on tasks that are beyond his skill and ability. SCP-8391 has cooperated with Foundation personnel, although he appears to be guarded and secretive about his personal life. Further investigation is ongoing.
