Item #: 1040

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 1040 (also known as "The Train Driver") is to be monitored at all times by at least two armed personnel. Subject is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell with all personal items removed. Any requests made by subject must first be approved by a Level 3 personnel. 

Description: 

Subject 1040 is a male human of average height and weight. His most noticeable facial features are the numerous spots covering his cheeks and a clown mouth. He also has blue clown-like eyes and wears a short peak cap on his head with goggles resting on his forehead. 

Subject is often seen wearing a gold earring on his left ear and a silver chain around his neck. He is known to carry around a candy stick as a form of stress relief. 

Subject displays a creative and artistic side, often expressing himself through various hobbies such as painting or writing. Despite his quirky appearance and demeanor, subject is highly intelligent and cunning, making it difficult to predict his actions. 

Subject currently works as a train driver and uses his position to subtly gather information and influence those around him. It is unknown if subject possesses any supernatural abilities, although caution is advisable when dealing with him.
