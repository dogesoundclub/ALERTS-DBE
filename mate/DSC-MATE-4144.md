Item #: DSC-MATE-4144

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4144 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-███. Access to DSC-MATE-4144 is only granted with Level 2 clearance or higher. DSC-MATE-4144 must wear a standard gas mask at all times when outside of its containment chamber.

Description: DSC-MATE-4144 is a female human of unknown age, standing at 172 cm tall and weighing 73 kg. DSC-MATE-4144's most notable features are its red clown eyes and orange stripes on its face. Its head is covered in green hair and wears a purple cap. It also has a green cross earring and a silver chain around its neck.

Despite its odd appearance, DSC-MATE-4144 is a trained firefighter with no prior criminal history. Researchers have noted that DSC-MATE-4144 tends to be very nurturing and caring, extending this trait to inanimate objects as well. DSC-MATE-4144 may name its car, talk to plants or even treat household appliances like sentient beings.

DSC-MATE-4144's presence has been linked to several anomalous occurrences, including fires that reach temperatures exceeding 1,000 degrees Celsius in a short amount of time. Its ability to cause and control these fires is still unknown.

Addendum: Interview Log 4144-1

Interviewer: Dr. ████

DSC-MATE-4144: [wearing a gas mask] Hi there! I'm DSC-MATE-4144. Nice to meet you.

Dr. ████: Can you tell us about your ability to cause and control fires?

DSC-MATE-4144: Oh, that! Yeah, I don't really know how it works. It's just something that happens. Kinda like when I talk to plants, they just seem to grow better.

Dr. ████: What do you mean?

DSC-MATE-4144: Well, sometimes, when I talk to them, they just seem to perk up, you know? And I can almost feel their energy. Same goes for the fires. I just feel this energy, and I can make things happen.

Dr. ████: Do you understand how dangerous your abilities can be?

DSC-MATE-4144: Of course! That's why I became a firefighter. I want to use my abilities to help people.

Closing Statement: DSC-MATE-4144's ability to control fires is still unknown. Further study is required to understand the full scope of its abilities.
