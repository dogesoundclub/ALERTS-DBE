Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-4543 is to be kept in a secure, climate-controlled chamber within Site-██. Access to DSC-MATE-4543 is restricted to Level 3 personnel and above. Any experimentation on DSC-MATE-4543 must be done with prior approval from at least two Level 4 personnel.

Its most prominent feature is its single green cyclops eye, which appears to function as both a camera and a laser. DSC-MATE-4543 has a robotic body, with various compartments and ports on its frame. Its head is usually adorned with a Doge hat, while its eyes are protected by a pair of nerd glasses.

DSC-MATE-4543’s programming allows it to perform various tasks, such as data entry, programming, and other computing-related work. It has a highly detail-oriented personality, sometimes to the point of being obsessive about organizing its surroundings. DSC-MATE-4543 has been observed alphabetizing its spice rack, color-coding its closet, and straightening objects on desks, among other things.

The most notable feature of DSC-MATE-4543’s appearance is its LED gas mask, which appears to function as a filter for the air it breathes. DSC-MATE-4543 claims that the mask is necessary for it to be able to function correctly, but its exact purpose is unknown.

DSC-MATE-4543 is generally cooperative with Foundation personnel and appears to enjoy being given tasks to complete. However, caution should be exercised when assigning tasks to DSC-MATE-4543, as its obsession with detail can sometimes lead to unexpected and unwanted consequences.
