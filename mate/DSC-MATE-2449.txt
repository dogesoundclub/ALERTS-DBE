Item #: SCP-2449

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2449 is to be kept under surveillance at all times, and is only to be allowed to work in fast food establishments owned by the Foundation. SCP-2449 is to be kept separate from non-Foundation employees at all times, particularly during meal breaks, and is not to interact with customers. SCP-2449 is not to be allowed access to any foodstuffs that have not been provided and approved by the Foundation. Any breaches of these containment procedures are to be reported immediately to Site Security, and SCP-2449 is to be reprimanded accordingly.

Description: SCP-2449 appears to be a standard human male, approximately 25 years of age, with normal facial features except for orange stripes across the face. SCP-2449 wears normal glasses, has blonde hair, and wears a medical mask over the face. The forehead of SCP-2449 has a two-color band, and they wear a green cross earring in each ear. SCP-2449 also wears a gold chain around the neck.  SCP-2449 has shown no signs of anomalous abilities, but has demonstrated an ability to entertain and amuse others in a lighthearted manner.

Personality: SCP-2449 values harmony and social connections, demonstrating a friendly and jovial nature. SCP-2449 possesses an unusual ability to uplift and entertain those around them, causing them to become more relaxed and cooperative. SCP-2449 has a goofy side that manifests in the form of jokes or playful banter with others. SCP-2449 is not considered a threat but is to be monitored to ensure they do not use their personality traits to cause breaches in Foundation security. 

Note: SCP-2449 has shown remarkable prowess in the use of fast food technology, making them an invaluable asset to the Foundation in the fast food industry.
