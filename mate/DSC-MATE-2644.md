Item #: DSC-MATE-2644

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2644 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. Any and all visitors must be accompanied by at least two armed guards during any interview or observation sessions.

Description: DSC-MATE-2644, also known as "Rainbow Firefighter," is a female human with unique physical characteristics. She has purple lips, jade earrings, and a silver chain as well as Virtual Reality Rainbow LED eyes. Her head is adorned with Rainbow Festival-themed headgear and she wears an LED gas mask as an accessory.

DSC-MATE-2644 is a professional firefighter with a laid-back and easy-going demeanor. Her colleagues have described her as fiercely loyal and fiercely protective of her loved ones. In recent interviews, she has expressed that she is willing to risk anything to save the lives of innocent people.

DSC-MATE-2644's appearance and accessories have caused several incidents during containment. She has been known to engage in heated arguments with any individuals who insult or attempt to remove any of her accessories. Additionally, her LED gas mask has reportedly caused several security breaches in the past, as it interferes with containment cameras and security equipment.

On [REDACTED], DSC-MATE-2644's LED gas mask caused the communication system between her and the containment team to fail. DSC-MATE-2644 then escaped containment, leading to a five-hour-long search throughout the facility.
