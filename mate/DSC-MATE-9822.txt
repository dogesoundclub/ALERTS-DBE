SCP Foundation Description:

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9822 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber with access to a cigarette dispenser and other basic amenities. Due to its empathic abilities, it is to be monitored for any signs of emotional distress or instability. Any interactions with personnel are to be recorded and reviewed by Foundation psychologists.

Description: SCP-9822 appears to be a female human of average height and build. It has distinguishing orange stripes on its cheeks and a purple lip. It wears a choker and is often seen with a cigarette in hand. Its occupation is as an electrician.

SCP-9822 possesses a strong sense of empathy and is highly attuned to the emotions of others. This ability has both positive and negative effects. It can be helpful in diffusing tense situations or cheering up fellow personnel, but it can also lead to emotional exhaustion and instability if not properly managed.

SCP-9822's personality is generally friendly and easygoing, with a tendency towards humor and wit. However, it is important to note that its empathic abilities can sometimes lead to manipulation or coercion of others. Foundation personnel are reminded to maintain a professional boundary and not to share sensitive information with SCP-9822 without clearance from their superiors.

Overall, SCP-9822 is considered a low-risk anomaly but should still be monitored and contained according to Foundation standards. Any changes in behavior or manifestation of anomalous abilities should be reported immediately.
