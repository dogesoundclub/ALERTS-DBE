Item #: SCP-1358

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-1358 is to be held in a standard humanoid containment cell at all times. Any interactions with SCP-1358 must be approved by at least two Level 4 personnel, and all conversations must be recorded for analysis. SCP-1358 is not to be allowed to attend any public events or meetings without prior approval from the Ethics Committee.

Description: SCP-1358 is a humanoid entity identified as a female human politician. SCP-1358 displays two distinct physical features: spots that cover a majority of its facial area and bright red lips. SCP-1358's eyes are unnaturally bright green and appear to be electrical in nature, as if they are VR Jade LED.

SCP-1358 is often seen wearing a hoodie and a medical mask around its mouth and nose. A gold chain is frequently noticed hanging around the neck of SCP-1358.

SCP-1358 is highly organized and enjoys planning ahead. However, it possesses a spontaneous side that has resulted in some humorous situations. SCP-1358 has been known to surprise its colleagues with impromptu adventures or throw last-minute parties that turn out to be successful.

SCP-1358's motivations are currently unknown, and it has shown to be highly secretive about its personal life. Further research and analysis are necessary to determine its capabilities and true intentions.
