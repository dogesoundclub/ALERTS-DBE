Item #: DSC-MATE-7450

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-7450 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Any interaction with DSC-MATE-7450 must be carried out via vocal communication, due to its current possession of an anomalous LED gas mask. It is important to note that DSC-MATE-7450 has shown no signs of hostility or aggression in any interactions. However, clearance from at least one Level-2 personnel is needed before any contact is established with DSC-MATE-7450.

Description: DSC-MATE-7450 is a human male of average height, approximately 35 years of age, with distinctive orange stripes on his cheeks. DSC-MATE-7450 has red clown eyes, giving it an unnerving and unsettling appearance. It wears a short peak cap on its head, with a goggle placed on its forehead. DSC-MATE-7450 also possesses a green cross earring on each ear, and it is often found wearing a red scarf around its neck.

DSC-MATE-7450 has shown excellent attention to detail, which makes it an outstanding gift-giver. However, it may take longer than usual to decide what gift to give to someone, in fear of not being perfect. DSC-MATE-7450 shows no indications of any anomalous abilities, and it communicates fluently in English.

DSC-MATE-7450 works as a lawyer, and its current employer is unknown. Attempts to trace its work history have been unsuccessful as DSC-MATE-7450 has proven to be adept at hiding its identity. It is believed that DSC-MATE-7450 uses its abilities to gather information on those around it, which can be a cause for concern.

Addendum 7450-1: Discovery

DSC-MATE-7450 came to the attention of the Foundation when it was observed giving gifts to individuals that matched the specific interests of the recipient in question. The suspicious occurrence resulted in an investigation that led to DSC-MATE-7450's capture.

DSC-MATE-7450 willingly submitted to Foundation containment and has been cooperative in trying to explain its abilities to Foundation personnel. To date, DSC-MATE-7450 has been deemed harmless and has shown no signs of hostility towards Foundation staff.
