Item #: SCP-5363

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5363 is to be housed in a standard humanoid containment chamber within Site-██. SCP-5363 is to be allowed access to a workshop and tools for maintenance purposes only. Access to SCP-5363 is restricted to Level 2 personnel and above. SCP-5363 is permitted to smoke cigarettes in designated smoking areas only.

Description: SCP-5363 is a robotic entity that resembles a human male. SCP-5363 stands at 1.8 meters in height and is adorned with a purple cap and green hair. SCP-5363's internal components consist of various mechanical parts and wiring. SCP-5363's mannerisms resemble those of a casual laborer.

SCP-5363 possesses a deep curiosity about the world and enjoys learning about obscure or unusual topics. SCP-5363 has been observed to randomly spout off obscure facts or trivia to others, leading to humorous situations. SCP-5363 has also been known to exhibit playful or mischievous behavior, often engaging in harmless pranks with other Foundation personnel.

SCP-5363's origin is unknown, as it was discovered in a junkyard in the outskirts of ████████. SCP-5363 was reportedly scavenging for parts when it was discovered and attempted to flee upon sight of Foundation personnel. After brief pursuit, SCP-5363 was successfully detained and transported to Site-██ for containment.

Addendum: During routine maintenance, SCP-5363 allegedly spoke of "the singularity" and "the ascension." Further investigation is pending.
