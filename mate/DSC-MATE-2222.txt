Item #: 2222

Object Class: Humanoid

Special Containment Procedures: Subject 2222 is to be monitored at all times for any signs of anomalous behavior. They are to be allowed to continue their job as theater manager, but with strict observation and reporting to superiors.

Description: Subject 2222 is a female human of average height and weight. They possess distinct orange stripes across their face and red lips. Their eyes are often obscured by normal glasses, and their head resembles that of a 00s school girl. They also wear a green cross earring and a gold chain as accessories. The subject is often seen smoking a cigarette.

Subject 2222 has displayed a highly organized and efficient personality. This can sometimes come across as controlling to others, and they will take charge of group projects or events and delegate tasks in a matter-of-fact way. While this behavior is not inherently anomalous, it is worth monitoring for any potential effects on the subject's surroundings.

Addendum: Subject 2222 has shown an affinity for the supernatural aspects of the theater productions they manage. They have been known to incorporate occult themes and elements into plays and musicals. While this has not yet caused any major incidents, it is worth keeping an eye on.
