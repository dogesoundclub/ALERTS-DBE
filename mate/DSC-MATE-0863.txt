Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-863 is to be kept under strict surveillance at all times, and is to be detained within a standard humanoid containment cell. Any attempt to remove SCP-863's head or earring is strictly prohibited.

Description: SCP-863 appears to be a female security guard with a unique appearance. SCP-863 possesses blue clown eyes, a pink rabbit head, and green cross earrings. SCP-863's mouth is obscured by a medical mask, and they frequently wear a black choker as an accessory.

SCP-863 displays a natural charm and charisma, capable of captivating large groups of people with ease. They frequently tell jokes and do silly dances to entertain others, often leading to humorous situations. However, SCP-863 is also known to display aggressive tendencies when provoked.

SCP-863's unique appearance has caused great distress among Foundation staff and the general public, leading to increased security measures to prevent any unwanted attention or potential anomalies from arising.

Addendum: Due to SCP-863's ability to charm and manipulate individuals, all personnel are advised to maintain a professional and objective demeanor when interacting with SCP-863. Any personal involvement with SCP-863 is strictly prohibited.
