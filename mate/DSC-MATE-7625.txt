Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7625 is to be allowed freedom of movement and is currently employed as a firefighter. Any requests for access to educational materials are to be granted as long as they pose no threat to the safety of SCP-7625 or those around them.

Description: SCP-7625 is a human male in his mid-twenties with spots covering his face. His mouth is often found in a frightened expression, and his eyes are covered by nerd glasses. SCP-7625 often wears a retro cap on his head and a red victory band across his forehead. He has a gold earring in one ear and is often seen with a candy stick as an accessory.

SCP-7625 has a thirst for knowledge and spends a significant amount of time reading and researching obscure topics. They collect books, documentaries, and other educational materials, and are passionate about sharing their newfound knowledge with others. SCP-7625's interest in learning has led them to be a valuable member of the firefighting team, continuously seeking out new information and techniques to better protect against fires.

SCP-7625 is deemed safe as long as they are allowed access to educational materials and are not placed in harm's way. Any attempts to restrain or limit SCP-7625's pursuit of knowledge may result in anxiety or panic. SCP-7625's assignments should be carefully selected to ensure their safety and to maximize their contributions to the firefighting team.
