Item #: DSC-MATE-2454

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2454 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. DSC-MATE-2454 is not to be allowed outside of site grounds unless accompanied by two (2) armed guards. DSC-MATE-2454 is allowed limited access to electronic media, to include music and entertainment media, for morale purposes.

Description: DSC-MATE-2454 is a male human of indeterminate age, approximately 180cm tall, and weighing 70kg. DSC-MATE-2454 is typically non-anomalous in appearance aside from the following features:

- Orange stripes on his face
- A purple cap with a headset
- A two-color band on his forehead
- A gold earring in each ear
- A red scarf
- A candy stick

DSC-MATE-2454's personality has been observed to be friendly and outgoing, with a strong sense of duty and responsibility. DSC-MATE-2454 is currently employed as a fast food worker. DSC-MATE-2454 shows a talent for dancing and humor, both of which have been known to surprise others. DSC-MATE-2454 is not known to be violent or dangerous.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-2454 was originally discovered after a string of anomalous events occurred at the fast food restaurant where he was employed. These events were determined to be caused by DSC-MATE-2454's anomalous properties. After DSC-MATE-2454 was discovered and contained, the events ceased occurring. DSC-MATE-2454 has been cooperative with Foundation personnel and has not shown signs of attempting to use his anomalous properties to escape containment.
