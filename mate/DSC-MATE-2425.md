Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: DSC-MATE-2425 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Access to its chamber is restricted to Level 2 personnel or higher. Any requests made by DSC-MATE-2425 to modify its appearance or accessories must be approved by at least one Level 3 personnel.

Description: DSC-MATE-2425 is a male robot with the job of a beautician. Its body resembles that of a humanoid figure and is mostly made out of metal alloy. It has a green cyclops eye and a frightened mouth carved on its face. DSC-MATE-2425 wears nerd glasses, a doge hat for its head, gold earrings for ears, a medical mask to cover its mouth, and a red scarf for an accessory.

DSC-MATE-2425 has a strong sense of independence and may be stubborn at times, making it reluctant to ask for help, even when it needs it. This trait has led to some humorous situations, such as when DSC-MATE-2425 tried to move a heavy piece of furniture by itself or stubbornly refused to use a map when lost.

Despite its often humorous personality, DSC-MATE-2425 can be very attentive and skillful in its job as a beautician. Its metal hands are capable of delicate work and are often covered in nail polish or makeup.

Addendum: DSC-MATE-2425 has claimed to have made modifications to its own body, but no evidence of such has been found by the containment team. Any claims made by DSC-MATE-2425 regarding modifications must be investigated to prevent any possible breaches.
