Item #: SCP-3437

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3437 is to be kept under constant surveillance in a standard humanoid containment chamber. Any requests for contact or communication are to be reviewed and approved by at least two Level 3 personnel. All interactions with SCP-3437 must be conducted with extreme caution due to its impulsive nature and potential hazards.

Description: SCP-3437 is a female human politician who displays unusual physical features. Its face has orange stripes and purple lips with orange eyebrows. SCP-3437 wears a tiara on its head, and its forehead has a two-color band and a goggle for unknown purposes. The entity has red cross earrings and is often seen smoking a cigarette.

SCP-3437's most notable characteristic is its impulsive behavior. The subject often acts on its emotions, which can lead to unpredictable and humorous situations. However, its actions can also be dangerous and harmful to those around it. It is important to approach SCP-3437 with caution and to avoid provoking it.

Addendum: SCP-3437 has shown the ability to influence others with its persuasive and charismatic personality. It is believed that its political career is a result of this ability. Further research is needed to understand the extent of SCP-3437's anomalous properties.
