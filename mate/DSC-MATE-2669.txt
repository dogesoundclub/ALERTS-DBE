Item #: SCP-2669

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-2669 is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times. The chamber should be equipped with two-way communication devices and sufficient lighting to accommodate the object's nocturnal behavior. SCP-2669 is to receive regular veterinary care and nutrition, and may be provided with various reading and learning materials, as well as access to a computer terminal for research purposes. Interactions with personnel should be limited to those who have received training in handling and communication with SCP-2669.

Description: SCP-2669 is a male tiger of the Bengal subspecies, standing at approximately 2.7 meters in height when standing on its hind legs, and weighs approximately 260 kilograms. The subject's most notable features include a pair of horned rim glasses and a purple cap with a headset. SCP-2669 wears a red victory band on its forehead, a gold earring in its left ear, and a red scarf around its neck. The object also carries a whistle as an accessory.

SCP-2669 exhibits an unusually curious and inquisitive nature, often demonstrating an eagerness to learn and explore new things. The object is highly intelligent and enjoys learning about a variety of topics, ranging from obscure trivia to emerging scientific research. SCP-2669 is capable of engaging in complex conversations and demonstrates empathy and concern for the well-being of others.

Addendum: SCP-2669 has expressed interest in assisting with the care of other anomalous animals in Foundation custody. Due to the object's veterinary training, this may be a feasible option for future testing and containment procedures.
